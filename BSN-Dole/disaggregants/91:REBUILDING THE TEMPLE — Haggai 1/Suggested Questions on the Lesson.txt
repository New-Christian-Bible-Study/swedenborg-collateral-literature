
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Under what ruler were the people of Judah allowed to return to the Holy Land? Cyrus

Junior: How long was this after they were taken captive? seventy years

Primary: What did king Cyrus tell them to do? rebuild the temple

Primary: What did he give them to take back? gold and silver vessels taken from the temple

Junior: What excuse did they give for not finishing the temple? not yet time

Primary: What prophet was sent to rebuke them? Haggai

Junior: What did he tell them was the real reason they had stopped? more interested in their own comfort

Intermediate: What do the gold and silver vessels of the temple picture? teachings from the Word of how to live rightly

Senior: Why was it necessary that the Jews return to the Holy Land? to rebuild temple and re—establish Judaism in the Holy Land preparatory to the coming of the Lord

== PROPHETS OF THE RESTORATION — Zechariah 4

The teachers should read Ezra 1, 3, 4, 5, and 6 to get a complete background for the lesson on Zechariah.
