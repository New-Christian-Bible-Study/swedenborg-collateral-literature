
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: When did the Lord give His disciples the teaching of our lesson for today? the night before the crucifixion

Primary: What does He call Himself in this chapter? the vine

Primary: What does He call His disciples? branches

Primary: What does He say they must do in order to bring forth fruit? abide in Him

Primary: What happens to those who do not abide in the Lord? cast out

Junior: How do men abide in the Lord? obey Him

Junior: Why does the Lord say He tells us these things? so we may share His joy

Primary: What does He say in His commandment? love one another

Junior: Does He promise His disciples an easy life in this world? no

Junior: What does He promise them? to be hated and persecuted by the world

Intermediate: What does the vine represent? divine truth

Senior: What do the grapes represent? spiritual goodness
