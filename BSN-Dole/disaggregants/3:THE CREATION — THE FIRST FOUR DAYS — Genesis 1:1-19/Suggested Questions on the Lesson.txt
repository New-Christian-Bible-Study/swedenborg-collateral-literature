
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who made the world? God

Primary: What book tells us about God? the Bible

Junior: What does light do for us? lets us see

Primary: What special lights did the Lord make for us? sun, moon, stars

Primary: In how many days does the Bible say the world was created? six

Junior: What happened on the seventh day? God rested

Intermediate: In relation to our souls what are meant by the heaven and the earth? higher and lower nature

Junior: To what does light correspond? truth

Intermediate: With what is the Creation story really concerned? how God develops our souls

Senior: Why did the Lord create the world and man? heaven from human race

Senior: What spiritually is the rest on the sabbath day? regeneration, completed character
