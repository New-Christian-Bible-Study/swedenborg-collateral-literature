
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who made the world? God

Younger Children: What book tells us about God? the Bible

Older Children: What does light do for us? lets us see

Younger Children: What special lights did the Lord make for us? sun, moon, stars

Younger Children: In how many days does the Bible say the world was created? six

Older Children: What happened on the seventh day? God rested

Younger Teens: In relation to our souls what are meant by the heaven and the earth? higher and lower nature

Older Children: To what does light correspond? truth

Younger Teens: With what is the Creation story really concerned? how God develops our souls

Older Teens: Why did the Lord create the world and man? heaven from human race

Older Teens: What spiritually is the rest on the sabbath day? regeneration, completed character
