
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What is today called? Palm Sunday

Junior: What does the day commemorate? the Lord's triumphal entry into Jerusalem

Primary: What did the Lord ride when He entered Jerusalem? a donkey

Primary: What did the disciples put on the donkey? their garments

Primary: What did the people strew before the Lord as He rode? palm branches and garments

Primary: What did the multitude cry? Hosanna!

Junior: Where did the Lord go when He entered Jerusalem? the temple

Junior: What did He do there? drove out money—changers and dove—sellers

Junior: What did He say about the temple? it was to be a house of prayer

Junior: What else did He do in the temple? healed the blind and the lame

Junior: Where did He go to spend the night? Bethany

Junior: Who lived there? Mary, Martha, and Lazarus

Intermediate: What is pictured by the Lord's riding into Jerusalem on a donkey? that He alone had brought His natural reason under divine control

Intermediate: What do the palm branches picture? that the Lord alone can save

Senior: What does it mean in our lives to accept the Lord as king? to acknowledge that His laws rule our lives and are to be obeyed
