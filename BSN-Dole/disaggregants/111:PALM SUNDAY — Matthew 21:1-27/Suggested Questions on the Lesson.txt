
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What is today called? Palm Sunday

Older Children: What does the day commemorate? the Lord's triumphal entry into Jerusalem

Younger Children: What did the Lord ride when He entered Jerusalem? a donkey

Younger Children: What did the disciples put on the donkey? their garments

Younger Children: What did the people strew before the Lord as He rode? palm branches and garments

Younger Children: What did the multitude cry? Hosanna!

Older Children: Where did the Lord go when He entered Jerusalem? the temple

Older Children: What did He do there? drove out money—changers and dove—sellers

Older Children: What did He say about the temple? it was to be a house of prayer

Older Children: What else did He do in the temple? healed the blind and the lame

Older Children: Where did He go to spend the night? Bethany

Older Children: Who lived there? Mary, Martha, and Lazarus

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the Lord's riding into Jerusalem on a donkey? that He alone had brought His natural reason under divine control

Younger Teens: What do the palm branches picture? that the Lord alone can save

Older Teens: What does it mean in our lives to accept the Lord as king? to acknowledge that His laws rule our lives and are to be obeyed
