
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: From what book is our lesson today? Micah

Younger Teens: In which division of the divided kingdom did Micah prophesy? Judah

Younger Teens: Do you remember the name of one of the three kings in whose reigns he prophesied? Jotham, Ahaz, Hezekiah

Younger Children: Why did the Lord send prophets? to warn people about their wrongdoing

Younger Children: What were some of the bad things the people were doing in Micah's time? cheating, lying, violence

Younger Teens: What kind of worship did the people think would please the Lord? sacrifice

Younger Children: What did Micah tell them would please the Lord? to do justly, love mercy, walk humbly

Younger Teens: What does "to do justly" mean? be fair and honest

Younger Children: What does "to love mercy" mean? be kind, helpful, forgiving

Younger Children: What does "to walk humbly with thy God" mean? read Word, attend worship, try to do the Lord's will

Younger Teens: To what three planes of our lives do these three requirements of the good life apply? conduct, thought, will

Older Teens: Why are they given in this order in Micah's prophecy? must begin by making our conduct right, progress to cleansing our thoughts, and finally receive a new will from the Lord
