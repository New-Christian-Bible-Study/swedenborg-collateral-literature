
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: From what book is our lesson today? Micah

Intermediate: In which division of the divided kingdom did Micah prophesy? Judah

Intermediate: Do you remember the name of one of the three kings in whose reigns he prophesied? Jotham, Ahaz, Hezekiah

Primary: Why did the Lord send prophets? to warn people about their wrongdoing

Primary: What were some of the bad things the people were doing in Micah's time? cheating, lying, violence

Intermediate: What kind of worship did the people think would please the Lord? sacrifice

Primary: What did Micah tell them would please the Lord? to do justly, love mercy, walk humbly

Intermediate: What does "to do justly" mean? be fair and honest

Primary: What does "to love mercy" mean? be kind, helpful, forgiving

Primary: What does "to walk humbly with thy God" mean? read Word, attend worship, try to do the Lord's will

Intermediate: To what three planes of our lives do these three requirements of the good life apply? conduct, thought, will

Senior: Why are they given in this order in Micah's prophecy? must begin by making our conduct right, progress to cleansing our thoughts, and finally receive a new will from the Lord
