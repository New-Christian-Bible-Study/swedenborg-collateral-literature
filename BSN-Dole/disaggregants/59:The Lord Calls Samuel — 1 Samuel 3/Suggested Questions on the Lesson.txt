
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Younger Children: Who was the high priest in his day? Eli

Older Children: Why was Samuel brought to the tabernacle? his mother's vow

Younger Children: What did he do there? served Eli

Older Children: What wrong things did Eli's sons do? took offering for themselves

Older Children: Why did not Eli make them stop? weak

Younger Children: Where was Samuel when the Lord called him? sleeping in tabernacle

Older Children: What did Samuel at first think? Eli was calling

Younger Children: How many times did the Lord call before Samuel learned who had called him? three

Younger Children: What message for Eli did the Lord give him? sons to lose priesthood

Older Children: How did the people regard Samuel after this? prophet

Younger Teens: To what does hearing correspond? obeying

Older Teens: Why did the Lord have to speak to Samuel three times? appeal to three planes: outer life, intelligence, will
