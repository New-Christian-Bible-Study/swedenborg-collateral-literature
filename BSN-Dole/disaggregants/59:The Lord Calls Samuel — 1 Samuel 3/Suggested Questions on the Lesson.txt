
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Primary: Who was the high priest in his day? Eli

Junior: Why was Samuel brought to the tabernacle? his mother's vow

Primary: What did he do there? served Eli

Junior: What wrong things did Eli's sons do? took offering for themselves

Junior: Why did not Eli make them stop? weak

Primary: Where was Samuel when the Lord called him? sleeping in tabernacle

Junior: What did Samuel at first think? Eli was calling

Primary: How many times did the Lord call before Samuel learned who had called him? three

Primary: What message for Eli did the Lord give him? sons to lose priesthood

Junior: How did the people regard Samuel after this? prophet

Intermediate: To what does hearing correspond? obeying

Senior: Why did the Lord have to speak to Samuel three times? appeal to three planes: outer life, intelligence, will
