
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the last of the Judges? +
*A:* Samuel

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the high priest in his day? +
*A:* Eli

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was Samuel brought to the tabernacle? +
*A:* his mother's vow

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he do there? +
*A:* served Eli

*Q:* (Older Children): What wrong things did Eli's sons do? +
*A:* took offering for themselves

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did not Eli make them stop? +
*A:* weak

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was Samuel when the Lord called him? +
*A:* sleeping in tabernacle

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Samuel at first think? +
*A:* Eli was calling

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many times did the Lord call before Samuel learned who had called him? +
*A:* three

*Q:* (Younger Children): What message for Eli did the Lord give him? +
*A:* sons to lose priesthood

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the people regard Samuel after this? +
*A:* prophet

*Q:* (Younger Teens): To what does hearing correspond? +
*A:* obeying

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did the Lord have to speak to Samuel three times? +
*A:* appeal to three planes: outer life, intelligence, will
