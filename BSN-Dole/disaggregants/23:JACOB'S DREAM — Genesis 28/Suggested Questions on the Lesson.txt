
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who were the twin sons of Isaac and Rebekah? Esau and Jacob

Intermediate: Why was Jacob sent away from home? to find wife, flee brother

Primary: Where was he to go? Haran

Primary: What was one of the places at which he stopped at night? Bethel

Intermediate: Where have we heard of Bethel before? Abram stories

Primary: What did Jacob use for a pillow? stones

Primary: What did he see in his dream? ladder

Primary: Who was at the top of the ladder? the Lord

Primary: Who were going up and down? angels

Intermediate: What does angel mean? messenger

Intermediate: What did the Lord tell Jacob? land to be his

Junior: What did Jacob do when he woke? made pillar

Intermediate: Why was the place called Bethel? see verses 16-19

Junior: What did the dream do for Jacob? encouraged him

Junior: What is our Jacob's ladder? the Word

Intermediate: What are the angels ascending and descending upon it? thoughts

Intermediate: What were the stones which Jacob took for his pillow? foundation of truths

Senior: Why did he afterward set them up for a pillar? exalt basic truths as rules of life
