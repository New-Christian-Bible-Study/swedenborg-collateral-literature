
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who were the twin sons of Isaac and Rebekah? Esau and Jacob

Younger Teens: Why was Jacob sent away from home? to find wife, flee brother

Younger Children: Where was he to go? Haran

Younger Children: What was one of the places at which he stopped at night? Bethel

Younger Teens: Where have we heard of Bethel before? Abram stories

Younger Children: What did Jacob use for a pillow? stones

Younger Children: What did he see in his dream? ladder

Younger Children: Who was at the top of the ladder? the Lord

Younger Children: Who were going up and down? angels

Younger Teens: What does angel mean? messenger

Younger Teens: What did the Lord tell Jacob? land to be his

Older Children: What did Jacob do when he woke? made pillar

Younger Teens: Why was the place called Bethel? see verses 16-19

Older Children: What did the dream do for Jacob? encouraged him

Older Children: What is our Jacob's ladder? the Word

Younger Teens: What are the angels ascending and descending upon it? thoughts

Younger Teens: What were the stones which Jacob took for his pillow? foundation of truths

Older Teens: Why did he afterward set them up for a pillar? exalt basic truths as rules of life
