
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: In chapter 12, what did John first see? woman clothed with sun

Younger Children: What threatened the woman? dragon

Older Children: What can you tell about the dragon? seven heads, ten horns

Younger Children: How was the woman's child saved? caught up to God

Older Children: Where did the woman go? wilderness

Younger Children: Who fought with the dragon? Michael

Younger Children: Who won? Michael

Older Children: How was the woman saved? eagle wings

Younger Children: In our lesson for today what first appears? beast

Younger Children: Can you describe the beast? seven heads, ten horns, ten crowns

Older Children: What happened to one of his heads? wounded

Younger Children: What did the second beast look like? two horns like a lamb

Older Children: What part does the dragon play in this vision? gave power to the beasts

Older Children: What is the number of the beast? six hundred sixty-six

Younger Children: In the second part of our lesson, what appeared when heaven was opened? white horse

Older Children: What can you tell about the horse's rider? eyes like flame, many crowns, vesture dipped in blood

Older Children: What three things is he called? Faithful and True, Word of God, King of kings

Younger Children: Who followed him on white horses? armies

Older Children: What invitation was given? fowls to supper of great God

Older Children: What happened to the beast? cast into lake of fire
