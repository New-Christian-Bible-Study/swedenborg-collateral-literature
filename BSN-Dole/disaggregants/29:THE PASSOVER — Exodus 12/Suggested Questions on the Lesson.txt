
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What happened to the Israelites in Egypt after Joseph died? became slaves

Primary: Who was raised up to lead them out of Egypt? Moses

Primary: How were the Egyptians persuaded to let them go? plagues

Primary: How many plagues were there? ten

Primary: What was the last one? firstborn died

Junior: How were the Israelites told to save their own firstborn? blood of lamb on doorway

Junior: What did the Egyptians let them have to take away with them? gold, silver, clothing

Primary: What feast did they observe just before they left Egypt? Passover

Junior: Why was it called the Passover? "I will pass over you"

Primary: How was the lamb which they ate cooked? roasted

Primary: What did they eat with it? unleavened bread, bitter herbs

Primary: How were they dressed for the feast? loins girded, sandals on, staff in hand

Junior: What were they to do with any of the lamb that was left over? burn it

Junior: How many Israelites were there when they left Egypt? six hundred thousand plus children

Intermediate: What were they to remember whenever they kept the Passover? deliverance from slavery

Intermediate: How often were they told to observe it? annually forever

Intermediate: What feast takes the place of the Passover for Christians? Holy Supper

Intermediate: When and by whom was it instituted? Jesus, on Maundy Thursday

Senior: What does the lamb represent? innocence

Senior: What is innocence? harmlessness, willingness to be led by the Lord
