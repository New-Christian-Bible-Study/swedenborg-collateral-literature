
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What feast did the Lord celebrate during His last week on earth? Passover

Junior: What did this feast commemorate? release from Egyptian slavery

Junior: What new feast did He institute at that time? Holy Supper

Junior: What does this Christian feast commemorate? redemption from slavery to evil

Primary: Who betrayed the Lord? Judas Iscariot

Junior: Before whom was Jesus first tried? the high priests

Junior: Of what did the high priests declare Him guilty? blasphemy

Primary: To whom was He sent to be condemned? Pontius Pilate

Junior: What did Pilate think of Him? found no fault in Him

Junior: Why did he not release Him? wanted to satisfy the crowd

Junior: Whom did he release instead? Barabbas

Primary: What did Pilate's soldiers do to the Lord? mocked Him

Junior: Where was the Lord crucified? Golgotha

Junior: Who carried His cross to Golgotha? Simon of Cyrene (but see John 19:17)

Primary: What did the soldiers do with the Lord's garments? divided them among themselves

Primary: Who were crucified with the Lord? two thieves

Junior: What did the Lord's enemies tempt Him to do? come down from the cross

Junior: What happened in the temple at the time of His death? curtain tore in two

Junior: What did the centurion say? truly this man was son of God

Junior: Who asked Pilate for the Lord's body? Joseph of Arimathea

Junior: What did he do with it? laid it in new rock-hewn tomb

Junior: What women stayed near the Lord until His body was placed in the tomb? the two Marys

Intermediate: Why did the Lord allow Himself to be put to death? to show that evil is really powerless against good

Senior: What is pictured by the parting of the Lord's garments? picking and choosing from the Word what we want to believe
