
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What feast did the Lord celebrate during His last week on earth? Passover

Older Children: What did this feast commemorate? release from Egyptian slavery

Older Children: What new feast did He institute at that time? Holy Supper

Older Children: What does this Christian feast commemorate? redemption from slavery to evil

Younger Children: Who betrayed the Lord? Judas Iscariot

Older Children: Before whom was Jesus first tried? the high priests

Older Children: Of what did the high priests declare Him guilty? blasphemy

Younger Children: To whom was He sent to be condemned? Pontius Pilate

Older Children: What did Pilate think of Him? found no fault in Him

Older Children: Why did he not release Him? wanted to satisfy the crowd

Older Children: Whom did he release instead? Barabbas

Younger Children: What did Pilate's soldiers do to the Lord? mocked Him

Older Children: Where was the Lord crucified? Golgotha

Older Children: Who carried His cross to Golgotha? Simon of Cyrene (but see John 19:17)

Younger Children: What did the soldiers do with the Lord's garments? divided them among themselves

Younger Children: Who were crucified with the Lord? two thieves

Older Children: What did the Lord's enemies tempt Him to do? come down from the cross

Older Children: What happened in the temple at the time of His death? curtain tore in two

Older Children: What did the centurion say? truly this man was son of God

Older Children: Who asked Pilate for the Lord's body? Joseph of Arimathea

Older Children: What did he do with it? laid it in new rock-hewn tomb

Older Children: What women stayed near the Lord until His body was placed in the tomb? the two Marys

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord allow Himself to be put to death? to show that evil is really powerless against good

Older Teens: What is pictured by the parting of the Lord's garments? picking and choosing from the Word what we want to believe
