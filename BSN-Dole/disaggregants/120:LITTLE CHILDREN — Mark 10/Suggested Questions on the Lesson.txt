
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Where does our lesson for today take place? in Judea

Older Children: Where is the Lord going? to Jerusalem

Older Children: What did the Pharisees ask Him? is it lawful to divorce?

Older Children: Why did He say Moses permitted divorce? their hardness of heart

Younger Children: How did He say people must receive the kingdom of God? like a child

Younger Teens: What is innocence? literally, harmlessness; spiritually, willingness to be led by the Lord

Older Children: What did the rich young man ask the Lord? what must I do to inherit eternal life?

Younger Children: What did the Lord first tell him to do? obey the commandments

Younger Teens: What did He tell him next? sell his goods, give to the poor

Older Children: Why did the young man turn away? he was very rich

Younger Teens: What did the Lord say about riches? make it hard to get into heaven

Younger Teens: What does trusting in riches mean? trusting in one's own goodness and wisdom

Younger Teens: What did James and John ask for? seats of honor in kingdom

Older Children: What did the Lord tell them? not mine to give

Older Teens: What reason did the Lord give them for His coming into the world? to serve, and to give His life
