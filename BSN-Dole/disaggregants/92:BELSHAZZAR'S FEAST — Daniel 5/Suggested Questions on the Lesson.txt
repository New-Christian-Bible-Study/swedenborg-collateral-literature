
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is a prophet? +
*A:* one who speaks for the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the Lord send prophets to the people of Israel and Judah? +
*A:* rebuke, warn

*Q:* (Younger Children): What prophets do you remember who did not write books of the Word? +
*A:* Nathan, Elijah, Elisha, Ahijah

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is the difference between (1) "Major," and (2) "Minor" Prophets? +
*A:* (1) longer, (2) shorter

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who were the four Major Prophets? +
*A:* Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

*Q:* (Older Children): When and where did Isaiah live? +
*A:* Judah, Hezekiah's time

*Q:* (Older Children): When and where did Jeremiah live? +
*A:* Judah, time of Babylonian captivity

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Jeremiah die? +
*A:* probably in Egypt

*Q:* (Older Children): Who wrote the book of Lamentations? +
*A:* probably Jeremiah

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was it written? +
*A:* Egypt

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did Ezekiel and Daniel prophesy? +
*A:* in captivity

*Q:* (Older Children): What was Ezekiel's occupation? +
*A:* priest

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was he sometimes taken in vision? +
*A:* Jerusalem

*Q:* (Older Children): What power did the Lord give Daniel? +
*A:* to interpret dreams

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why was He able to give Daniel this ability? +
*A:* he was faithful to the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was king of Babylon when Daniel was first brought there? +
*A:* Nebuchadnezzar

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he happen to make Daniel a ruler in the kingdom? +
*A:* interpreted dreams

*Q:* (Older Children): Of what other well-known person in the Bible story does this remind us? +
*A:* Joseph

*Q:* (Older Children): Who followed Nebuchadnezzar on the throne of Babylon? +
*A:* Belshazzar

*Q:* (Younger Children): What great sin did he commit? +
*A:* profaned temple vessels

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened that frightened him? +
*A:* hand writing on wall

*Q:* (Older Children): What were the words written on the wall? +
*A:* MENE, MENE, TEKEL, UPHARSIN

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Daniel tell him they meant? +
*A:* measured, weighed, divided

*Q:* (Younger Children): When was Daniel's prophecy fulfilled? +
*A:* same night

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Belshazzar's act represent? +
*A:* selfish use of spiritual knowledge

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the writing on the wall represent? +
*A:* judgment from the Word
