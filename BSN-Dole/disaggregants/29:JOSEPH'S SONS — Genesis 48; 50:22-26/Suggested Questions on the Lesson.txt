
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did Joseph's brothers go to Egypt? +
*A:* famine

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Joseph do for his family? +
*A:* fed, housed

*Q:* (Younger Children): What two sons were born to Joseph in Egypt? +
*A:* Manasseh, Ephraim

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which was the older? +
*A:* Manasseh

*Q:* (Younger Children): When Jacob was about to die, why did Joseph bring his sons to him? +
*A:* blessing

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which son did Joseph expect to receive the better blessing? +
*A:* Manasseh

*Q:* (Younger Children): To which one did Jacob give it? +
*A:* Ephraim

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What special honor did Jacob give to Joseph's sons? +
*A:* "adopted" them

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was Jacob buried? +
*A:* cave of Machpelah

*Q:* (Older Children): Who else had been buried there? +
*A:* Abraham, Sarah, Isaac, Rebekah, Leah

*Q:* (Older Children): When Joseph died, what did he make his family promise? +
*A:* take bones to Canaan

*Q:* (Older Children): How did they preserve his body? +
*A:* embalming

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do Manasseh and Ephraim represent? +
*A:* Ephraim = knowledge of truth, and Manasseh = desire to live according to it

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did Jacob give the younger son the first blessing? +
*A:* our continuing need first to learn what is right
