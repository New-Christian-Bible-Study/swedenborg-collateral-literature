
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Why did Joseph's brothers go to Egypt? famine

Younger Children: What did Joseph do for his family? fed, housed

Younger Children: What two sons were born to Joseph in Egypt? Manasseh, Ephraim

Younger Children: Which was the older? Manasseh

Younger Children: When Jacob was about to die, why did Joseph bring his sons to him? blessing

Younger Children: Which son did Joseph expect to receive the better blessing? Manasseh

Younger Children: To which one did Jacob give it? Ephraim

Younger Teens: What special honor did Jacob give to Joseph's sons? "adopted" them

Older Children: Where was Jacob buried? cave of Machpelah

Older Children: Who else had been buried there? Abraham, Sarah, Isaac, Rebekah, Leah

Older Children: When Joseph died, what did he make his family promise? take bones to Canaan

Older Children: How did they preserve his body? embalming

Younger Teens: What do Manasseh and Ephraim represent? Ephraim = knowledge of truth, and Manasseh = desire to live according to it

Older Teens: Why did Jacob give the younger son the first blessing? our continuing need first to learn what is right
