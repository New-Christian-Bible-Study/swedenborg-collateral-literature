
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the son of Abraham and Sarah? +
*A:* Isaac

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was Isaac's wife? +
*A:* Rebekah

*Q:* (Younger Children): What place did she come from? +
*A:* Haran

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What children did Isaac and Rebekah have? +
*A:* Esau and Jacob

*Q:* (Older Children): In time of famine where did the Lord forbid Isaac to go? +
*A:* Egypt

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Isaac go instead? +
*A:* Philistia

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the king of the Philistines called? +
*A:* Abimelech

*Q:* (Younger Children): Did he welcome Isaac? +
*A:* yes, but later feared him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who had previously dug wells in the Philistine country? +
*A:* Abraham

*Q:* (Younger Children): What had the Philistines done to these wells? +
*A:* filled them in

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many wells did they prevent Isaac from opening? +
*A:* two

*Q:* (Younger Children): Did Isaac quarrel with them? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): What agreement did Abimelech finally make with Isaac? +
*A:* oath of peace

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they name the place where the agreement was made? +
*A:* Beersheba

*Q:* (Older Children): What does Beersheba mean? +
*A:* well of oath

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Isaac represent? +
*A:* our ability to reason spiritually

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does a well represent? +
*A:* the Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): What do the Philistines picture? +
*A:* desire to know truth, but no desire to live it

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by their filling up Abraham's wells with earth? +
*A:* preferring one's own opinion to the Lord's guidance
