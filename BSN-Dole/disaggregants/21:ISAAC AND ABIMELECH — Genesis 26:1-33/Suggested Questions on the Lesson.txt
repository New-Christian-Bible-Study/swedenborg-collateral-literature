
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the son of Abraham and Sarah? Isaac

Primary: Who was Isaac's wife? Rebekah

Primary: What place did she come from? Haran

Intermediate: What children did Isaac and Rebekah have? Esau and Jacob

Junior: In time of famine where did the Lord forbid Isaac to go? Egypt

Junior: Where did Isaac go instead? Philistia

Junior: What was the king of the Philistines called? Abimelech

Primary: Did he welcome Isaac? yes, but later feared him

Primary: Who had previously dug wells in the Philistine country? Abraham

Primary: What had the Philistines done to these wells? filled them in

Primary: How many wells did they prevent Isaac from opening? two

Primary: Did Isaac quarrel with them? no

Junior: What agreement did Abimelech finally make with Isaac? oath of peace

Junior: What did they name the place where the agreement was made? Beersheba

Junior: What does Beersheba mean? well of oath

Intermediate: What does Isaac represent? our ability to reason spiritually

Intermediate: What does a well represent? the Word

Senior: What do the Philistines picture? desire to know truth, but no desire to live it

Senior: What is pictured by their filling up Abraham's wells with earth? preferring one's own opinion to the Lord's guidance
