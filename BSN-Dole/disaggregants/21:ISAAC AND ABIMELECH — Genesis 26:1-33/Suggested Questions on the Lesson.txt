
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the son of Abraham and Sarah? Isaac

Younger Children: Who was Isaac's wife? Rebekah

Younger Children: What place did she come from? Haran

Younger Teens: What children did Isaac and Rebekah have? Esau and Jacob

Older Children: In time of famine where did the Lord forbid Isaac to go? Egypt

Older Children: Where did Isaac go instead? Philistia

Older Children: What was the king of the Philistines called? Abimelech

Younger Children: Did he welcome Isaac? yes, but later feared him

Younger Children: Who had previously dug wells in the Philistine country? Abraham

Younger Children: What had the Philistines done to these wells? filled them in

Younger Children: How many wells did they prevent Isaac from opening? two

Younger Children: Did Isaac quarrel with them? no

Older Children: What agreement did Abimelech finally make with Isaac? oath of peace

Older Children: What did they name the place where the agreement was made? Beersheba

Older Children: What does Beersheba mean? well of oath

Younger Teens: What does Isaac represent? our ability to reason spiritually

Younger Teens: What does a well represent? the Word

Older Teens: What do the Philistines picture? desire to know truth, but no desire to live it

Older Teens: What is pictured by their filling up Abraham's wells with earth? preferring one's own opinion to the Lord's guidance
