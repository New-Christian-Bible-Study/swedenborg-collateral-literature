
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What nation finally conquered Israel? Assyria

Older Children: What happened to the people? taken away forever

Older Children: Why was Judah able to hold out longer against its enemies? faithful to David's line

Older Children: What did its good kings try to do? reform

Younger Children: What great nation finally conquered Judah? Babylon

Older Children: What king of Judah first allowed messengers of Babylon to spy out the land? Hezekiah

Older Children: What great prophet foretold what the result would be? Isaiah

Younger Children: Who was the last king of Judah? Zedekiah

Younger Children: To what prophet did he send to inquire of the Lord? Jeremiah

Older Children: What did Jeremiah tell him? Jerusalem would be destroyed

Older Children: What happened to Zedekiah? blinded, taken to Babylon

Older Children: Who were carried away to Babylon? princes, mighty men, craftsmen

Younger Children: Who were left in the land? the poor

Younger Teens: What do Israel and Judah represent? mind and heart divided

Older Teens: What does Assyria represent? worldly reasoning

Younger Teens: What does Babylon represent? love of dominion from love of self

Older Teens: Who are pictured by "the poor of the land"? those who are humbly conscious of their need of the Lord
