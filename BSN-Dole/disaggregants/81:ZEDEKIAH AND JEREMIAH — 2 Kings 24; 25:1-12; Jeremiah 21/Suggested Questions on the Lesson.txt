
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What nation finally conquered Israel? Assyria

Junior: What happened to the people? taken away forever

Junior: Why was Judah able to hold out longer against its enemies? faithful to David's line

Junior: What did its good kings try to do? reform

Primary: What great nation finally conquered Judah? Babylon

Junior: What king of Judah first allowed messengers of Babylon to spy out the land? Hezekiah

Junior: What great prophet foretold what the result would be? Isaiah

Primary: Who was the last king of Judah? Zedekiah

Primary: To what prophet did he send to inquire of the Lord? Jeremiah

Junior: What did Jeremiah tell him? Jerusalem would be destroyed

Junior: What happened to Zedekiah? blinded, taken to Babylon

Junior: Who were carried away to Babylon? princes, mighty men, craftsmen

Primary: Who were left in the land? the poor

Intermediate: What do Israel and Judah represent? mind and heart divided 

Senior: What does Assyria represent? worldly reasoning

Intermediate: What does Babylon represent? love of dominion from love of self

Senior: Who are pictured by "the poor of the land"? those who are humbly conscious of their need of the Lord
