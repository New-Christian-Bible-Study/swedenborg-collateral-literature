
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Under what ruler were the people of Judah allowed to return to the Holy Land? Cyrus

Older Children: How long was this after they were taken captive? seventy years

Younger Children: What did king Cyrus tell them to do? rebuild the temple

Younger Children: What did he give them to take back? gold and silver vessels taken from the temple

Older Children: What excuse did they give for not finishing the temple? not yet time

Younger Children: What prophet was sent to rebuke them? Haggai

Older Children: What did he tell them was the real reason they had stopped? more interested in their own comfort

Younger Teens: What do the gold and silver vessels of the temple picture? teachings from the Word of how to live rightly
