
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Daniel prophesy? +
*A:* Babylon

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why could the Lord always protect and enlighten Daniel? +
*A:* his faith

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was the king of Babylon at this time? +
*A:* Nebuchadnezzar

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he ask of his wise men? +
*A:* recall and interpret his dream

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did Daniel learn the answer? +
*A:* through prayer

*Q:* (Older Children): What was Nebuchadnezzar's dream? +
*A:* great image

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the head of the image made of? +
*A:* gold

*Q:* (Older Children): What other metals formed other parts? +
*A:* silver, bronze, iron

*Q:* (Older Children): Of what were its feet? +
*A:* iron and clay

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was it destroyed? +
*A:* great stone

*Q:* (Younger Children): What became of the stone which destroyed it? +
*A:* became a mountain

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Nebuchadnezzar say about Daniel's God? +
*A:* "a God of gods"

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he reward Daniel? +
*A:* ruler of province

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do the four metals in the image correspond to? +
*A:* gold, love to God; silver, faith; bronze, natural goodness; iron, natural truth

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do the feet correspond to? +
*A:* outer life

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the whole vision picture? +
*A:* the spiritual decline of one who allows love of dominion to develop in his life
