
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Where did Daniel prophesy? Babylon

Younger Children: Why could the Lord always protect and enlighten Daniel? his faith

Older Children: Who was the king of Babylon at this time? Nebuchadnezzar

Younger Children: What did he ask of his wise men? recall and interpret his dream

Younger Children: How did Daniel learn the answer? through prayer

Older Children: What was Nebuchadnezzar's dream? great image

Younger Children: What was the head of the image made of? gold

Older Children: What other metals formed other parts? silver, bronze, iron

Older Children: Of what were its feet? iron and clay

Younger Children: How was it destroyed? great stone

Younger Children: What became of the stone which destroyed it? became a mountain

Older Children: What did Nebuchadnezzar say about Daniel's God? "a God of gods"

Older Children: How did he reward Daniel? ruler of province

Younger Teens: What do the four metals in the image correspond to? gold, love to God; silver, faith; bronze, natural goodness; iron, natural truth

Younger Teens: What do the feet correspond to? outer life

Older Teens: What does the whole vision picture? the spiritual decline of one who allows love of dominion to develop in his life
