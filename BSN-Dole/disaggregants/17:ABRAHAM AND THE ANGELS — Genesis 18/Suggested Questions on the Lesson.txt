
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Teens: Where was Abraham's first home? Ur

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him to do? leave, go to Canaan

Younger Teens: Where did he finally settle? Hebron (Mamre)

Younger Children: Who came to him there one day? three men

Younger Children: How did he receive them? as honored guests

Younger Children: Who were they really? angels

Younger Teens: What did they tell Abraham? Sarah would have a son

Younger Teens: Why did Sarah laugh? she was old, doubted

Younger Teens: Where did the angels go when they left Abraham? to Sodom

Younger Teens: Why were they going to Sodom and Gomorrah? to destroy them

Older Children: What was the first question Abraham asked of the Lord? destroy the good with the bad?

Older Children: What did the Lord tell him? If I find fifty righteous ...

Older Children: How many questions did Abraham ask? -5,40,30,20,10 (five questions)

Older Children: How did the Lord answer each time? I will not destroy if ...

Older Children: What was the smallest number of good people Abraham mentioned? ten

Intermediate. What does Abraham represent? state of simple trust and obedience

Older Teens: What is pictured by his being told to leave Ur and go to Canaan? leave selfish outlook, start to develop heavenly character

Older Teens: What is pictured by the birth of a son? new development of truth or new intellectual faculty
