
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Where was Abraham's first home? +
*A:* Ur

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord tell him to do? +
*A:* leave, go to Canaan

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Where did he finally settle? +
*A:* Hebron (Mamre)

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who came to him there one day? +
*A:* three men

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did he receive them? +
*A:* as honored guests

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who were they really? +
*A:* angels

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did they tell Abraham? +
*A:* Sarah would have a son

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did Sarah laugh? +
*A:* she was old, doubted

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Where did the angels go when they left Abraham? +
*A:* to Sodom

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why were they going to Sodom and Gomorrah? +
*A:* to destroy them

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the first question Abraham asked of the Lord? +
*A:* destroy the good with the bad?

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell him? +
*A:* If I find fifty righteous ...

*Q:* (Older Children): How many questions did Abraham ask? +
*A:* -5,40,30,20,10 (five questions)

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord answer each time? +
*A:* I will not destroy if ...

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the smallest number of good people Abraham mentioned? +
*A:* ten

Younger Teens. What does Abraham represent? state of simple trust and obedience

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by his being told to leave Ur and go to Canaan? +
*A:* leave selfish outlook, start to develop heavenly character

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by the birth of a son? +
*A:* new development of truth or new intellectual faculty
