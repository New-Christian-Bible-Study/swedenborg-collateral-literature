
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Intermediate: Where was Abraham's first home? Ur

Primary: What did the Lord tell him to do? leave, go to Canaan

Intermediate: Where did he finally settle? Hebron (Mamre)

Primary: Who came to him there one day? three men

Primary: How did he receive them? as honored guests

Primary: Who were they really? angels

Intermediate: What did they tell Abraham? Sarah would have a son

Intermediate: Why did Sarah laugh? she was old, doubted

Intermediate: Where did the angels go when they left Abraham? to Sodom

Intermediate: Why were they going to Sodom and Gomorrah? to destroy them

Junior: What was the first question Abraham asked of the Lord? destroy the good with the bad?

Junior: What did the Lord tell him? If I find fifty righteous ...

Junior: How many questions did Abraham ask? -5,40,30,20,10 (five questions)

Junior: How did the Lord answer each time? I will not destroy if ...

Junior: What was the smallest number of good people Abraham mentioned? ten

Intermediate. What does Abraham represent? state of simple trust and obedience

Senior: What is pictured by his being told to leave Ur and go to Canaan? leave selfish outlook, start to develop heavenly character

Senior: What is pictured by the birth of a son? new development of truth or new intellectual faculty
