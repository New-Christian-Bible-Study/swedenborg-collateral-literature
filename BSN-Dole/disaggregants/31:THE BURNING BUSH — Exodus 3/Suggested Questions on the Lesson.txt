
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Where was Moses brought up? palace in Egypt

Junior: How long did he live in Egypt? forty years (Acts 7:23)

Junior: Why did he have to flee? killed Egyptian

Primary: Where did he go? land of Midian

Junior: Who was Midian? son of Abraham and Keturah

Junior: How long did Moses stay in the land of Midian? forty years

Primary: Near what mountain was he when the Lord called him? Horeb (Sinai)

Primary: What was he doing there? tending sheep

Primary: How did the Lord call him? burning bush

Junior: What did He tell him he was to do? lead Israelites out of Egypt

Junior: Was Moses eager to undertake this great task? no

Junior: What did the Lord tell him? "I will be with you"

Junior: What did the Lord tell Moses to have the women do before they left Egypt? "borrow"

Intermediate: What does Egypt represent? state of acquiring knowledge

Intermediate: Why did the Israelites become slaves when they stayed there too long? knowledge must be used in service to Lord

Senior: What does the land of Midian represent in a good sense? simple faith

Intermediate: Why did Moses stay in each land forty years? full state of testing

Senior: What is pictured by the burning bush? Lord's speaking to us through our memory

Intermediate: What does Moses represent? divine law

Senior: In what ways do we sometimes act like Moses? hate to start serving the Lord, make excuses
