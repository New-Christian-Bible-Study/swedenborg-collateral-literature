
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What is a parable? a story with an inner meaning

Younger Children: Why did the Lord always speak in parables? His words always had a deeper meaning

Older Children: What are all the parables in our chapter about? the kingdom of heaven

Younger Children: What is the first parable in the chapter called? the sower

Younger Children: Why could the Lord explain it to His disciples? they were eager to learn

Older Children: Who is the sower? the Lord

Older Children: What is the seed? truth from the Word

Younger Children: What are the different kinds of ground? different kinds of minds

Older Children: Where must the kingdom of heaven be if we are to live in it when we die? within us

Older Children: How many parables of the kingdom of heaven are there in this chapter? seven

Older Children: How many of them can you name?

Younger Teens: What does the parable of the tares teach us? why we cannot overcome all our evils at once

Younger Teens: What is the general lesson of the whole series of parables? development of heavenly character

Older Teens: What is the pearl of great price? knowledge and acknowledgment of the Lord
