
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What was the tabernacle? portable church

Younger Children: Who was the high priest? Aaron

Younger Children: Who were his assistants? his four sons

Older Children: What two altars are involved in our lesson for today? great bronze, incense

Older Children: In what division of the tabernacle was the great altar? outer court

Older Children: What was it used for? sacrifices

Older Children: In what division of the tabernacle was the altar of incense? Holy Place

Older Children: How often was incense to be burned upon it? morning and evening

Younger Children: What were the coals to burn the incense carried in? a censer

Younger Children: Where were these coals to be obtained? great altar

Older Children: Who were the two oldest sons of Aaron? Nadab and Abihu

Younger Children: What did they do which was wrong? offered "strange" or unholy fire

Younger Children: What is meant by the fire's being "strange"? not proper

Younger Children: What happened to Nadab and Abihu? fire killed them

Younger Children: Where were their bodies taken? outside camp

Older Children: What command did Moses give to Aaron and his two younger sons? don't mourn

Older Children: What did he tell the people to do? bewail the burning

Younger Teens: What does incense represent? prayers

Older Teens: What does the fire on the great altar represent? genuine love to the Lord

Younger Teens: Spiritually, what is "strange" or unholy fire? acting religious for selfish reasons
