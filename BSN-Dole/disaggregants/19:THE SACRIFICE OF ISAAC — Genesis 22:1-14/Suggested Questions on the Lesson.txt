
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was Abram's wife? Sarai

Primary: What was the name of their only son? Isaac

Junior: How old were Abraham and Sarah when Isaac was born? a hundred, ninety

Junior: What was Abraham tempted to do? sacrifice Isaac

Junior: Why did he think he ought to sacrifice Isaac? thought God wanted him to give up his dearest possession

Intermediate: Why did the Lord let him think this? to test his faith

Junior: Where was Abraham told to go for the sacrifice? Mount Moriah

Junior: What was later built on Mount Moriah? Temple

Junior: How long was the journey? three days

Junior: Who went with Abraham and Isaac? two young men

Junior: What did they take with them? donkey, wood, fire, knife

Primary: Who went up the mountain? Isaac, Abraham

Junior: What did Isaac ask? where is lamb?

Junior: What did Abraham answer? God will provide

Primary: When did the Lord stop Abraham? as he raised knife

Primary: How did He stop him? angel

Primary: What did Abraham find to sacrifice instead of Isaac? ram

Intermediate. Does temptation really come from the Lord? no

Intermediate: Why does the Lord permit us to be tempted? to strengthen our character

Senior: What does Isaac represent? spiritual rational

Senior: How are people today tempted to sacrifice Isaac? stop reasoning about religion

Senior: What is meant by the substitution of a ram for Isaac in the sacrifice? continue to reason, but submit our ideas of what is good and useful to the guidance of the Lord's truth
