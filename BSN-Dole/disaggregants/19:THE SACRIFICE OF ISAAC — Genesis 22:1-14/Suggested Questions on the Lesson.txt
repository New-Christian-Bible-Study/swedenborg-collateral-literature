
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was Abram's wife? Sarai

Younger Children: What was the name of their only son? Isaac

Older Children: How old were Abraham and Sarah when Isaac was born? a hundred, ninety

Older Children: What was Abraham tempted to do? sacrifice Isaac

Older Children: Why did he think he ought to sacrifice Isaac? thought God wanted him to give up his dearest possession

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord let him think this? to test his faith

Older Children: Where was Abraham told to go for the sacrifice? Mount Moriah

Older Children: What was later built on Mount Moriah? Temple

Older Children: How long was the journey? three days

Older Children: Who went with Abraham and Isaac? two young men

Older Children: What did they take with them? donkey, wood, fire, knife

Younger Children: Who went up the mountain? Isaac, Abraham

Older Children: What did Isaac ask? where is lamb?

Older Children: What did Abraham answer? God will provide

Younger Children: When did the Lord stop Abraham? as he raised knife

Younger Children: How did He stop him? angel

Younger Children: What did Abraham find to sacrifice instead of Isaac? ram

Younger Teens. Does temptation really come from the Lord? no

Younger Teens: Why does the Lord permit us to be tempted? to strengthen our character

Older Teens: What does Isaac represent? spiritual rational

Older Teens: How are people today tempted to sacrifice Isaac? stop reasoning about religion

Older Teens: What is meant by the substitution of a ram for Isaac in the sacrifice? continue to reason, but submit our ideas of what is good and useful to the guidance of the Lord's truth
