
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was Abram's wife? +
*A:* Sarai

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the name of their only son? +
*A:* Isaac

*Q:* (Older Children): How old were Abraham and Sarah when Isaac was born? +
*A:* a hundred, ninety

*Q:* (Older Children): What was Abraham tempted to do? +
*A:* sacrifice Isaac

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did he think he ought to sacrifice Isaac? +
*A:* thought God wanted him to give up his dearest possession

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did the Lord let him think this? +
*A:* to test his faith

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was Abraham told to go for the sacrifice? +
*A:* Mount Moriah

*Q:* (Older Children): What was later built on Mount Moriah? +
*A:* Temple

*Q:* (Older Children): How long was the journey? +
*A:* three days

*Q:* (Older Children): Who went with Abraham and Isaac? +
*A:* two young men

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they take with them? +
*A:* donkey, wood, fire, knife

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who went up the mountain? +
*A:* Isaac, Abraham

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Isaac ask? +
*A:* where is lamb?

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Abraham answer? +
*A:* God will provide

*Q:* (Younger Children): When did the Lord stop Abraham? +
*A:* as he raised knife

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did He stop him? +
*A:* angel

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Abraham find to sacrifice instead of Isaac? +
*A:* ram

Younger Teens. Does temptation really come from the Lord? no

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why does the Lord permit us to be tempted? +
*A:* to strengthen our character

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does Isaac represent? +
*A:* spiritual rational

*Q:* (Older Teens): How are people today tempted to sacrifice Isaac? +
*A:* stop reasoning about religion

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is meant by the substitution of a ram for Isaac in the sacrifice? +
*A:* continue to reason, but submit our ideas of what is good and useful to the guidance of the Lord's truth
