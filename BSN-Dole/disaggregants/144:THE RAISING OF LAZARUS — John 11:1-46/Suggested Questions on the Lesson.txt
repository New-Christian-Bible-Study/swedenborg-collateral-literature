
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was Lazarus? a friend of Jesus

Primary: Who were his sisters? Martha and Mary

Primary: Where did they live? Bethany

Junior: When did the miracle of our chapter take place? near the end of Jesus' life

Primary: When Lazarus became sick, did the Lord go to Bethany at once? no

Primary: When He arrived, how long had Lazarus been in the grave? four days

Primary: What did Martha say when she met the Lord? "If you had been here ... "

Junior: How did the Lord answer? " ... will rise again."

Junior: What was Martha's idea of the resurrection of the dead? at the "last" day

Junior: What did the Lord tell her? I am the resurrection

Primary: Can you tell about the raising of Lazarus? took stone away, called loudly, Lazarus come forth

Junior: What did the chief priests and Pharisees think about it? plotted to kill Him

Intermediate: Why did the Lord wait so long to go to Bethany? to show His power over death

Senior: Why did He first say Lazarus slept and afterward that he was dead? death is a sort of sleep, with reawakening in the spiritual world
