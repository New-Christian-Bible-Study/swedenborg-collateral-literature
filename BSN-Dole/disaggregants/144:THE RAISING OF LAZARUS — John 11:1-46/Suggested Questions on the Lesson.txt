
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was Lazarus? a friend of Jesus

Younger Children: Who were his sisters? Martha and Mary

Younger Children: Where did they live? Bethany

Older Children: When did the miracle of our chapter take place? near the end of Jesus' life

Younger Children: When Lazarus became sick, did the Lord go to Bethany at once? no

Younger Children: When He arrived, how long had Lazarus been in the grave? four days

Younger Children: What did Martha say when she met the Lord? "If you had been here ... "

Older Children: How did the Lord answer? " ... will rise again."

Older Children: What was Martha's idea of the resurrection of the dead? at the "last" day

Older Children: What did the Lord tell her? I am the resurrection

Younger Children: Can you tell about the raising of Lazarus? took stone away, called loudly, Lazarus come forth

Older Children: What did the chief priests and Pharisees think about it? plotted to kill Him

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord wait so long to go to Bethany? to show His power over death

Older Teens: Why did He first say Lazarus slept and afterward that he was dead? death is a sort of sleep, with reawakening in the spiritual world
