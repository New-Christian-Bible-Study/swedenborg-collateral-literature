
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was Lazarus? +
*A:* a friend of Jesus

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who were his sisters? +
*A:* Martha and Mary

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did they live? +
*A:* Bethany

*Q:* (Older Children): When did the miracle of our chapter take place? +
*A:* near the end of Jesus' life

*Q:* (Younger Children): When Lazarus became sick, did the Lord go to Bethany at once? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): When He arrived, how long had Lazarus been in the grave? +
*A:* four days

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Martha say when she met the Lord? +
*A:* "If you had been here ... "

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord answer? +
*A:* " ... will rise again."

*Q:* (Older Children): What was Martha's idea of the resurrection of the dead? +
*A:* at the "last" day

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell her? +
*A:* I am the resurrection

*Q:* (Younger Children): Can you tell about the raising of Lazarus? +
*A:* took stone away, called loudly, Lazarus come forth

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the chief priests and Pharisees think about it? +
*A:* plotted to kill Him

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did the Lord wait so long to go to Bethany? +
*A:* to show His power over death

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did He first say Lazarus slept and afterward that he was dead? +
*A:* death is a sort of sleep, with reawakening in the spiritual world
