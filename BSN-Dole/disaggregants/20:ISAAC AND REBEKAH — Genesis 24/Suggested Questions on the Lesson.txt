
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Who was the "father of the Jews"? Abraham

Junior: Where did he come from? Ur

Junior: Why did he go to the Holy Land? God called him

Junior: Where did he settle there? Hebron

Primary: Who was Abraham's wife? Sarah

Primary: Who was Hagar? her maid

Primary: Who was Abraham's first son? Ishmael

Junior: Whose son was Isaac? Sarah's

Junior: Why were Hagar and Ishmael sent away? mocking attitude

Intermediate: Why did Abraham not want Isaac to marry a Canaanite? idolaters

Primary: Whom did Abraham send to find a wife for Isaac? servant

Primary: Where did he send him? Haran

Junior: Who had settled there? Abraham's brother

Primary: Where did Abraham's servant stop? at a well

Junior: What sign did he ask of the Lord? woman who offered to draw water

Primary: Who fulfilled the sign? Rebekah

Intermediate: Who was Rebekah? Nahor's granddaughter

Primary: Why did her parents let her go with Abraham's servant? believed it was God's will

Intermediate: Where did Isaac meet her? in field

Primary: Was Isaac satisfied with Rebekah? yes

Senior: To what do Ishmael and Isaac correspond? first and second rational

Intermediate: What is represented by the well? the Word
