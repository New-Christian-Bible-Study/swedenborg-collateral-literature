
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was the "father of the Jews"? +
*A:* Abraham

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did he come from? +
*A:* Ur

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did he go to the Holy Land? +
*A:* God called him

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did he settle there? +
*A:* Hebron

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was Abraham's wife? +
*A:* Sarah

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was Hagar? +
*A:* her maid

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was Abraham's first son? +
*A:* Ishmael

*Q:* (Older Children): Whose son was Isaac? +
*A:* Sarah's

*Q:* (Older Children): Why were Hagar and Ishmael sent away? +
*A:* mocking attitude

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did Abraham not want Isaac to marry a Canaanite? +
*A:* idolaters

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whom did Abraham send to find a wife for Isaac? +
*A:* servant

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did he send him? +
*A:* Haran

*Q:* (Older Children): Who had settled there? +
*A:* Abraham's brother

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did Abraham's servant stop? +
*A:* at a well

*Q:* (Older Children): What sign did he ask of the Lord? +
*A:* woman who offered to draw water

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who fulfilled the sign? +
*A:* Rebekah

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Who was Rebekah? +
*A:* Nahor's granddaughter

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did her parents let her go with Abraham's servant? +
*A:* believed it was God's will

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Where did Isaac meet her? +
*A:* in field

*Q:* (Younger Children): Was Isaac satisfied with Rebekah? +
*A:* yes

*Q:* (Older Teens): To what do Ishmael and Isaac correspond? +
*A:* first and second rational

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is represented by the well? +
*A:* the Word
