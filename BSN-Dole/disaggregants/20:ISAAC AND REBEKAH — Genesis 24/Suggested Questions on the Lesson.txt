
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Who was the "father of the Jews"? Abraham

Older Children: Where did he come from? Ur

Older Children: Why did he go to the Holy Land? God called him

Older Children: Where did he settle there? Hebron

Younger Children: Who was Abraham's wife? Sarah

Younger Children: Who was Hagar? her maid

Younger Children: Who was Abraham's first son? Ishmael

Older Children: Whose son was Isaac? Sarah's

Older Children: Why were Hagar and Ishmael sent away? mocking attitude

Younger Teens: Why did Abraham not want Isaac to marry a Canaanite? idolaters

Younger Children: Whom did Abraham send to find a wife for Isaac? servant

Younger Children: Where did he send him? Haran

Older Children: Who had settled there? Abraham's brother

Younger Children: Where did Abraham's servant stop? at a well

Older Children: What sign did he ask of the Lord? woman who offered to draw water

Younger Children: Who fulfilled the sign? Rebekah

Younger Teens: Who was Rebekah? Nahor's granddaughter

Younger Children: Why did her parents let her go with Abraham's servant? believed it was God's will

Younger Teens: Where did Isaac meet her? in field

Younger Children: Was Isaac satisfied with Rebekah? yes

Older Teens: To what do Ishmael and Isaac correspond? first and second rational

Younger Teens: What is represented by the well? the Word
