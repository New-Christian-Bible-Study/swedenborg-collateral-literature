
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What book are we studying? Bible

Primary: Who wrote it? God

Primary: What is the first chapter of the Bible about? creation

Primary: What does Genesis mean? the beginning

Primary: What beautiful place was given to the first people for their home? Eden

Primary: How was the Garden of Eden watered? river

Primary: How did the Lord provide for men there? fruit trees, seed plants

Primary: What two trees are specifically mentioned? life, knowledge

Primary: In our chapter by what name are the first people called? Adam

Primary: What one thing did Adam want that he did not have? wife

Primary: What did the Lord ask Adam to do with regard to the animals and birds? name them

Primary: What did the Lord do when Adam was asleep? took rib, formed woman

Primary: What does mythology call the Garden of Eden? Golden Age

Junior: What does Swedenborg call it? Most Ancient Church

Intermediate: What is meant by man's being "alone"? close to God

Senior: What is meant by the rib? sense of self-hood, proprium

Intermediate: What is meant by the rib's being built into a woman? made beautiful by God

Senior: What is the basis of a true marriage? desire to serve the Lord together
