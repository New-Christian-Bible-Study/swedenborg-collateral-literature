
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What book are we studying? Bible

Younger Children: Who wrote it? God

Younger Children: What is the first chapter of the Bible about? creation

Younger Children: What does Genesis mean? the beginning

Younger Children: What beautiful place was given to the first people for their home? Eden

Younger Children: How was the Garden of Eden watered? river

Younger Children: How did the Lord provide for men there? fruit trees, seed plants

Younger Children: What two trees are specifically mentioned? life, knowledge

Younger Children: In our chapter by what name are the first people called? Adam

Younger Children: What one thing did Adam want that he did not have? wife

Younger Children: What did the Lord ask Adam to do with regard to the animals and birds? name them

Younger Children: What did the Lord do when Adam was asleep? took rib, formed woman

Younger Children: What does mythology call the Garden of Eden? Golden Age

Older Children: What does Swedenborg call it? Most Ancient Church

Younger Teens: What is meant by man's being "alone"? close to God

Older Teens: What is meant by the rib? sense of self-hood, proprium

Younger Teens: What is meant by the rib's being built into a woman? made beautiful by God

Older Teens: What is the basis of a true marriage? desire to serve the Lord together
