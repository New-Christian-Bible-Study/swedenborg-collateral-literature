
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What is the whole story of the Old Testament about? +
*A:* history of Israel

*Q:* (Younger Children): What event marks the beginning of the New Testament? +
*A:* birth of Jesus Christ

*Q:* (Older Children): Why were the Jews allowed to return from Babylon? +
*A:* to reâ€”establish Judaism in Canaan

*Q:* (Older Children): What three prophets prophesied after the return? +
*A:* Haggai, Zechariah, Malachi

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the last of these? +
*A:* Malachi

*Q:* (Older Children): How many years passed after the time of Malachi before the Lord was born? +
*A:* about four hundred

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why are the events of these years not described in the Word? +
*A:* no prophet arose

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many Gospels are there? +
*A:* four

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the Lord give us so many accounts of His life? +
*A:* fill out one another

*Q:* (Older Children): What can you tell about Mark? +
*A:* John Marcus, mother Christian, traveled with Peter

*Q:* (Younger Children): At what point in the Lord's life does Mark's Gospel begin? +
*A:* baptism of the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): What prophecy did John the Baptist fulfill? +
*A:* Malachi's

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did he live? +
*A:* wilderness

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he wear? +
*A:* camel's hair, leather belt

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was his food? +
*A:* locusts and wild honey

*Q:* (Older Children): What was his message? +
*A:* Repent!

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does baptism represent? +
*A:* cleansing lives with the Lord's truth

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who came from Nazareth to be baptized? +
*A:* Jesus

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened when the Lord was baptized? +
*A:* Spirit descended like dove

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the voice from heaven say? +
*A:* "Thou art my beloved Son"

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were the first four men the Lord called to be His disciples? +
*A:* Simon Peter, Andrew, James, John

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the rest of our chapter about? +
*A:* beginning of Lord's ministry of teaching, preaching, and healing
