
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What event marks the beginning of the New Testament? birth of Jesus Christ

Older Children: What three prophets prophesied after the return? Haggai, Zechariah, Malachi

Older Children: How many years passed after the time of Malachi before the Lord was born? about four hundred

Older Teens: Why are the events of these years not described in the Word? no prophet arose

Younger Children: How many Gospels are there? four

Older Children: Why did the Lord give us so many accounts of His life? fill out one another

Older Children: What can you tell about Mark? John Marcus, mother Christian, traveled with Peter

Younger Children: At what point in the Lord's life does Mark's Gospel begin? baptism of the Lord

Older Children: What prophecy did John the Baptist fulfill? Malachi's

Younger Children: Where did he live? wilderness

Younger Children: What did he wear? camel's hair, leather belt

Younger Children: What was his food? locusts and wild honey

Older Children: What was his message? Repent!

Younger Teens: What does baptism represent? cleansing lives with the Lord's truth

Younger Children: Who came from Nazareth to be baptized? Jesus

Younger Children: What happened when the Lord was baptized? Spirit descended like dove

Older Children: What did the voice from heaven say? "Thou art my beloved Son"

Older Children: Who were the first four men the Lord called to be His disciples? Simon Peter, Andrew, James, John

Younger Teens: What is the rest of our chapter about? beginning of Lord's ministry of teaching, preaching, and healing
