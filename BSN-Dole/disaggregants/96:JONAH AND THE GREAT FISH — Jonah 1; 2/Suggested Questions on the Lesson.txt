
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Which one of the Minor Prophets are we studying today? Jonah (fifth)

Older Children: When and where did Jonah prophesy? Israel, in time of Jeroboam 2

Older Children: Where do we find this information? 2 Kings 14:25

Older Children: What did the Lord tell Jonah to do? go and prophesy to Nineveh

Older Children: Where was Nineveh? capital of Assyria

Older Children: Why did Jonah not want to obey? too proud

Older Children: How did he try to avoid obeying? took a ship in the opposite direction

Younger Children: What happened to the ship? endangered by storm

Younger Children: How did the sailors find out who was to blame? cast lots

Younger Children: What did Jonah tell them to do with him? throw him overboard

Younger Children: What happened when they cast him into the sea? it became calm

Younger Children: How long was Jonah in the fish's belly? three days

Older Children: What kind of prayer did he make? thanks

Older Children: What did he promise? to obey God

Older Children: Do you know what he did after he was returned to land? went to Nineveh

Younger Teens: What does this story teach us? (1) not to run away from duty, (2) not to blame others for our troubles

Older Teens: What did the Lord say it pictured? His death and resurrection
