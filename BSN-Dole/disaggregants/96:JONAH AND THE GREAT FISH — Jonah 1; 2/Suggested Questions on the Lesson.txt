
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which one of the Minor Prophets are we studying today? +
*A:* Jonah (fifth)

*Q:* (Older Children): When and where did Jonah prophesy? +
*A:* Israel, in time of Jeroboam 2

*Q:* (Older Children): Where do we find this information? +
*A:* 2 Kings 14:25

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell Jonah to do? +
*A:* go and prophesy to Nineveh

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was Nineveh? +
*A:* capital of Assyria

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did Jonah not want to obey? +
*A:* too proud

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he try to avoid obeying? +
*A:* took a ship in the opposite direction

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to the ship? +
*A:* endangered by storm

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the sailors find out who was to blame? +
*A:* cast lots

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Jonah tell them to do with him? +
*A:* throw him overboard

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened when they cast him into the sea? +
*A:* it became calm

*Q:* (Younger Children): How long was Jonah in the fish's belly? +
*A:* three days

*Q:* (Older Children): What kind of prayer did he make? +
*A:* thanks

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he promise? +
*A:* to obey God

*Q:* (Older Children): Do you know what he did after he was returned to land? +
*A:* went to Nineveh

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does this story teach us? +
*A:* (1) not to run away from duty, (2) not to blame others for our troubles

*Q:* (Older Teens): What did the Lord say it pictured? +
*A:* His death and resurrection
