
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Where did Ezekiel and Daniel prophesy? in Babylon

Younger Children: Where was Ezekiel taken in his final vision? Jerusalem

Older Children: What did his angel guide first measure? the temple

Younger Children: What did he then see which was not in the temple as he had known it? water

Older Children: How far did the angel measure the river each time? a thousand cubits

Older Children: What were the three depths at which Ezekiel was allowed to cross it? ankle, knee, loins

Younger Children: How deep did it finally become? over his head

Younger Children: What grew beside the river? trees

Younger Children: Who stood upon its banks? fishermen

Older Children: What happened to everything the river touched? became healthy

Older Children: Was there any place to which it did not bring life? marshes

Older Children: What was done with such places? left to salt

Younger Children: What were the leaves of the trees for? healing

Younger Children: What does the river picture? the Word

Younger Teens: What are pictured by (1) leaves, and (2) fruit? (1) good thoughts, (2) good deeds

Older Teens: How must we think of the Word and use it if we wish to understand it more and more deeply all the time? the inexhaustible Word of God to us
