
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Ezekiel and Daniel prophesy? +
*A:* in Babylon

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was Ezekiel taken in his final vision? +
*A:* Jerusalem

*Q:* (Older Children): What did his angel guide first measure? +
*A:* the temple

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he then see which was not in the temple as he had known it? +
*A:* water

*Q:* (Older Children): How far did the angel measure the river each time? +
*A:* a thousand cubits

*Q:* (Older Children): What were the three depths at which Ezekiel was allowed to cross it? +
*A:* ankle, knee, loins

*Q:* (Younger Children): How deep did it finally become? +
*A:* over his head

*Q:* (Younger Children): What grew beside the river? +
*A:* trees

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who stood upon its banks? +
*A:* fishermen

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to everything the river touched? +
*A:* became healthy

*Q:* (Older Children): Was there any place to which it did not bring life? +
*A:* marshes

*Q:* (Older Children): What was done with such places? +
*A:* left to salt

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were the leaves of the trees for? +
*A:* healing

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does the river picture? +
*A:* the Word

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What are pictured by (1) leaves, and (2) fruit? +
*A:* (1) good thoughts, (2) good deeds

*Q:* (Older Teens): How must we think of the Word and use it if we wish to understand it more and more deeply all the time? +
*A:* the inexhaustible Word of God to us
