
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Where did Moses die? Mount Nebo

Younger Children: Who succeeded him as leader? Joshua

Older Children: What charge was given to Joshua? Be strong and of a good courage

Older Children: Where was the last encampment of the Israelites in the wilderness? opposite Jericho

Older Children: At what time of year did they reach it? harvest

Younger Children: What barred their way into the Holy Land? Jordan

Younger Children: How was the river parted for them? by a miracle

Older Children: What did Joshua set up in the middle of the river bed? twelve stones

Older Children: What did he set up at their first camp in the Holy Land? twelve stones

Younger Teens: Why did he set up these stones? reminder of miracle

Older Children: What was this first camping place called? Gilgal (rolling)

Older Children: Why? reproach of Egypt "rolled away"

Older Children: What else happened there? Passover, circumcision

Younger Teens: What does Joshua represent in us? the truth fighting

Younger Teens: What does the Jordan represent? introductory truths to spiritual living

Older Teens: What is pictured by crossing the Jordan into the Holy Land? beginning to change will and hence to live truly spiritual lives
