
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What three kinds of work did the Lord do in His ministry? +
*A:* teaching, preaching, healing

*Q:* (Younger Children): About which kind is our lesson for today? +
*A:* healing

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is a miracle? +
*A:* wonderful thing done by the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): Can you tell what some of the troubles the Lord healed were? +
*A:* leprosy, palsy, fever, possession

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did He do for Peter's wife's mother? +
*A:* cured fever

*Q:* (Older Children): How did He heal the centurion's servant? +
*A:* by His words

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did He go with some of His disciples? +
*A:* into a boat

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened while they were on the sea? +
*A:* storm

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord say when they woke Him? +
*A:* Why are you afraid?

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord do? +
*A:* stilled the storm

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do different kinds of diseases represent? +
*A:* weaknesses and deformities of the soul

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What was the condition which had to be fulfilled if the Lord was to heal a person? +
*A:* had to have faith in the Lord

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did the Lord perform miracles when He was on earth? +
*A:* to strengthen faith; to record in Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why should we not ask for miracles today? +
*A:* tend to force belief
