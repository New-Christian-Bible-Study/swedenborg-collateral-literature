
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What three kinds of work did the Lord do in His ministry? teaching, preaching, healing

Primary: About which kind is our lesson for today? healing

Primary: What is a miracle? wonderful thing done by the Lord

Junior: Can you tell what some of the troubles the Lord healed were? leprosy, palsy, fever, possession

Primary: What did He do for Peter's wife's mother? cured fever

Junior: How did He heal the centurion's servant? by His words

Primary: Where did He go with some of His disciples? into a boat

Primary: What happened while they were on the sea? storm

Primary: What did the Lord say when they woke Him? Why are you afraid?

Primary: What did the Lord do? stilled the storm

Intermediate: What do different kinds of diseases represent? weaknesses and deformities of the soul

Intermediate: What was the condition which had to be fulfilled if the Lord was to heal a person? had to have faith in the Lord

Senior: Why did the Lord perform miracles when He was on earth? to strengthen faith; to record in Word

Senior: Why should we not ask for miracles today? tend to force belief
