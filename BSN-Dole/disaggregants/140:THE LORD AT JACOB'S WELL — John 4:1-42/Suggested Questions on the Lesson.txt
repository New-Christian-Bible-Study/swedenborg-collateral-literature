
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What were the three divisions of the Holy Land in the Lord's time? Galilee, Samaria, Judea

Older Children: Which was the middle one? Samaria

Older Children: Who were the Samaritans? foreigners brought in by Assyria

Older Children: What journey was the Lord making at the time of our lesson for today? Judea to Galilee

Younger Children: Where did He stop to rest? Jacob's well

Older Children: What can you tell about the parcel of ground where Jacob's well was? Jacob had bought it, and later gave it to Joseph

Younger Children: Who came to the well as the Lord sat there? a Samaritan woman

Older Children: Why was she surprised when the Lord asked her to give Him a drink? Jews didn't talk to Samaritans

Older Children: What did the Lord tell her? you should have asked me ...

Older Children: What did He tell her about herself which showed her He was a prophet? five husbands

Older Children: What did He tell her about Himself? I am Christ

Older Children: What did He tell the disciples about His food? to do God's will

Older Children: What made the Samaritans believe in the Lord? His words

Younger Teens: What does Jacob's well represent? the Word

Older Teens: What is "living" water? divine truth lived
