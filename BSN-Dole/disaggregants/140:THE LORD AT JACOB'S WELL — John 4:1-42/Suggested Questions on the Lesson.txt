
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What were the three divisions of the Holy Land in the Lord's time? +
*A:* Galilee, Samaria, Judea

*Q:* (Older Children): Which was the middle one? +
*A:* Samaria

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were the Samaritans? +
*A:* foreigners brought in by Assyria

*Q:* (Older Children): What journey was the Lord making at the time of our lesson for today? +
*A:* Judea to Galilee

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did He stop to rest? +
*A:* Jacob's well

*Q:* (Older Children): What can you tell about the parcel of ground where Jacob's well was? +
*A:* Jacob had bought it, and later gave it to Joseph

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who came to the well as the Lord sat there? +
*A:* a Samaritan woman

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was she surprised when the Lord asked her to give Him a drink? +
*A:* Jews didn't talk to Samaritans

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell her? +
*A:* you should have asked me ...

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell her about herself which showed her He was a prophet? +
*A:* five husbands

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell her about Himself? +
*A:* I am Christ

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell the disciples about His food? +
*A:* to do God's will

*Q:* (Older Children): What made the Samaritans believe in the Lord? +
*A:* His words

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Jacob's well represent? +
*A:* the Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is "living" water? +
*A:* divine truth lived
