
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who created the earth and everything on it? +
*A:* God

*Q:* (Younger Children): In how many days or stages was it created? +
*A:* six

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the final creation? +
*A:* man

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord do on the seventh day? +
*A:* rested

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He say of this day? +
*A:* blessed and sanctified

*Q:* (Older Children): What do blessed and sanctified mean? +
*A:* made happy, made holy

*Q:* (Older Children): What does this teach us about how we should use Sunday? +
*A:* make others happy, worship

*Q:* (Younger Teens): By what story in the Bible is the state of the people of the first church on earth pictured? +
*A:* Eden

*Q:* (Older Children): What one prohibition did the Lord give them? +
*A:* not eat of tree of knowledge

*Q:* (Older Children): How were they tempted to disobey? +
*A:* serpent

*Q:* (Older Children): What was their punishment? +
*A:* driven from garden

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our story for today what two sons were born to Adam and Eve? +
*A:* Cain, Abel

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was Cain's occupation? +
*A:* farmer

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was Abel's occupation? +
*A:* shepherd

*Q:* (Older Children): What offerings did they bring to the Lord? +
*A:* grain, lamb

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whose offering was accepted? +
*A:* Abel's

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was Cain's offering rejected? +
*A:* insincere

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Cain do which showed his true character? +
*A:* killed Abel

*Q:* (Older Children): What was his punishment? +
*A:* wanderer

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord save him from being destroyed? +
*A:* mark

Younger Teens. What do Cain and Abel represent? faith, charity

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is represented by Cain's slaying Abel? +
*A:* thinking it is enough to know what is right without doing it
