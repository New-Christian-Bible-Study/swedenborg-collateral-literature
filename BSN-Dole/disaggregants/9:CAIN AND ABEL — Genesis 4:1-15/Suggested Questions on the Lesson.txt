
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who created the earth and everything on it? God

Younger Children: In how many days or stages was it created? six

Older Children: What was the final creation? man

Younger Children: What did the Lord do on the seventh day? rested

Older Children: What did He say of this day? blessed and sanctified

Older Children: What do blessed and sanctified mean? made happy, made holy

Older Children: What does this teach us about how we should use Sunday? make others happy, worship

Younger Teens: By what story in the Bible is the state of the people of the first church on earth pictured? Eden

Older Children: What one prohibition did the Lord give them? not eat of tree of knowledge

Older Children: How were they tempted to disobey? serpent

Older Children: What was their punishment? driven from garden

Younger Children: In our story for today what two sons were born to Adam and Eve? Cain, Abel

Younger Children: What was Cain's occupation? farmer

Younger Children: What was Abel's occupation? shepherd

Older Children: What offerings did they bring to the Lord? grain, lamb

Younger Children: Whose offering was accepted? Abel's

Older Children: Why was Cain's offering rejected? insincere

Younger Children: What did Cain do which showed his true character? killed Abel

Older Children: What was his punishment? wanderer

Older Children: How did the Lord save him from being destroyed? mark

Intermediate. What do Cain and Abel represent? faith, charity

Older Teens: What is represented by Cain's slaying Abel? thinking it is enough to know what is right without doing it
