
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who created the earth and everything on it? God

Primary: In how many days or stages was it created? six

Junior: What was the final creation? man

Primary: What did the Lord do on the seventh day? rested

Junior: What did He say of this day? blessed and sanctified

Junior: What do blessed and sanctified mean? made happy, made holy

Junior: What does this teach us about how we should use Sunday? make others happy, worship

Intermediate: By what story in the Bible is the state of the people of the first church on earth pictured? Eden

Junior: What one prohibition did the Lord give them? not eat of tree of knowledge

Junior: How were they tempted to disobey? serpent

Junior: What was their punishment? driven from garden

Primary: In our story for today what two sons were born to Adam and Eve? Cain, Abel

Primary: What was Cain's occupation? farmer

Primary: What was Abel's occupation? shepherd

Junior: What offerings did they bring to the Lord? grain, lamb

Primary: Whose offering was accepted? Abel's

Junior: Why was Cain's offering rejected? insincere

Primary: What did Cain do which showed his true character? killed Abel

Junior: What was his punishment? wanderer

Junior: How did the Lord save him from being destroyed? mark

Intermediate. What do Cain and Abel represent? faith, charity

Senior: What is represented by Cain's slaying Abel? thinking it is enough to know what is right without doing it
