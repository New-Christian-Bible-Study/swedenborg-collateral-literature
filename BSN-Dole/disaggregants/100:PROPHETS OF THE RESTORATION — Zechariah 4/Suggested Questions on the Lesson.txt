
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: How long did the captivity in Babylon last? about seventy years

Primary: What king permitted the people to go back to the Holy Land? Cyrus of Persia

Primary: What did he tell them to do? rebuild the temple

Junior: Who was their leader? Zerubabbel

Primary: Did all the people want to go back? no

Primary: What were those who remained in Babylon told to do? give money and supplies

Junior: Why did those who returned interrupt the building of the temple? more interested in their own comfort

Primary: What did they build instead? houses for themselves

Primary: How did the Lord rebuke them? through prophets (Haggai and Zechariah)

Junior: In our chapter how did Zechariah's vision come to him? from an angel

Junior: What did he see in the vision? lampstand and two olive trees

Junior: What message was given him for Zerubbabel? "not by might, nor by power, but by my spirit"

Junior: What did the angel tell him the lamps were? the eyes of the Lord

Intermediate: What were the two olive trees? two anointed ones (love of Lord and neighbor)

Intermediate: What does the temple represent? a heavenly character, or the church in us

Intermediate: What is pictured by the lampstand? the Word, containing divine truth

Senior: What are the "eyes of the Lord"? His divine providence

Senior: What do the olive trees picture? the good of love to the Lord and love to the neighbor
