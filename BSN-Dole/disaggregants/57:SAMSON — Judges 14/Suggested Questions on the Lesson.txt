
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What mistake did the Israelites make after Joshua's death? forgot God

Older Children: What was the result? enemies rose up

Younger Children: How did the Lord help them when they turned to Him? Judges

Older Children: What tribe did Samson belong to? Dan

Younger Children: What were his parents told he should be? Nazarite

Younger Children: What was the special sign of a Nazarite? uncut hair

Older Children: What enemy was threatening Israel in Samson's part of the country? Philistines

Younger Children: What was the secret of Samson's strength? his hair

Older Children: What was his weakness? women, pride

Younger Children: What feat of strength did he perform when he first went into Philistia? killed lion

Younger Children: What did he find the second time he went down? honey

Older Children: What riddle did he ask the Philistines? "Out of the eater ... "

Older Children: What can you tell about Samson's later life and his death? hair cut, blinded ...

Younger Teens: What do the Philistines represent? desire to know truth but not live according to it

Younger Teens: What is meant by Samson's strength being in his hair? power in ultimates

Older Teens: What does Samson's riddle teach us? happiness can come from struggle
