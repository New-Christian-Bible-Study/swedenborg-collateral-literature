
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who prepared the way of the Lord? +
*A:* John the Baptist

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened when the Lord was baptized? +
*A:* dove appeared

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were the first four disciples called? +
*A:* Peter, Andrew, James, John

*Q:* (Older Children): What three forms did the Lord's ministry take? +
*A:* preaching, teaching, healing

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did He preach? +
*A:* in the synagogues, many other places

*Q:* (Older Children): Into what three parts was the Holy Land divided in the Lord's time? +
*A:* Galilee, Samaria, Judea

*Q:* (Older Children): In what part was He born? +
*A:* Judea

*Q:* (Older Children): In what part did He grow up? +
*A:* Galilee

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did most of His ministry take place? +
*A:* Galilee

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did He live during His ministry? +
*A:* Capernaum (by the Sea of Galilee)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What miracle is described in our chapter? +
*A:* healing paralytic or palsied man

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was the palsied man brought to the Lord? +
*A:* on a bed or pallet

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord do for him first? +
*A:* forgave his sins

*Q:* (Older Children): What disciple was called in our chapter? +
*A:* Levi (Matthew)

*Q:* (Older Children): What was his occupation? +
*A:* tax collector

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were (1) the scribes, and (2) the Pharisees? +
*A:* (1) those who copied Scripture, (2) legalists

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the people despise the publicans? +
*A:* collected taxes for Rome

*Q:* (Older Children): What was their first complaint against the Lord? +
*A:* He ate with tax collectors

*Q:* (Older Children): What was their second complaint? +
*A:* His disciples didn't fast

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord say about the new cloth and new wine? +
*A:* need to go together

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the third complaint? +
*A:* He broke the sabbath (picking grain)

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord say about the sabbath? +
*A:* made for man

*Q:* (Older Teens): How should the sabbath be used? +
*A:* to worship, to learn about God, to do good
