
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who prepared the way of the Lord? John the Baptist

Primary: What happened when the Lord was baptized? dove appeared

Junior: Who were the first four disciples called? Peter, Andrew, James, John

Junior: What three forms did the Lord's ministry take? preaching, teaching, healing

Junior: Where did He preach? in the synagogues, many other places

Junior: Into what three parts was the Holy Land divided in the Lord's time? Galilee, Samaria, Judea

Junior: In what part was He born? Judea

Junior: In what part did He grow up? Galilee

Junior: Where did most of His ministry take place? Galilee

Junior: Where did He live during His ministry? Capernaum (by the Sea of Galilee)

Primary: What miracle is described in our chapter? healing paralytic or palsied man

Primary: How was the palsied man brought to the Lord? on a bed or pallet

Junior: What did the Lord do for him first? forgave his sins

Junior: What disciple was called in our chapter? Levi (Matthew)

Junior: What was his occupation? tax collector

Junior: Who were (1) the scribes, and (2) the Pharisees? (1) those who copied Scripture, (2) legalists

Junior: Why did the people despise the publicans? collected taxes for Rome

Junior: What was their first complaint against the Lord? He ate with tax collectors

Junior: What was their second complaint? His disciples didn't fast

Primary: What did the Lord say about the new cloth and new wine? need to go together

Junior: What was the third complaint? He broke the sabbath (picking grain)

Intermediate: What did the Lord say about the sabbath? made for man

Senior: How should the sabbath be used? to worship, to learn about God, to do good
