
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What king of Judah is our lesson about today? Asa

Younger Children: Was he a good or a bad king? good

Younger Children: What did he do that was good? destroyed idols

Older Children: What did he not do that he should have done? remove high places

Older Children: How did the king of Israel try to overcome Asa? built Ramah

Older Children: To whom did Asa send for help? Ben-hadad, king of Syria

Younger Children: What did Asa use for presents for the king of Syria? gold and silver of temple

Younger Children: What physical trouble did Asa have in his old age? diseased feet

Younger Teens: What does idolatry mean for us? "worshiping" such things as money, power, etc.

Older Teens: What do the "high places" mean? pride in one's own intelligence
