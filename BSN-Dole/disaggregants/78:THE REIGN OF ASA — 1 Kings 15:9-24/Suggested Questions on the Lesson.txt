
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What king of Judah is our lesson about today? Asa

Primary: Was he a good or a bad king? good

Primary: What did he do that was good? destroyed idols

Junior: What did he not do that he should have done? remove high places

Junior: How did the king of Israel try to overcome Asa? built Ramah

Junior: To whom did Asa send for help? Ben-hadad, king of Syria

Primary: What did Asa use for presents for the king of Syria? gold and silver of temple

Primary: What physical trouble did Asa have in his old age? diseased feet

Intermediate: What does idolatry mean for us? "worshiping" such things as money, power, etc.

Senior: What do the "high places" mean? pride in one's own intelligence
