
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What was the book which John saw in the right hand of the Lord? the Word

Younger Children: With how many seals was it sealed? seven

Older Children: Who was to open the book? the Lord

Older Children: In our lesson today who are standing before the throne? the dead

Younger Teens: What are the "books" which are opened? individual character

Younger Teens: How are the dead judged? by the degree of love to the Lord and the neighbor

Older Children: What happened to those who were not found written in the book of life? cast into lake of fire

Older Children: At the beginning of the next chapter, what does John see? new heaven, new earth

Younger Children: Where in the Bible did we first hear of the heaven and the earth? Genesis 1

Younger Children: What does John see coming down from God out of heaven? holy city

Younger Children: What is the name of the city? New Jerusalem

Older Children: Why is this name so familiar to us? name of our church

Older Children: What are some of the blessings people find in this city? no tears, pain, death

Older Children: What does the Lord call Himself? Alpha and omega

Older Children: What are Alpha and omega? first and last letters of Greek alphabet

Older Children: What does the Lord promise? water of life

Younger Teens: What kind of thirst is He speaking about? for truth

Older Teens: What is the "fountain of the water of life"? divine truth in the Word

Older Teens: What is meant by becoming a son of God? developing in the image and likeness of God
