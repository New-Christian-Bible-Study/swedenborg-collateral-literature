
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: How long was the Lord's ministry? about three years

Junior: Why did He frequently go down to Jerusalem? for the feast days

Primary: Why did He go there at the beginning of the last week of His life on earth? Passover

Primary: What kind of animal did He ride into Jerusalem on that day? a donkey

Junior: Why did most of the people welcome Him? thought He would become king

Primary: What did they carry in their hands? palm branches

Primary: What did they cry? Hosanna

Junior: What does Hosanna mean? save now

Intermediate: Why did the Lord ride on a young donkey? sign of royalty, to fulfill prophecy (Zechariah 9:9)

Junior: Who were angry at His coming? scribes and Pharisees

Junior: What Gentiles were anxious to see Him? Greeks

Junior: Which apostles were asked to introduce them to Him? Philip and Andrew

Junior: How did the Lord explain the necessity of His death? parable of grain of wheat

Junior: What did He say we must do to save our spiritual lives? lose our selfish life

Junior: Why did He say the scribes and Pharisees could not see the truth? didn't want to

Junior: Why did some who did believe Him not support Him openly? loved praises of men ...

Intermediate: What is it that judges us? divine truth

Senior: What is represented by the Lord's riding on a young donkey? His mastery of inclination to judge by worldly reasoning or outward appearance

Senior: What is represented by the palm branches? the divine truth of the letter of the Word
