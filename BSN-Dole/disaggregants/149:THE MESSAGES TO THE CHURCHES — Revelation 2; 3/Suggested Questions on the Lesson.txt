
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who saw the vision described in the book of Revelation? +
*A:* the apostle John

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was John when he saw it? +
*A:* Patmos

*Q:* (Younger Children): At the beginning of the vision whom did he see? +
*A:* the risen Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): Can you describe how the Lord looked? +
*A:* [see Chapter 1]

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did John see around the Lord? +
*A:* seven lampstands

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell John the seven lampstands were? +
*A:* churches

*Q:* (Older Children): Where were these seven churches? +
*A:* Asia Minor

*Q:* (Older Children): What is our lesson today about? +
*A:* messages to those churches

*Q:* (Older Children): How were all the messages alike? +
*A:* praised good, condemned faults, made promise

*Q:* (Older Children): How were they different? +
*A:* different faults and rewards

*Q:* (Older Children): Which church is described as the most faithful of all? +
*A:* Philadelphia

*Q:* (Younger Children): In the message to Philadelphia what is said about a door? +
*A:* open

*Q:* (Older Children): What was wrong with the church of Laodicea? +
*A:* lukewarm

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord say to that church about a door? +
*A:* closed

*Q:* (Older Children): With what words does each of the seven messages close? +
*A:* He that hath an ear ...

*Q:* (Older Children): What do these words mean? +
*A:* obey, if you are willing

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How do these two chapters apply to us? +
*A:* we are free to open or close "door"

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why is the condemnation of Laodicea's "lukewarmness" so severe? +
*A:* hardest fault to overcome
