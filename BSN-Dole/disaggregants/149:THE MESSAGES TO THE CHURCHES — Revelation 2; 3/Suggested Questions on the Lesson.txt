
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who saw the vision described in the book of Revelation? the apostle John

Junior: Where was John when he saw it? Patmos

Primary: At the beginning of the vision whom did he see? the risen Lord

Primary: Can you describe how the Lord looked? [see Chapter 1]

Primary: What did John see around the Lord? seven lampstands

Junior: What did the Lord tell John the seven lampstands were? churches

Junior: Where were these seven churches? Asia Minor

Junior: What is our lesson today about? messages to those churches

Junior: How were all the messages alike? praised good, condemned faults, made promise

Junior: How were they different? different faults and rewards

Junior: Which church is described as the most faithful of all? Philadelphia

Primary: In the message to Philadelphia what is said about a door? open

Junior: What was wrong with the church of Laodicea? lukewarm

Primary: What did the Lord say to that church about a door? closed

Junior: With what words does each of the seven messages close? He that hath an ear ...

Junior: What do these words mean? obey, if you are willing

Intermediate: How do these two chapters apply to us? we are free to open or close "door"

Senior: Why is the condemnation of Laodicea's "lukewarmness" so severe? hardest fault to overcome
