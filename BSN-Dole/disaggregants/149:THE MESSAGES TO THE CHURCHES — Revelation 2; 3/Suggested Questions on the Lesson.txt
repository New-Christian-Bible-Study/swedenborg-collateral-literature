
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who saw the vision described in the book of Revelation? the apostle John

Older Children: Where was John when he saw it? Patmos

Younger Children: At the beginning of the vision whom did he see? the risen Lord

Younger Children: Can you describe how the Lord looked? [see Chapter 1]

Younger Children: What did John see around the Lord? seven lampstands

Older Children: What did the Lord tell John the seven lampstands were? churches

Older Children: Where were these seven churches? Asia Minor

Older Children: What is our lesson today about? messages to those churches

Older Children: How were all the messages alike? praised good, condemned faults, made promise

Older Children: How were they different? different faults and rewards

Older Children: Which church is described as the most faithful of all? Philadelphia

Younger Children: In the message to Philadelphia what is said about a door? open

Older Children: What was wrong with the church of Laodicea? lukewarm

Younger Children: What did the Lord say to that church about a door? closed

Older Children: With what words does each of the seven messages close? He that hath an ear ...

Older Children: What do these words mean? obey, if you are willing

Younger Teens: How do these two chapters apply to us? we are free to open or close "door"

Older Teens: Why is the condemnation of Laodicea's "lukewarmness" so severe? hardest fault to overcome
