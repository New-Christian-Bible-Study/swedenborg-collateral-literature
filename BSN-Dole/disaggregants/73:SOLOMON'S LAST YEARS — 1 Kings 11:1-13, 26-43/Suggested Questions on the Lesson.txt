
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the second son of David and Bathsheba? Solomon

Younger Children: Who was the third king of Israel? Solomon

Older Children: What did he ask of the Lord? wisdom

Older Children: What did the Lord give him? that, plus wealth, fame

Younger Children: What did he build in Jerusalem? temple

Older Children: What sin did he commit? married many foreign women

Older Children: To what further sin did it lead him? idolatry

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him? kingdom would be lost

Older Children: Would this happen before he died? no

Older Children: How much of Solomon's kingdom would his son have? one (two?) tribes {cf 1 Kings 11:32}

Older Children: Who was to be king of the rest? Jeroboam

Older Children: Who was Jeroboam? servant of Solomon

Older Children: Who told him he would be king? Ahijah

Older Children: What sign was given him? torn robe

Older Children: What did Solomon try to do? kill him

Older Children: Where did Jeroboam take refuge? Egypt

Younger Teens: What does Solomon represent? peace of soul

Older Teens: What does Solomon's sin picture? excusing or ignoring evil
