
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the second son of David and Bathsheba? Solomon

Primary: Who was the third king of Israel? Solomon

Junior: What did he ask of the Lord? wisdom

Junior: What did the Lord give him? that, plus wealth, fame

Primary: What did he build in Jerusalem? temple

Junior: What sin did he commit? married many foreign women

Junior: To what further sin did it lead him? idolatry

Primary: What did the Lord tell him? kingdom would be lost

Junior: Would this happen before he died? no

Junior: How much of Solomon's kingdom would his son have? one (two?) tribes {cf 1 Kings 11:32}

Junior: Who was to be king of the rest? Jeroboam

Junior: Who was Jeroboam? servant of Solomon

Junior: Who told him he would be king? Ahijah

Junior: What sign was given him? torn robe

Junior: What did Solomon try to do? kill him

Junior: Where did Jeroboam take refuge? Egypt

Intermediate: What does Solomon represent? peace of soul

Senior: What does Solomon's sin picture? excusing or ignoring evil
