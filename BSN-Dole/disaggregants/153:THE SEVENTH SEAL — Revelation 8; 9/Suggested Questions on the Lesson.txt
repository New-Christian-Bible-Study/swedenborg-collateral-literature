
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What was the book sealed with seven seals? the Word

Junior: Who opened it? the Lord

Primary: What did John see when the first seal was opened? white horse

Primary: What were the colors of the other three horses? red, black, pale

Junior: What was the rider on the pale horse called? Death

Junior: Who were seen when the fifth seal was opened? the good under the altar

Junior: Who were seen when the sixth seal was opened? the evil

Junior: What did these last people ask? let the rocks hide us

Junior: What happened immediately after the seventh seal was opened? silence

Primary: How many angels did John see standing before God? seven

Primary: What were given them? trumpets

Primary: What did an eighth angel have? gold censer

Junior: What did he first do with his censer? put incense on it

Junior: What did he then fill it with? fire

Primary: What happened when he cast it to the earth? thunder, lightning, earthquake

Junior: In the two chapters of our lesson how many trumpets were sounded? six

Junior: Can you tell some of the things that happened? hail, fire, mountains into sea, star fell, third of sun darkened

Junior: What was the name of the star that fell from heaven? Wormwood

Junior: What two names are given for the angel of the bottomless pit? Abaddon, Apollyon (destroyer)

Primary: Did the people to whom all these things happened repent? no

Intermediate: What do these two chapters treat of in their internal sense? the gradual destruction of truth and goodness in first Christian era

Senior: What is it which determines whether we go to heaven or to hell? whether we follow or reject the Lord's guidance
