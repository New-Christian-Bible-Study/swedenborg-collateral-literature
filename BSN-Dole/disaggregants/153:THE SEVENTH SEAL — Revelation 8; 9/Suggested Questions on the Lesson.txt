
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What was the book sealed with seven seals? the Word

Older Children: Who opened it? the Lord

Younger Children: What did John see when the first seal was opened? white horse

Younger Children: What were the colors of the other three horses? red, black, pale

Older Children: What was the rider on the pale horse called? Death

Older Children: Who were seen when the fifth seal was opened? the good under the altar

Older Children: Who were seen when the sixth seal was opened? the evil

Older Children: What did these last people ask? let the rocks hide us

Older Children: What happened immediately after the seventh seal was opened? silence

Younger Children: How many angels did John see standing before God? seven

Younger Children: What were given them? trumpets

Younger Children: What did an eighth angel have? gold censer

Older Children: What did he first do with his censer? put incense on it

Older Children: What did he then fill it with? fire

Younger Children: What happened when he cast it to the earth? thunder, lightning, earthquake

Older Children: In the two chapters of our lesson how many trumpets were sounded? six

Older Children: Can you tell some of the things that happened? hail, fire, mountains into sea, star fell, third of sun darkened

Older Children: What was the name of the star that fell from heaven? Wormwood

Older Children: What two names are given for the angel of the bottomless pit? Abaddon, Apollyon (destroyer)

Younger Children: Did the people to whom all these things happened repent? no

Younger Teens: What do these two chapters treat of in their internal sense? the gradual destruction of truth and goodness in first Christian era

Older Teens: What is it which determines whether we go to heaven or to hell? whether we follow or reject the Lord's guidance
