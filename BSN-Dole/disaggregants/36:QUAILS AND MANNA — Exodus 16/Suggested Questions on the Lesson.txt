
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What feast did the Israelites hold just before they left Egypt? Passover

Younger Children: How were they to be led on their journey? pillar of cloud/fire

Older Children: How did the Lord help them at the Red Sea? parted water

Younger Children: What complaint did they make when they reached the wilderness of Sin? no meat

Younger Children: What did the Lord send them in the evening? quails

Younger Children: What did they find on the ground in the morning? morsels of "bread"

Younger Children: What did they call it? manna

Older Children: What does manna mean? "what is it?"

Older Children: What directions did the Lord give them for gathering it? one omer each daily

Older Children: What happened when some of the people disobeyed? it spoiled

Older Children: How were they fed on the sabbath? double amount on sixth day

Younger Children: How long did the Lord feed the children of Israel with the manna? forty years

Younger Children: Of what petition in the Lord's Prayer does this lesson remind us? ... our daily bread

Younger Teens: What does the journey of the Israelites through the wilderness picture? reformation

Older Teens: What do the quails represent? external satisfaction from doing good

Older Teens: What does the manna represent? spiritual goodness which comes from the Lord as we try to learn and live truths from the Word
