
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What feast did the Israelites hold just before they left Egypt? +
*A:* Passover

*Q:* (Younger Children): How were they to be led on their journey? +
*A:* pillar of cloud/fire

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord help them at the Red Sea? +
*A:* parted water

*Q:* (Younger Children): What complaint did they make when they reached the wilderness of Sin? +
*A:* no meat

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord send them in the evening? +
*A:* quails

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they find on the ground in the morning? +
*A:* morsels of "bread"

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they call it? +
*A:* manna

*Q:* (Older Children): What does manna mean? +
*A:* "what is it?"

*Q:* (Older Children): What directions did the Lord give them for gathering it? +
*A:* one omer each daily

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened when some of the people disobeyed? +
*A:* it spoiled

*Q:* (Older Children): How were they fed on the sabbath? +
*A:* double amount on sixth day

*Q:* (Younger Children): How long did the Lord feed the children of Israel with the manna? +
*A:* forty years

*Q:* (Younger Children): Of what petition in the Lord's Prayer does this lesson remind us? +
*A:* ... our daily bread

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the journey of the Israelites through the wilderness picture? +
*A:* reformation

*Q:* (Older Teens): What do the quails represent? +
*A:* external satisfaction from doing good

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the manna represent? +
*A:* spiritual goodness which comes from the Lord as we try to learn and live truths from the Word
