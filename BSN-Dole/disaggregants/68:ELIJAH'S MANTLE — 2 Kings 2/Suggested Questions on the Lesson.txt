
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What happened to Solomon's kingdom after he died? divided

Primary: What were the two divisions of the kingdom called? Israel, Judah

Primary: Which remained faithful to the line of David? Judah

Primary: What two great prophets were sent to Israel? Elijah, Elisha

Primary: When Elijah's work was ended, what did Elisha ask? double portion of power

Primary: What did Elijah tell him? granted, if you see me go

Primary: What vision was granted to Elisha? fiery chariots and horses

Primary: What fell from Elijah as he was taken up to heaven? his mantle

Junior: How did the sons of the prophets know that Elisha had become the successor of Elijah? saw him part waters of Jordan with mantle

Junior: What miracle did Elisha perform for them? "healed" water

Junior: What name did some children call Elisha? baldhead

Junior: Why was this so bad? a sign of disgrace

Junior: What happened to the children? mauled by bears

Intermediate: What do the two parts of the divided kingdom represent? mind, heart 

Intermediate: What does the prophet's mantle represent? letter of Word

Senior: What is the difference in correspondence between (1) Elijah, and (2) Elisha? (1) Word studied from obedience (2) Word studied with awareness of inner sense
