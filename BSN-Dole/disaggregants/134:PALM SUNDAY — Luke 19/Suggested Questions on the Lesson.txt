
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is today called? Why? +
*A:* Palm Sunday; people spread palm branches (John 12:13)

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our chapter, where was the Lord going? +
*A:* Jerusalem

*Q:* (Younger Children): What place did He pass through as noted at the beginning of the chapter? +
*A:* Jericho

*Q:* (Older Children): What is the difference between this journey and that of the man in the parable of the Good Samaritan? +
*A:* going the other way

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was Zacchaeus? +
*A:* a tax collector (publican)

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why couldn't Zacchaeus see the Lord? +
*A:* he was short

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he do in order to see Him? +
*A:* climbed a tree

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord say to him? +
*A:* come down ...

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Zacchaeus say which showed his character? +
*A:* "I give half to poor ... "

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell him? +
*A:* this day is salvation come to this house

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Pharisees feel about it? +
*A:* murmured against Him

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell them? +
*A:* parable

*Q:* (Older Children): What parable did He give them? +
*A:* pounds

*Q:* (Older Children): Can you tell this parable? +
*A:* ten servants, pound apiece ...

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does it teach us? +
*A:* use as well as we can what the Lord has given us

*Q:* (Younger Children): When the Lord came near Jerusalem, what did He tell two of His disciples to do? +
*A:* go and untie colt

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were they to say to the owner of the colt? +
*A:* the Lord needs him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Was the colt used to being ridden? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the disciples prepare the colt to carry the Lord? +
*A:* put garments on it

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the multitude cast in the road before the Lord? +
*A:* branches

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they cry? +
*A:* blessed be the king

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell the Pharisees when they objected? +
*A:* the stones would cry out

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord do and say as He looked at Jerusalem? +
*A:* wept, "if thou hadst dreamed ... "

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He prophesy? +
*A:* destruction of Jerusalem

*Q:* (Older Teens): What did He do when He entered the temple? +
*A:* cleansed

*Q:* (Older Teens): What did He say about the temple? +
*A:* house of prayer
