
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What happened on the first Easter Sunday? Jesus rose from death

Primary: Who first learned of the Lord's resurrection? the women

Junior: What did the apostles think of the women's story? idle tales

Primary: What were the two disciples talking about later the same day on the way to Emmaus? all that had happened

Primary: Who came and walked with them? Jesus

Junior: Why did they not recognize Him? their eyes were "held"

Junior: What did He say when they told Him why they were sad? "ought not Christ ... ?"

Junior: What did He teach them? about Himself in the Scriptures

Primary: What happened as they were eating together? they recognized Him; He vanished

Primary: How did they say they felt when the Lord was talking to them? heart burned within

Primary: What happened later as they told their story to the apostles? Jesus appeared

Junior: What did the apostles think? He was a ghost

Junior: How did the Lord reassure them? let them touch Him; ate some food

Junior: What did He eat? fish, honey

Junior: What did He then teach them? inner meaning of Scriptures

Junior: What did He tell them to do? wait in Jerusalem for power

Junior: What promise did He make to them? I am with you always

Primary: How was the Lord finally separated from them? ascended into heaven

Primary: Where did the ascension take place? Bethany

Intermediate: What does "breaking bread" represent? sharing in love

Senior: How can we now know many of the things in the Word which the Lord revealed to the disciples? the heavenly doctrines
