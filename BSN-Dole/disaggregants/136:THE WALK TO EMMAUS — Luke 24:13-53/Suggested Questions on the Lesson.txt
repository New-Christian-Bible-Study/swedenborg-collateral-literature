
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened on the first Easter Sunday? +
*A:* Jesus rose from death

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who first learned of the Lord's resurrection? +
*A:* the women

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the apostles think of the women's story? +
*A:* idle tales

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were the two disciples talking about later the same day on the way to Emmaus? +
*A:* all that had happened

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who came and walked with them? +
*A:* Jesus

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did they not recognize Him? +
*A:* their eyes were "held"

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He say when they told Him why they were sad? +
*A:* "ought not Christ ... ?"

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He teach them? +
*A:* about Himself in the Scriptures

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened as they were eating together? +
*A:* they recognized Him; He vanished

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did they say they felt when the Lord was talking to them? +
*A:* heart burned within

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened later as they told their story to the apostles? +
*A:* Jesus appeared

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the apostles think? +
*A:* He was a ghost

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord reassure them? +
*A:* let them touch Him; ate some food

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He eat? +
*A:* fish, honey

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He then teach them? +
*A:* inner meaning of Scriptures

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell them to do? +
*A:* wait in Jerusalem for power

*Q:* (Older Children): What promise did He make to them? +
*A:* I am with you always

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was the Lord finally separated from them? +
*A:* ascended into heaven

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did the ascension take place? +
*A:* Bethany

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does "breaking bread" represent? +
*A:* sharing in love

*Q:* (Older Teens): How can we now know many of the things in the Word which the Lord revealed to the disciples? +
*A:* the heavenly doctrines
