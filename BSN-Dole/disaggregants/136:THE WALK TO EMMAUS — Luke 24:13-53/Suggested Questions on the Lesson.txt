
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What happened on the first Easter Sunday? Jesus rose from death

Younger Children: Who first learned of the Lord's resurrection? the women

Older Children: What did the apostles think of the women's story? idle tales

Younger Children: What were the two disciples talking about later the same day on the way to Emmaus? all that had happened

Younger Children: Who came and walked with them? Jesus

Older Children: Why did they not recognize Him? their eyes were "held"

Older Children: What did He say when they told Him why they were sad? "ought not Christ ... ?"

Older Children: What did He teach them? about Himself in the Scriptures

Younger Children: What happened as they were eating together? they recognized Him; He vanished

Younger Children: How did they say they felt when the Lord was talking to them? heart burned within

Younger Children: What happened later as they told their story to the apostles? Jesus appeared

Older Children: What did the apostles think? He was a ghost

Older Children: How did the Lord reassure them? let them touch Him; ate some food

Older Children: What did He eat? fish, honey

Older Children: What did He then teach them? inner meaning of Scriptures

Older Children: What did He tell them to do? wait in Jerusalem for power

Older Children: What promise did He make to them? I am with you always

Younger Children: How was the Lord finally separated from them? ascended into heaven

Younger Children: Where did the ascension take place? Bethany

Younger Teens: What does "breaking bread" represent? sharing in love

Older Teens: How can we now know many of the things in the Word which the Lord revealed to the disciples? the heavenly doctrines
