
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What kind of animal did the Lord ride into Jerusalem on Palm Sunday? a donkey

Younger Children: What did the people shout? Hosanna

Older Children: What did they think the Lord had come to do? overthrow Rome

Older Children: What happened when they found that He had not come to be an earthly king? rejected Him

Younger Children: On what day was He put to death? Friday

Older Children: What was done with His body when it was taken down from the cross? put in new tomb

Older Children: What two men performed this service? Joseph of Arimathea, Nicodemus

Younger Children: Who first came to the sepulcher on Sunday morning? Mary Magdalene

Younger Children: Why did she come? to anoint body

Younger Children: What did she find? stone rolled away

Older Children: What did Peter and the other disciple see in the tomb? linen cloths

Younger Children: What did Mary see there? two angels

Younger Children: When the Lord appeared to her, how did He make Himself known? spoke her name

Older Children: Why did He tell her not to touch Him? not yet ascended

Older Children: How did He appear to the disciples? through closed doors

Older Children: Which disciple was not present? Thomas

Older Children: What did Thomas say would be necessary to convince him that the Lord was alive? "Unless I see ... and touch ... "

Older Children: How did the Lord convince him? appeared again

Older Children: What did the Lord afterward say to Thomas? "Blessed ... not seen and believed"

Older Children: What does John say about things not recorded in the Gospel? too much to record

Younger Teens: What does burial represent? resurrection

Older Teens: What is represented by the stone which closed the sepulcher? false idea of God

Older Teens: What is represented by the linen cloths? ideas of disciples as to who the Lord was
