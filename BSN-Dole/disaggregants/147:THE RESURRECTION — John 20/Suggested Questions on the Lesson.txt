
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What kind of animal did the Lord ride into Jerusalem on Palm Sunday? a donkey

Primary: What did the people shout? Hosanna

Junior: What did they think the Lord had come to do? overthrow Rome

Junior: What happened when they found that He had not come to be an earthly king? rejected Him

Primary: On what day was He put to death? Friday

Junior: What was done with His body when it was taken down from the cross? put in new tomb

Junior: What two men performed this service? Joseph of Arimathea, Nicodemus

Primary: Who first came to the sepulcher on Sunday morning? Mary Magdalene

Primary: Why did she come? to anoint body

Primary: What did she find? stone rolled away

Junior: What did Peter and the other disciple see in the tomb? linen cloths

Primary: What did Mary see there? two angels

Primary: When the Lord appeared to her, how did He make Himself known? spoke her name

Junior: Why did He tell her not to touch Him? not yet ascended

Junior: How did He appear to the disciples? through closed doors

Junior: Which disciple was not present? Thomas

Junior: What did Thomas say would be necessary to convince him that the Lord was alive? "Unless I see ... and touch ... "

Junior: How did the Lord convince him? appeared again

Junior: What did the Lord afterward say to Thomas? "Blessed ... not seen and believed"

Junior: What does John say about things not recorded in the Gospel? too much to record

Intermediate: What does burial represent? resurrection

Senior: What is represented by the stone which closed the sepulcher? false idea of God

Senior: What is represented by the linen cloths? ideas of disciples as to who the Lord was
