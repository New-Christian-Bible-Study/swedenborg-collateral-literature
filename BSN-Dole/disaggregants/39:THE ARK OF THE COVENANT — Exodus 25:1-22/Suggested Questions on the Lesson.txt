
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord give all the people from the mountain top? +
*A:* ten commandments

*Q:* (Older Children): What else did He tell Moses? +
*A:* many minor laws

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was their place of worship to be? +
*A:* tabernacle (tent)

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the inmost room called? +
*A:* Holy of Holies

*Q:* (Younger Children): What piece of furniture was in it? +
*A:* ark of covenant

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the ark to be made of? +
*A:* acacia wood

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was on top of the ark? +
*A:* cover (mercy seat, propitiatory)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was inside of it? +
*A:* two stone tablets (commandments)

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord tell Moses He would use the ark? +
*A:* to speak to them

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the ark represent? +
*A:* our inner being, where the Lord can speak to us

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the "mercy seat" represent? +
*A:* our cleansing from evil

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does Swedenborg call the mercy seat? +
*A:* propitiatory

*Q:* (Older Teens): What are pictured by the cherubim? +
*A:* protection of divine providence

Younger Teens. Why does the ark represent heaven? heaven must be in our "hearts"
