
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What did the Lord give all the people from the mountain top? ten commandments

Older Children: What else did He tell Moses? many minor laws

Younger Children: What was their place of worship to be? tabernacle (tent)

Older Children: What was the inmost room called? Holy of Holies

Younger Children: What piece of furniture was in it? ark of covenant

Younger Children: What was the ark to be made of? acacia wood

Younger Children: What was on top of the ark? cover (mercy seat, propitiatory)

Younger Children: What was inside of it? two stone tablets (commandments)

Older Children: How did the Lord tell Moses He would use the ark? to speak to them

Younger Teens: What does the ark represent? our inner being, where the Lord can speak to us

Older Teens: What does the "mercy seat" represent? our cleansing from evil

Older Teens: What does Swedenborg call the mercy seat? propitiatory

Older Teens: What are pictured by the cherubim? protection of divine providence

Intermediate. Why does the ark represent heaven? heaven must be in our "hearts"
