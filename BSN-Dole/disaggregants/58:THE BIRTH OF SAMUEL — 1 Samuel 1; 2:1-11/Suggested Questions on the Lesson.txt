
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Intermediate: Why do we not study the book of Ruth? no inner sense

Primary: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Junior: Who were Samuel's parents? Elkanah and Hannah

Junior: Where was the tabernacle at this time? Shiloh

Primary: Who was the high priest? Eli

Primary: What promise did Hannah make? if she had a son, to give him to the Lord

Junior: How did she keep it? she brought Samuel to live with Eli

Primary: Where did Samuel grow up? at Shiloh

Intermediate: Why did the people get into so much trouble in the time of the Judges? they forgot the Lord

Senior: What does Samuel represent in us? the Lord's Word to us through our childhood remains


It is important that the children see the fallacy of thinking that we can lead and govern ourselves apart from regular worship of the Lord and obedience to Him. The sin of Eli's sons shows how even worship can be degraded by selfishness, self-satisfaction, and disobedience.
