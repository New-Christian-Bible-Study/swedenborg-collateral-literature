
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Teens: Why do we not study the book of Ruth? no inner sense

Younger Children: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Older Children: Who were Samuel's parents? Elkanah and Hannah

Older Children: Where was the tabernacle at this time? Shiloh

Younger Children: Who was the high priest? Eli

Younger Children: What promise did Hannah make? if she had a son, to give him to the Lord

Older Children: How did she keep it? she brought Samuel to live with Eli

Younger Children: Where did Samuel grow up? at Shiloh

Younger Teens: Why did the people get into so much trouble in the time of the Judges? they forgot the Lord

Older Teens: What does Samuel represent in us? the Lord's Word to us through our childhood remains


It is important that the children see the fallacy of thinking that we can lead and govern ourselves apart from regular worship of the Lord and obedience to Him. The sin of Eli's sons shows how even worship can be degraded by selfishness, self-satisfaction, and disobedience.
