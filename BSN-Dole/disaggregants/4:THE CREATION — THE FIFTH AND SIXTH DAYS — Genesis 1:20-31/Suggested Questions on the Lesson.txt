
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What book do we study in Sunday School? +
*A:* Bible

*Q:* (Older Children): Why is it called the Word of God? +
*A:* God's Word to us

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How do the first eleven chapters of Genesis differ from the rest? +
*A:* pure symbol

*Q:* (Older Children): Into what two parts is the Bible divided? +
*A:* Old Testament and New Testament

*Q:* (Older Children): What are the first five books called? +
*A:* Moses, Law, Torah, Pentateuch

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is the name of the first book? +
*A:* Genesis

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does Genesis mean? +
*A:* the beginning

*Q:* (Older Children): Can you repeat the first verse of the Bible? +
*A:* In the beginning ...

*Q:* (Younger Children): In how many days are the earth and everything on it said to have been created? +
*A:* six

*Q:* (Older Children): Does this mean days of twenty-four hours each? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): What appeared on the fourth day? +
*A:* sun, moon, stars

*Q:* (Younger Teens): To what do the sun and moon correspond? +
*A:* love to God, faith in Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were created on the fifth day? +
*A:* fish, birds

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were created first on the sixth day? +
*A:* animals

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the final creation? +
*A:* man

*Q:* (Older Children): How was man different from every other living creature? +
*A:* image of God

*Q:* (Older Children): What was he told to do? +
*A:* multiply, subdue earth

*Q:* (Older Children): Was just one created in the beginning? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened on the seventh day? +
*A:* God rested

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What kind of lessons does the Lord teach us in the Bible? +
*A:* about Himself and our relationship to Him

*Q:* (Younger Teens): To what do the fish, birds, and animals correspond? +
*A:* knowledges, thoughts, affections

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the command to "have dominion" mean? +
*A:* control
