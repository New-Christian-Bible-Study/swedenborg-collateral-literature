
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What book do we study in Sunday School? Bible

Older Children: Why is it called the Word of God? God's Word to us

Younger Teens: How do the first eleven chapters of Genesis differ from the rest? pure symbol

Older Children: Into what two parts is the Bible divided? Old Testament and New Testament

Older Children: What are the first five books called? Moses, Law, Torah, Pentateuch

Younger Children: What is the name of the first book? Genesis

Younger Children: What does Genesis mean? the beginning

Older Children: Can you repeat the first verse of the Bible? In the beginning ...

Younger Children: In how many days are the earth and everything on it said to have been created? six

Older Children: Does this mean days of twenty-four hours each? no

Older Children: What appeared on the fourth day? sun, moon, stars

Younger Teens: To what do the sun and moon correspond? love to God, faith in Him

Younger Children: What were created on the fifth day? fish, birds

Younger Children: What were created first on the sixth day? animals

Younger Children: What was the final creation? man

Older Children: How was man different from every other living creature? image of God

Older Children: What was he told to do? multiply, subdue earth

Older Children: Was just one created in the beginning? no

Older Children: What happened on the seventh day? God rested

Younger Teens: What kind of lessons does the Lord teach us in the Bible? about Himself and our relationship to Him

Younger Teens: To what do the fish, birds, and animals correspond? knowledges, thoughts, affections

Older Teens: What does the command to "have dominion" mean? control
