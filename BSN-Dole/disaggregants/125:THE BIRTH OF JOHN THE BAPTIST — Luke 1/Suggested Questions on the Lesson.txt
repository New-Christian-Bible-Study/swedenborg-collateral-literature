
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What is the last book in the Old Testament? Malachi

Older Children: What event did Malachi foretell? coming of the Lord

Older Children: Of what two messengers did he speak? (1) to prepare the way, (2) of the covenant

Older Children: What two kinds of people would the Lord find in the Holy Land when He came? (1) self-satisfied, (2) those who wanted to be good

Younger Children: How would his coming affect the wicked? burn like an oven

Younger Children: What would His coming be like to the good? healing

Older Children: Who was the first messenger to be? Elijah (John)

Younger Children: What angel is named in our lesson for today? Gabriel

Older Children: To whom did he come first? Zacharias

Younger Children: What message did he give to Zacharias? he would have a son

Older Children: What sign did he give him? unable to speak until son was born

Older Children: To what other person did Gabriel appear? Mary

Older Children: What was his message to Mary? she would have a son

Younger Teens: How do we know that John was the promised Elijah? the Lord said so

Older Children: What was John's message? Repent!

Younger Children: Why was he called John the Baptist? he baptized

Younger Teens: What does dumbness represent? spiritual ignorance

Older Teens: Why did John have to "prepare the way of the Lord"? only the humble and repentant can receive the Lord

Older Teens: What does baptism represent? our intention to cleanse our lives
