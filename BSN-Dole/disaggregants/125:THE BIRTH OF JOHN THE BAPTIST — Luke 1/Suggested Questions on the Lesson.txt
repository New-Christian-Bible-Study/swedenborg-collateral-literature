
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What is the last book in the Old Testament? Malachi

Junior: What event did Malachi foretell? coming of the Lord

Junior: Of what two messengers did he speak? (1) to prepare the way, (2) of the covenant

Junior: What two kinds of people would the Lord find in the Holy Land when He came? (1) self-satisfied, (2) those who wanted to be good

Primary: How would his coming affect the wicked? burn like an oven

Primary: What would His coming be like to the good? healing

Junior: Who was the first messenger to be? Elijah (John)

Primary: What angel is named in our lesson for today? Gabriel

Junior: To whom did he come first? Zacharias

Primary: What message did he give to Zacharias? he would have a son

Junior: What sign did he give him? unable to speak until son was born

Junior: To what other person did Gabriel appear? Mary

Junior: What was his message to Mary? she would have a son

Intermediate: How do we know that John was the promised Elijah? the Lord said so

Junior: What was John's message? Repent!

Primary: Why was he called John the Baptist? he baptized 

Intermediate: What does dumbness represent? spiritual ignorance

Senior: Why did John have to "prepare the way of the Lord"? only the humble and repentant can receive the Lord

Senior: What does baptism represent? our intention to cleanse our lives
