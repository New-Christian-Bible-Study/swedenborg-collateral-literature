
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What is the last book in the Old Testament? +
*A:* Malachi

*Q:* (Older Children): What event did Malachi foretell? +
*A:* coming of the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): Of what two messengers did he speak? +
*A:* (1) to prepare the way, (2) of the covenant

*Q:* (Older Children): What two kinds of people would the Lord find in the Holy Land when He came? +
*A:* (1) self-satisfied, (2) those who wanted to be good

*Q:* (Younger Children): How would his coming affect the wicked? +
*A:* burn like an oven

*Q:* (Younger Children): What would His coming be like to the good? +
*A:* healing

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was the first messenger to be? +
*A:* Elijah (John)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What angel is named in our lesson for today? +
*A:* Gabriel

*Q:* (Older Children): To whom did he come first? +
*A:* Zacharias

*Q:* (Younger Children): What message did he give to Zacharias? +
*A:* he would have a son

*Q:* (Older Children): What sign did he give him? +
*A:* unable to speak until son was born

*Q:* (Older Children): To what other person did Gabriel appear? +
*A:* Mary

*Q:* (Older Children): What was his message to Mary? +
*A:* she would have a son

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How do we know that John was the promised Elijah? +
*A:* the Lord said so

*Q:* (Older Children): What was John's message? +
*A:* Repent!

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why was he called John the Baptist? +
*A:* he baptized

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does dumbness represent? +
*A:* spiritual ignorance

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did John have to "prepare the way of the Lord"? +
*A:* only the humble and repentant can receive the Lord

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does baptism represent? +
*A:* our intention to cleanse our lives
