
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): When did the Lord give His disciples the teaching of our lesson for today? +
*A:* the night before the crucifixion

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does He call Himself in this chapter? +
*A:* the vine

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does He call His disciples? +
*A:* branches

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does He say they must do in order to bring forth fruit? +
*A:* abide in Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happens to those who do not abide in the Lord? +
*A:* cast out

*Q:* (Older Children): How do men abide in the Lord? +
*A:* obey Him

*Q:* (Older Children): Why does the Lord say He tells us these things? +
*A:* so we may share His joy

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does He say in His commandment? +
*A:* love one another

*Q:* (Older Children): Does He promise His disciples an easy life in this world? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): What does He promise them? +
*A:* to be hated and persecuted by the world

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the vine represent? +
*A:* divine truth

*Q:* (Older Teens): What do the grapes represent? +
*A:* spiritual goodness
