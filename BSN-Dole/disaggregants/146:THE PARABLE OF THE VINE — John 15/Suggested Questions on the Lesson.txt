
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: When did the Lord give His disciples the teaching of our lesson for today? the night before the crucifixion

Younger Children: What does He call Himself in this chapter? the vine

Younger Children: What does He call His disciples? branches

Younger Children: What does He say they must do in order to bring forth fruit? abide in Him

Younger Children: What happens to those who do not abide in the Lord? cast out

Older Children: How do men abide in the Lord? obey Him

Older Children: Why does the Lord say He tells us these things? so we may share His joy

Younger Children: What does He say in His commandment? love one another

Older Children: Does He promise His disciples an easy life in this world? no

Older Children: What does He promise them? to be hated and persecuted by the world

Younger Teens: What does the vine represent? divine truth

Older Teens: What do the grapes represent? spiritual goodness
