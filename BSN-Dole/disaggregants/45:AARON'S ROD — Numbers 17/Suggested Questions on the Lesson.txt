
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What set of laws did the Lord first give the children of Israel at Sinai? +
*A:* ten commandments

*Q:* (Older Children): What other instructions did He give them there? +
*A:* many tribal laws

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the people at one time think that Moses was gone for good? +
*A:* gone forty days

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they demand of Aaron? +
*A:* "make us gods"

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Aaron make for them? +
*A:* calf

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he use to make it? +
*A:* gold earrings

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Moses do when he returned with the first tables of the law? +
*A:* broke them

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he do with the golden calf? +
*A:* destroyed it

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whom did the Lord appoint to be high priest of Israel? +
*A:* Aaron

*Q:* (Older Children): Why were the people kept wandering in the wilderness for forty years? +
*A:* lack of faith

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whom did they blame for their troubles? +
*A:* Moses and Aaron

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to the men who rebelled against Moses and Aaron? +
*A:* destroyed

*Q:* (Younger Children): In this lesson, what did the Lord tell Moses to have the leader of each tribe bring? +
*A:* rod

*Q:* (Younger Children): How were the twelve rods distinguished from one another? +
*A:* names put on them

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whose name was written on the rod of the tribe of Levi? +
*A:* Aaron's

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did Moses put the rods? +
*A:* in front of ark

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they find the next morning? +
*A:* Aaron's rod bore almonds

*Q:* (Older Children): What did this show? +
*A:* Aaron to remain priest

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the Lord tell Moses to keep Aaron's rod? +
*A:* near ark

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Aaron represent? +
*A:* love of obeying the Lord

*Q:* (Older Teens): What spiritual quality do almonds represent? +
*A:* lasting goodness based on perception of spiritual truth
