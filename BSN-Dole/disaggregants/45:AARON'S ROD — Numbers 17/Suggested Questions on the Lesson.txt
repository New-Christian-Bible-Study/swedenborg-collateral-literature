
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What set of laws did the Lord first give the children of Israel at Sinai? ten commandments

Junior: What other instructions did He give them there? many tribal laws

Junior: Why did the people at one time think that Moses was gone for good? gone forty days

Junior: What did they demand of Aaron? "make us gods"

Primary: What did Aaron make for them? calf

Primary: What did he use to make it? gold earrings

Primary: What did Moses do when he returned with the first tables of the law? broke them

Junior: What did he do with the golden calf? destroyed it

Primary: Whom did the Lord appoint to be high priest of Israel? Aaron

Junior: Why were the people kept wandering in the wilderness for forty years? lack of faith

Primary: Whom did they blame for their troubles? Moses and Aaron

Junior: What happened to the men who rebelled against Moses and Aaron? destroyed

Primary: In this lesson, what did the Lord tell Moses to have the leader of each tribe bring? rod

Primary: How were the twelve rods distinguished from one another? names put on them

Primary: Whose name was written on the rod of the tribe of Levi? Aaron's

Primary: Where did Moses put the rods? in front of ark

Primary: What did they find the next morning? Aaron's rod bore almonds

Junior: What did this show? Aaron to remain priest

Junior: Where did the Lord tell Moses to keep Aaron's rod? near ark

Intermediate: What does Aaron represent? love of obeying the Lord

Senior: What spiritual quality do almonds represent? lasting goodness based on perception of spiritual truth
