
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What is a psalm? a song

Younger Children: How did the Jews use the book of Psalms? hymnal

Younger Children: Through whom were many of the Psalms written? David

Older Children: What did David say about the Psalms? inspired by God

Younger Teens: What did the Lord say about them? prophesied about Him

Younger Children: Which one is the longest of the Psalms? 119th

Older Children: What is peculiar about its structure? "alphabetical"

Older Children: What is its general subject? the Word

Older Children: How is this subject impressed upon us? in every verse

Younger Teens: Why should we study the Word? to learn how to live

Older Children: Why should we obey its precepts? to find eternal happiness

Older Teens: Why are some of the Psalms written in alphabetical order? to show they contain a complete summary of a subject

Older Teens: Where in the Word does its power rest? letter or literal sense
