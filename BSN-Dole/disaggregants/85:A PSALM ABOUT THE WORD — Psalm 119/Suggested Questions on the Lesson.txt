
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What is a psalm? a song

Primary: How did the Jews use the book of Psalms? hymnal

Primary: Through whom were many of the Psalms written? David

Junior: What did David say about the Psalms? inspired by God

Intermediate: What did the Lord say about them? prophesied about Him

Primary: Which one is the longest of the Psalms? 119th

Junior: What is peculiar about its structure? "alphabetical"

Junior: What is its general subject? the Word

Junior: How is this subject impressed upon us? in every verse

Intermediate: Why should we study the Word? to learn how to live

Junior: Why should we obey its precepts? to find eternal happiness

Senior: Why are some of the Psalms written in alphabetical order? to show they contain a complete summary of a subject

Senior: Where in the Word does its power rest? letter or literal sense
