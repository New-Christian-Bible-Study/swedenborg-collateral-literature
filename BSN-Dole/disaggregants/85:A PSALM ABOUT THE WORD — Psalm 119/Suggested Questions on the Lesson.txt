
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is a psalm? +
*A:* a song

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the Jews use the book of Psalms? +
*A:* hymnal

*Q:* (Younger Children): Through whom were many of the Psalms written? +
*A:* David

*Q:* (Older Children): What did David say about the Psalms? +
*A:* inspired by God

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord say about them? +
*A:* prophesied about Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which one is the longest of the Psalms? +
*A:* 119th

*Q:* (Older Children): What is peculiar about its structure? +
*A:* "alphabetical"

*Q:* (Older Children): What is its general subject? +
*A:* the Word

*Q:* (Older Children): How is this subject impressed upon us? +
*A:* in every verse

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why should we study the Word? +
*A:* to learn how to live

*Q:* (Older Children): Why should we obey its precepts? +
*A:* to find eternal happiness

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why are some of the Psalms written in alphabetical order? +
*A:* to show they contain a complete summary of a subject

*Q:* (Older Teens): Where in the Word does its power rest? +
*A:* letter or literal sense
