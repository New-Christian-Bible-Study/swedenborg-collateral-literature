
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Where was the Lord born? Bethlehem

Primary: Where did He grow up? Nazareth

Primary: Who was sent before Him to prepare His way? John the Baptist

Junior: How long was His public ministry? about three years

Primary: What is today called? Palm Sunday

Junior: What did the Lord do on that day? rode into Jerusalem as a king

Primary: What animal did He ride into Jerusalem on? a donkey 

Junior: Where did His disciples find the donkey? tied outside a door

Primary: Why do we call this Palm Sunday? people spread palm branches (John 12:13)

Junior: Where did the Lord spend the night? Bethany

Intermediate: What did He do in the temple? drove out money—changers and sellers of doves

Junior: What happened to the fig tree which had no fruit on it? withered

Junior: What did the Lord tell His disciples they could do if they had faith? move mountains

Junior: To what part of the Lord's Prayer does He refer in this chapter? forgive debts

Junior: What question did the priests and scribes ask? "By what authority ... ?"

Intermediate: How did the Lord answer them? asked source of John's baptism

Junior: What is this week called? Holy Week

Junior: What happened on Thursday of the week? Last Supper, arrest

Junior: What happened on Friday? crucifixion

Intermediate: What is represented by the Lord's riding on a donkey on which never man sat? His mastery of His natural reason

Senior: What does the incident of the fig tree mean? if there are no good works there is no spiritual life

Primary: What will next Sunday be? Easter

== THE HOLY SUPPER — Mark 14

As an introduction to this lesson review briefly the Palm Sunday account, calling attention to the fact that the Lord came to Jerusalem to die and told His disciples so, but that they did not understand or remember what He said. Note that during the last week of His earthly life He spent the nights at Bethany.
