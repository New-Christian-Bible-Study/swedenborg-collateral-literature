
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was the Lord born? +
*A:* Bethlehem

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did He grow up? +
*A:* Nazareth

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was sent before Him to prepare His way? +
*A:* John the Baptist

*Q:* (Older Children): How long was His public ministry? +
*A:* about three years

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is today called? +
*A:* Palm Sunday

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord do on that day? +
*A:* rode into Jerusalem as a king

*Q:* (Younger Children): What animal did He ride into Jerusalem on? +
*A:* a donkey

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did His disciples find the donkey? +
*A:* tied outside a door

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why do we call this Palm Sunday? +
*A:* people spread palm branches (John 12:13)

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the Lord spend the night? +
*A:* Bethany

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to the fig tree which had no fruit on it? +
*A:* withered

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell His disciples they could do if they had faith? +
*A:* move mountains

*Q:* (Older Children): To what part of the Lord's Prayer does He refer in this chapter? +
*A:* forgive debts

*Q:* (Older Children): What question did the priests and scribes ask? +
*A:* "By what authority ... ?"

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How did the Lord answer them? +
*A:* asked source of John's baptism

*Q:* (Older Children): What is this week called? +
*A:* Holy Week

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened on Thursday of the week? +
*A:* Last Supper, arrest

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened on Friday? +
*A:* crucifixion

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is represented by the Lord's riding on a donkey on which never man sat? +
*A:* His mastery of His natural reason

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the incident of the fig tree mean? +
*A:* if there are no good works there is no spiritual life

*Q:* (Younger Children): What will next Sunday be? +
*A:* Easter
