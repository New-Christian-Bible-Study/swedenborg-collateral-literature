
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Where was the Lord born? Bethlehem

Younger Children: Where did He grow up? Nazareth

Younger Children: Who was sent before Him to prepare His way? John the Baptist

Older Children: How long was His public ministry? about three years

Younger Children: What is today called? Palm Sunday

Older Children: What did the Lord do on that day? rode into Jerusalem as a king

Younger Children: What animal did He ride into Jerusalem on? a donkey

Older Children: Where did His disciples find the donkey? tied outside a door

Younger Children: Why do we call this Palm Sunday? people spread palm branches (John 12:13)

Older Children: Where did the Lord spend the night? Bethany

Older Children: What happened to the fig tree which had no fruit on it? withered

Older Children: What did the Lord tell His disciples they could do if they had faith? move mountains

Older Children: To what part of the Lord's Prayer does He refer in this chapter? forgive debts

Older Children: What question did the priests and scribes ask? "By what authority ... ?"

Younger Teens: How did the Lord answer them? asked source of John's baptism

Older Children: What is this week called? Holy Week

Older Children: What happened on Thursday of the week? Last Supper, arrest

Older Children: What happened on Friday? crucifixion

Younger Teens: What is represented by the Lord's riding on a donkey on which never man sat? His mastery of His natural reason

Older Teens: What does the incident of the fig tree mean? if there are no good works there is no spiritual life

Younger Children: What will next Sunday be? Easter
