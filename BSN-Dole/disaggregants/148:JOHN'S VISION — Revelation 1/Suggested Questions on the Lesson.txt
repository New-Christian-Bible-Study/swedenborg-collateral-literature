
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Of what two Johns do we read in the Gospels? John the Baptist, apostle John

Junior: Through which one was the Gospel of John written? the apostle

Junior: What happened to Jerusalem after the Lord's death? it was destroyed

Junior: Where were the first Christian churches started? Asia Minor

Junior: What other books in the Bible were written through John? three letters, Revelation

Junior: Where was John when the Revelation was given him? Isle of Patmos

Primary: What did John hear first? great voice like a trumpet

Primary: What did he first see when he turned? seven gold lampstands

Primary: Who stood in the midst of the seven golden lampstands? the risen Lord

Primary: How did the Lord appear? dressed in long robe ...

Primary: What did He have in His right hand? seven stars

Junior: What did the Lord say about Himself? I am alpha and omega

Junior: What did He say the seven lampstands and the seven stars were? churches/angels

Junior: What did He tell John to do? write down the vision

Intermediate: Why are there so many mentions of light in this vision? to show power of divine truth

Intermediate: What does the golden girdle or belt represent? divine love binding all together

Senior: What does this vision teach us? how we should "picture," i.e., think of, the Lord
