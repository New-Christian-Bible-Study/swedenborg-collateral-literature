
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Of what two Johns do we read in the Gospels? +
*A:* John the Baptist, apostle John

*Q:* (Older Children): Through which one was the Gospel of John written? +
*A:* the apostle

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to Jerusalem after the Lord's death? +
*A:* it was destroyed

*Q:* (Older Children): Where were the first Christian churches started? +
*A:* Asia Minor

*Q:* (Older Children): What other books in the Bible were written through John? +
*A:* three letters, Revelation

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was John when the Revelation was given him? +
*A:* Isle of Patmos

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did John hear first? +
*A:* great voice like a trumpet

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he first see when he turned? +
*A:* seven gold lampstands

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who stood in the midst of the seven golden lampstands? +
*A:* the risen Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the Lord appear? +
*A:* dressed in long robe ...

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did He have in His right hand? +
*A:* seven stars

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord say about Himself? +
*A:* I am alpha and omega

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He say the seven lampstands and the seven stars were? +
*A:* churches/angels

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell John to do? +
*A:* write down the vision

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why are there so many mentions of light in this vision? +
*A:* to show power of divine truth

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the golden girdle or belt represent? +
*A:* divine love binding all together

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does this vision teach us? +
*A:* how we should "picture," i.e., think of, the Lord
