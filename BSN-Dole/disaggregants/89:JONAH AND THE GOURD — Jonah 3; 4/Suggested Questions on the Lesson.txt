
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Is the book of Jonah one of the Major or one of the Minor Prophets? Minor

Junior: When and where did Jonah live? shortly after Elisha, in Israel

Junior: Why did he try to run away from the Lord? too proud to warn Nineveh 

Primary: What happened to him? thrown overboard, swallowed by great fish

Junior: Where was Nineveh? Assyria

Junior: When Jonah went to Nineveh, what did he tell the people? forty days till doom

Junior: Why did his prophecy not come true? the people repented

Primary: Was Jonah pleased? no

Primary: Where did he go? edge of town

Primary: What grew up to give him shade? "gourd"

Primary: What came to destroy the gourd? worm

Primary: Was Jonah angry? yes

Junior: What did the Lord tell him? he should have as great pity for people 

Intermediate: What does the gourd represent? arrogant pride, sense of superiority 

Senior: What does the worm represent? false ideas associated with pride

Senior: Do people today ever behave as Jonah did? Explain. yes, every time we feel our ways and our ideas must be better than those of anyone who is "different"
