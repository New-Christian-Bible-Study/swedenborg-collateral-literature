
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did the Lord place the first people? +
*A:* Garden of Eden

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were they to do there? +
*A:* dress it and keep it

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was the Garden of Eden watered? +
*A:* four rivers

*Q:* (Older Children): What tree was in the middle of the garden? +
*A:* tree of life

*Q:* (Older Children): What other tree is mentioned? +
*A:* the tree of the knowledge of good and evil

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were the people told not to do? +
*A:* eat of tree of knowledge of good and evil

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord take out of Adam while he slept? +
*A:* a rib

*Q:* (Older Children): Into what did He make it? +
*A:* a woman

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the first people disobey the Lord? +
*A:* ate forbidden fruit

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was their punishment? +
*A:* driven from the garden

*Q:* (Older Children): Did they repent? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord finally decide to do? +
*A:* destroy man

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did He save Noah and his family? +
*A:* they were good

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did He tell Noah to build? +
*A:* ark

*Q:* (Older Children): Can you describe the ark? +
*A:* gopher wood, coated with pitch, three stories, rooms, a window, a door

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who went into the ark with Noah? +
*A:* wife, sons, their wives

*Q:* (Older Children): What were the names of Noah's three sons? +
*A:* Shem, Ham, Japheth

*Q:* (Younger Children): What else did Noah take into the ark? +
*A:* pairs of all creatures

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is meant by eating of the tree of knowledge of good and evil? +
*A:* looking to self for truth

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by the flood? +
*A:* truth falsified and made destructive

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is the symbolic meaning of the ark? +
*A:* good character formed by obeying the Lord
