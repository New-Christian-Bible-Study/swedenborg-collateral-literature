
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Where did the Lord place the first people? Garden of Eden

Younger Children: What were they to do there? dress it and keep it

Younger Children: How was the Garden of Eden watered? four rivers

Older Children: What tree was in the middle of the garden? tree of life

Older Children: What other tree is mentioned? the tree of the knowledge of good and evil

Younger Children: What were the people told not to do? eat of tree of knowledge of good and evil

Older Children: What did the Lord take out of Adam while he slept? a rib

Older Children: Into what did He make it? a woman

Younger Children: How did the first people disobey the Lord? ate forbidden fruit

Younger Children: What was their punishment? driven from the garden

Older Children: Did they repent? no

Younger Children: What did the Lord finally decide to do? destroy man

Older Children: Why did He save Noah and his family? they were good

Younger Children: What did He tell Noah to build? ark

Older Children: Can you describe the ark? gopher wood, coated with pitch, three stories, rooms, a window, a door

Younger Children: Who went into the ark with Noah? wife, sons, their wives

Older Children: What were the names of Noah's three sons? Shem, Ham, Japheth

Younger Children: What else did Noah take into the ark? pairs of all creatures

Older Teens: What is meant by eating of the tree of knowledge of good and evil? looking to self for truth

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the flood? truth falsified and made destructive

Older Teens: What is the symbolic meaning of the ark? good character formed by obeying the Lord
