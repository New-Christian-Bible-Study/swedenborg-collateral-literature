
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Where did the Lord place the first people? Garden of Eden

Primary: What were they to do there? dress it and keep it

Primary: How was the Garden of Eden watered? four rivers

Junior: What tree was in the middle of the garden? tree of life

Junior: What other tree is mentioned? the tree of the knowledge of good and evil

Primary: What were the people told not to do? eat of tree of knowledge of good and evil

Junior: What did the Lord take out of Adam while he slept? a rib

Junior: Into what did He make it? a woman

Primary: How did the first people disobey the Lord? ate forbidden fruit

Primary: What was their punishment? driven from the garden

Junior: Did they repent? no

Primary: What did the Lord finally decide to do? destroy man

Junior: Why did He save Noah and his family? they were good

Primary: What did He tell Noah to build? ark

Junior: Can you describe the ark? gopher wood, coated with pitch, three stories, rooms, a window, a door

Primary: Who went into the ark with Noah? wife, sons, their wives

Junior: What were the names of Noah's three sons? Shem, Ham, Japheth

Primary: What else did Noah take into the ark? pairs of all creatures

Senior: What is meant by eating of the tree of knowledge of good and evil? looking to self for truth

Intermediate: What is pictured by the flood? truth falsified and made destructive

Senior: What is the symbolic meaning of the ark? good character formed by obeying the Lord
