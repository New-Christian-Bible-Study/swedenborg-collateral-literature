
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How did the Israelites cross the Red Sea? miraculous parting

Older Children: How were they led in their journey? pillar of cloud/fire

Older Children: To what place have they come in our lesson for today? Rephidim

Younger Children: What are they complaining about? no water

Younger Children: What does the Lord tell Moses to do? strike rock

Younger Children: What happened when Moses struck the rock? water came out

Older Children: After this what enemy attacked Israel? Amalek

Younger Children: What leader was appointed for the battle? Joshua

Younger Children: What did Moses do? went up on hill

Older Children: Who went up on the hill with him? Aaron and Hur

Younger Children: How was Moses able to control the course of the battle? holding hands up

Younger Children: How was he helped when he grew tired? Aaron, Hur, rock

Older Children: What did he build as a memorial of the victory? altar

Younger Teens: What is represented by getting water out of the rock? making effort to use "dry" truths

Younger Teens: What is pictured by Amalek? discouragement, based on hereditary self will

Older Teens: What is pictured by the Israelites' being victorious as long as Moses held up his hands? lifting thoughts to God, remembering His past help

Older Teens: What do (1) Aaron, and (2) Hur, represent in this story? (1) doctrines of church (2) thoughts learned in Sunday school, church, from parents, etc.
