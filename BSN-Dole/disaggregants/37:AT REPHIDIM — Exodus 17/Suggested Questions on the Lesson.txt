
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: How did the Israelites cross the Red Sea? miraculous parting

Junior: How were they led in their journey? pillar of cloud/fire

Junior: To what place have they come in our lesson for today? Rephidim

Primary: What are they complaining about? no water

Primary: What does the Lord tell Moses to do? strike rock

Primary: What happened when Moses struck the rock? water came out

Junior: After this what enemy attacked Israel? Amalek

Primary: What leader was appointed for the battle? Joshua

Primary: What did Moses do? went up on hill

Junior: Who went up on the hill with him? Aaron and Hur

Primary: How was Moses able to control the course of the battle? holding hands up

Primary: How was he helped when he grew tired? Aaron, Hur, rock

Junior: What did he build as a memorial of the victory? altar

Intermediate: What is represented by getting water out of the rock? making effort to use "dry" truths

Intermediate: What is pictured by Amalek? discouragement, based on hereditary self will

Senior: What is pictured by the Israelites' being victorious as long as Moses held up his hands? lifting thoughts to God, remembering His past help

Senior: What do (1) Aaron, and (2) Hur, represent in this story? (1) doctrines of church (2) thoughts learned in Sunday school, church, from parents, etc.
