
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Israelites cross the Red Sea? +
*A:* miraculous parting

*Q:* (Older Children): How were they led in their journey? +
*A:* pillar of cloud/fire

*Q:* (Older Children): To what place have they come in our lesson for today? +
*A:* Rephidim

*Q:* (Younger Children): What are they complaining about? +
*A:* no water

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does the Lord tell Moses to do? +
*A:* strike rock

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened when Moses struck the rock? +
*A:* water came out

*Q:* (Older Children): After this what enemy attacked Israel? +
*A:* Amalek

*Q:* (Younger Children): What leader was appointed for the battle? +
*A:* Joshua

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Moses do? +
*A:* went up on hill

*Q:* (Older Children): Who went up on the hill with him? +
*A:* Aaron and Hur

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was Moses able to control the course of the battle? +
*A:* holding hands up

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was he helped when he grew tired? +
*A:* Aaron, Hur, rock

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he build as a memorial of the victory? +
*A:* altar

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is represented by getting water out of the rock? +
*A:* making effort to use "dry" truths

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by Amalek? +
*A:* discouragement, based on hereditary self will

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by the Israelites' being victorious as long as Moses held up his hands? +
*A:* lifting thoughts to God, remembering His past help

*Q:* (Older Teens): What do (1) Aaron, and (2) Hur, represent in this story? +
*A:* (1) doctrines of church (2) thoughts learned in Sunday school, church, from parents, etc.
