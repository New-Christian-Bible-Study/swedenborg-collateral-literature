
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Who was Daniel? a young prince who became a prophet

Older Children: Why was he first brought to Babylon? to be educated

Younger Children: What made him a great man? his faith in God

Older Children: What other nation finally conquered Babylon? Persia

Younger Children: Who was its king? Darius

Younger Children: What office did he give Daniel? ruler of land

Older Children: Who plotted against Daniel? other political leaders

Older Children: What law did they persuade Darius to sign? no prayer for thirty days

Younger Children: What did Daniel do? prayed as usual

Younger Children: Did the king want to lose Daniel? no

Older Children: Why could he not change the law? unchangeable

Younger Children: How long was Daniel shut up in the lions' den? all night

Younger Children: How was he saved? angel

Younger Children: What happened to his enemies? thrown to lions

Older Children: What new law did Darius make? everyone worship Daniel's God

Younger Teens: What do all the prophets represent? the Word of God

Older Teens: What does a lion represent in a good and in a bad sense? power of truth, power of falsity
