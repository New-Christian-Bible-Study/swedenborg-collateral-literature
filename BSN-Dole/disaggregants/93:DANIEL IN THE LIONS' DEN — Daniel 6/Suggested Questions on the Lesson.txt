
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Who was Daniel? a young prince who became a prophet

Junior: Why was he first brought to Babylon? to be educated

Primary: What made him a great man? his faith in God

Junior: What other nation finally conquered Babylon? Persia

Primary: Who was its king? Darius

Primary: What office did he give Daniel? ruler of land

Junior: Who plotted against Daniel? other political leaders

Junior: What law did they persuade Darius to sign? no prayer for thirty days

Primary: What did Daniel do? prayed as usual

Primary: Did the king want to lose Daniel? no

Junior: Why could he not change the law? unchangeable

Primary: How long was Daniel shut up in the lions' den? all night

Primary: How was he saved? angel

Primary: What happened to his enemies? thrown to lions

Junior: What new law did Darius make? everyone worship Daniel's God

Intermediate: What do all the prophets represent? the Word of God

Senior: What does a lion represent in a good and in a bad sense? power of truth, power of falsity
