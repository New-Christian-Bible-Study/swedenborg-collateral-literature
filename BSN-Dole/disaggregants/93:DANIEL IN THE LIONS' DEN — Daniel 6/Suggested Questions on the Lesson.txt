
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was Daniel? +
*A:* a young prince who became a prophet

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was he first brought to Babylon? +
*A:* to be educated

*Q:* (Younger Children): What made him a great man? +
*A:* his faith in God

*Q:* (Older Children): What other nation finally conquered Babylon? +
*A:* Persia

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was its king? +
*A:* Darius

*Q:* (Younger Children): What office did he give Daniel? +
*A:* ruler of land

*Q:* (Older Children): Who plotted against Daniel? +
*A:* other political leaders

*Q:* (Older Children): What law did they persuade Darius to sign? +
*A:* no prayer for thirty days

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Daniel do? +
*A:* prayed as usual

*Q:* (Younger Children): Did the king want to lose Daniel? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): Why could he not change the law? +
*A:* unchangeable

*Q:* (Younger Children): How long was Daniel shut up in the lions' den? +
*A:* all night

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was he saved? +
*A:* angel

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to his enemies? +
*A:* thrown to lions

*Q:* (Older Children): What new law did Darius make? +
*A:* everyone worship Daniel's God

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do all the prophets represent? +
*A:* the Word of God

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does a lion represent in a good and in a bad sense? +
*A:* power of truth, power of falsity
