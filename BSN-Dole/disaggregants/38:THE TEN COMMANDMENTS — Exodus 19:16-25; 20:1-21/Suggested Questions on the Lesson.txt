
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Through what kind of country did the Israelites have to travel to reach the Holy Land? wilderness or desert

Older Children: What things did they miss which they had had in Egypt? fresh food, water

Older Children: How did the Lord take care of them? manna, quails, water from rock

Younger Children: To what mountain did they come in the third month? Sinai

Older Children: Were they allowed to go up on the mountain? no

Older Children: On the third morning what happened which made the people tremble? thunder, trumpets

Younger Children: Whose voice did they hear? God's

Younger Children: What did the Lord first say about Himself? "1 am the Lord thy God"

Younger Children: Then what did He give the people? the ten commandments

Older Children: Into what two groups can the commandments be divided? 1-5,6-10

Older Children: What does the first group teach? duties to God

Older Children: What does the second group teach? duties to man

Younger Teens: Had these commandments been known before? yes

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord speak them from Sinai? to show they were divine laws
