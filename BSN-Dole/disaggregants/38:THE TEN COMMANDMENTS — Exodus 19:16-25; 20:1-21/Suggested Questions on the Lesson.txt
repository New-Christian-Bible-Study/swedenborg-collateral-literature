
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Through what kind of country did the Israelites have to travel to reach the Holy Land? wilderness or desert

Junior: What things did they miss which they had had in Egypt? fresh food, water

Junior: How did the Lord take care of them? manna, quails, water from rock

Primary: To what mountain did they come in the third month? Sinai

Junior: Were they allowed to go up on the mountain? no

Junior: On the third morning what happened which made the people tremble? thunder, trumpets

Primary: Whose voice did they hear? God's

Primary: What did the Lord first say about Himself? "1 am the Lord thy God"

Primary: Then what did He give the people? the ten commandments

Junior: Into what two groups can the commandments be divided? 1-5,6-10

Junior: What does the first group teach? duties to God

Junior: What does the second group teach? duties to man

Intermediate: Had these commandments been known before? yes

Intermediate: Why did the Lord speak them from Sinai? to show they were divine laws
