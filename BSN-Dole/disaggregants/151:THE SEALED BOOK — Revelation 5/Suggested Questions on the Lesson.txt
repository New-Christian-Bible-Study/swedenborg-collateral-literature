
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did John see when a door was opened in heaven? +
*A:* a throne

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who sat on the throne? +
*A:* the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): How many elders were there? +
*A:* twenty-four

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many creatures were there? +
*A:* four

*Q:* (Older Children): What faces did they have? +
*A:* lion, calf, man, eagle

*Q:* (Older Children): What is another name for these creatures? +
*A:* cherubim

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they say when they worshiped the Lord? +
*A:* Holy, holy, holy

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our lesson for today what did the Lord have in His right hand? +
*A:* scroll or book

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did John weep? +
*A:* no one worthy to open seals

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who could open the book? +
*A:* Lion of Judah

*Q:* (Older Children): Can you describe the Lamb that John saw? +
*A:* as though slain, seven horns, seven eyes

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the elders and the beasts have in their hands? +
*A:* lamps, incense

*Q:* (Older Children): How many angels were singing? +
*A:* myriads

*Q:* (Older Children): What can you tell about the new song they sang? +
*A:* "Worthy is the Lamb ... "

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the four creatures say after the song? +
*A:* Amen

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does Amen mean? +
*A:* So be it

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is John's vision in the rest of this book about? +
*A:* judgment of all

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the New Church believe about the opening of the book? +
*A:* that the Lord has opened the book, making inner meaning available
