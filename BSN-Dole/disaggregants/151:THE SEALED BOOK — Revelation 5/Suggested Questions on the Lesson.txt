
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What did John see when a door was opened in heaven? a throne

Primary: Who sat on the throne? the Lord

Junior: How many elders were there? twenty-four

Primary: How many creatures were there? four

Junior: What faces did they have? lion, calf, man, eagle

Junior: What is another name for these creatures? cherubim

Junior: What did they say when they worshiped the Lord? Holy, holy, holy

Primary: In our lesson for today what did the Lord have in His right hand? scroll or book

Primary: Why did John weep? no one worthy to open seals

Primary: Who could open the book? Lion of Judah

Junior: Can you describe the Lamb that John saw? as though slain, seven horns, seven eyes

Junior: What did the elders and the beasts have in their hands? lamps, incense

Junior: How many angels were singing? myriads

Junior: What can you tell about the new song they sang? "Worthy is the Lamb ... "

Primary: What did the four creatures say after the song? Amen

Primary: What does Amen mean? So be it

Intermediate: What is John's vision in the rest of this book about? judgment of all

Senior: What does the New Church believe about the opening of the book? that the Lord has opened the book, making inner meaning available
