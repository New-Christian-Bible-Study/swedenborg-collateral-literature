
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What did John see when a door was opened in heaven? a throne

Younger Children: Who sat on the throne? the Lord

Older Children: How many elders were there? twenty-four

Younger Children: How many creatures were there? four

Older Children: What faces did they have? lion, calf, man, eagle

Older Children: What is another name for these creatures? cherubim

Older Children: What did they say when they worshiped the Lord? Holy, holy, holy

Younger Children: In our lesson for today what did the Lord have in His right hand? scroll or book

Younger Children: Why did John weep? no one worthy to open seals

Younger Children: Who could open the book? Lion of Judah

Older Children: Can you describe the Lamb that John saw? as though slain, seven horns, seven eyes

Older Children: What did the elders and the beasts have in their hands? lamps, incense

Older Children: How many angels were singing? myriads

Older Children: What can you tell about the new song they sang? "Worthy is the Lamb ... "

Younger Children: What did the four creatures say after the song? Amen

Younger Children: What does Amen mean? So be it

Younger Teens: What is John's vision in the rest of this book about? judgment of all

Older Teens: What does the New Church believe about the opening of the book? that the Lord has opened the book, making inner meaning available
