
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What happened after Joshua and the elders died? people forgot the Lord

Older Children: How did the Lord help the people whenever they turned to Him again? raised Judges

Younger Children: Which Judge is our lesson about today? Jephthah

Younger Children: Where was his home? across the Jordan

Younger Children: What enemy was attacking Israel? Ammon

Older Children: What did Jephthah vow? to sacrifice first who greeted him

Younger Children: What was the first thing that came out of his house when he returned? daughter

Younger Children: Did his daughter try to make him break his vow? no

Older Children: What favor did she ask? two months' time

Older Children: To what custom did this lead? annual four-day lament

Younger Teens: What do the Judges represent? particular truths to meet needs

Younger Teens: What does the land of Gilead represent? enjoyment of world

Older Teens: What is pictured by the sacrifice of Jephthah's daughter? giving up affection for thinking ourselves to be good
