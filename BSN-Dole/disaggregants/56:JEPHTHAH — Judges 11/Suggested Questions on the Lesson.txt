
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What happened after Joshua and the elders died? people forgot the Lord

Junior: How did the Lord help the people whenever they turned to Him again? raised Judges

Primary: Which Judge is our lesson about today? Jephthah

Primary: Where was his home? across the Jordan

Primary: What enemy was attacking Israel? Ammon

Junior: What did Jephthah vow? to sacrifice first who greeted him

Primary: What was the first thing that came out of his house when he returned? daughter

Primary: Did his daughter try to make him break his vow? no

Junior: What favor did she ask? two months' time

Junior: To what custom did this lead? annual four-day lament

Intermediate: What do the Judges represent? particular truths to meet needs 

Intermediate: What does the land of Gilead represent? enjoyment of world

Senior: What is pictured by the sacrifice of Jephthah's daughter? giving up affection for thinking ourselves to be good
