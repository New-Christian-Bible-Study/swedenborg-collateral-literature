
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What did Solomon build? temple

Junior: What did Solomon later do which was wrong? worshiped idols

Junior: What was the result? kingdom divided

Primary: What were the names of the two parts of the divided kingdom? Israel, Judah

Primary: In which one was Jerusalem? Judah

Junior: What did the first king of the northern kingdom do to keep his people from going to Jerusalem to worship? set up two gold calves

Junior: Whom do historians consider the greatest king of the northern division? Ahab

Junior: What was his wife's name? Jezebel

Junior: What disaster happened during his reign? drought

Primary: What great prophet was sent to warn Ahab? Elijah

Primary: What did Ahab try to do to Elijah? kill him

Primary: How did the Lord take care of Elijah? ravens fed him

Junior: When Elijah came back, what test did he suggest to Ahab? "The God that answers by fire ... "

Junior: Where was this test carried out? Mount Carmel

Junior: How long did the prophets of Baal call upon their god? all day

Primary: How did Elijah prepare his altar? twelve stones, twelve barrels of water

Primary: What happened when Elijah called upon the Lord? fire came

Junior: What did the people say? "The Lord is God"

Junior: What happened to the prophets of Baal? Elijah killed them

Junior: What came as a result of this test? rain

Intermediate: What do (1) Israel, and (2) Judah, represent? (1) the mind or understanding (2) the heart or will

Senior: What does Elijah represent? the prophetic Word
