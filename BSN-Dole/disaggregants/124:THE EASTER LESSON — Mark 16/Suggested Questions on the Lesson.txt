
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What do we call this Sunday? Easter

Primary: What do we celebrate on Easter? Jesus' rising from death

Primary: What was last Sunday? Palm Sunday

Primary: Why was it called Palm Sunday? people spread palm branches before the Lord

Intermediate: Why did the Lord ride into Jerusalem on a donkey? sign of kingship

Junior: Why did the people welcome Him? thought He would be worldly ruler

Intermediate: Why did they turn against Him so soon? found out his kingdom was not of this world

Junior: What happened on Thursday evening of that week? last supper

Junior: What happened on Friday? crucifixion

Primary: Who came to the sepulcher on Sunday morning? two Marys, Salome

Primary: Why did they come? to anoint body

Junior: What question was in their minds? who would roll away the stone for them

Primary: What did they find? stone rolled away, the Lord gone, young man in white

Primary: What did the angel tell them? He is risen

Junior: To whom did the Lord appear first? Mary Magdalene

Junior: Did the disciples believe when they were told that He had been seen? no

Junior: What did He say when He finally appeared to them all? upbraided them for unbelief

Junior: What did He tell them to do? go and preach Gospel, baptize

Junior: What did He say about those who believe? will be saved

Junior: How did He leave them? taken up into heaven

Intermediate: What is pictured by the stone which sealed the tomb? materialistic thinking

Senior: Why did the women see the Lord first after He rose? they represent our affections for divine qualities
