
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What do we call this Sunday? Easter

Younger Children: What do we celebrate on Easter? Jesus' rising from death

Younger Children: What was last Sunday? Palm Sunday

Younger Children: Why was it called Palm Sunday? people spread palm branches before the Lord

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord ride into Jerusalem on a donkey? sign of kingship

Older Children: Why did the people welcome Him? thought He would be worldly ruler

Younger Teens: Why did they turn against Him so soon? found out his kingdom was not of this world

Older Children: What happened on Thursday evening of that week? last supper

Older Children: What happened on Friday? crucifixion

Younger Children: Who came to the sepulcher on Sunday morning? two Marys, Salome

Younger Children: Why did they come? to anoint body

Older Children: What question was in their minds? who would roll away the stone for them

Younger Children: What did they find? stone rolled away, the Lord gone, young man in white

Younger Children: What did the angel tell them? He is risen

Older Children: To whom did the Lord appear first? Mary Magdalene

Older Children: Did the disciples believe when they were told that He had been seen? no

Older Children: What did He say when He finally appeared to them all? upbraided them for unbelief

Older Children: What did He tell them to do? go and preach Gospel, baptize

Older Children: What did He say about those who believe? will be saved

Older Children: How did He leave them? taken up into heaven

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the stone which sealed the tomb? materialistic thinking

Older Teens: Why did the women see the Lord first after He rose? they represent our affections for divine qualities
