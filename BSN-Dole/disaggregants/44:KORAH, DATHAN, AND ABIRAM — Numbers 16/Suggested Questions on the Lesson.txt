
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who led a rebellion against Moses and Aaron? Korah

Primary: What tribe was Korah from? Levi

Intermediate: What was the office of the Levites? care of tabernacle and worship

Intermediate: What was Aaron's office? high priest

Intermediate: Why was Korah dissatisfied? jealous of Aaron

Primary: What other men joined in the rebellion? Dathan and Abiram

Intermediate: What tribe were they from? Reuben

Primary: Where did they insist on staying? in their own tents

Primary: How were Dathan and Abiram and their families destroyed? earth opened

Intermediate: What other group of Korah's supporters went to the tabernacle with him? 250 princes of assembly

Primary: What happened to them? destroyed by fire

Junior: What became of their censers? made into plates to cover altar

Intermediate: What happened to the people who still murmured? plague

Intermediate: How was the plague stopped? Aaron with censer

Intermediate: What is pictured by the punishment of Dathan and Abiram? result of "faith alone"

Intermediate: What does a censer represent? worship

Senior: What is pictured by the punishment of Korah and the princes with him? result of twisting truth to support one's selfish desires

Senior: Why were the censers not destroyed? fault not with worship as such but with wrong attitude toward it
