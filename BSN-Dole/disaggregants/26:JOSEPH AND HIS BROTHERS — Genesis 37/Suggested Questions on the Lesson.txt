
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: How many sons were born to Jacob in Haran? eleven

Older Children: Who was the oldest? Reuben

Younger Children: Who was the last one born in Haran? Joseph

Younger Children: What son was born after they returned to Canaan? Benjamin

Older Children: Which two sons were the children of Rachel? Joseph and Benjamin

Younger Children: Which son did Jacob love best? Joseph

Younger Children: What did Jacob make for Joseph? tunic of various colors

Older Children: Why did Joseph's older brothers hate him? father's favorite, his dreams

Older Children: Can you tell about Joseph's two dreams? sheaves; sun, moon, stars

Younger Children: On what errand did Jacob send Joseph? to find brothers

Older Children: Where did Joseph find his brothers? Dothan

Younger Children: What did they decide to do? kill him

Older Children: Who saved Joseph's life? Reuben

Younger Children: What did the brothers finally do with Joseph? threw him in a pit

Younger Children: Into what country was he sold? Egypt

Older Teens: What do the twelve sons of Jacob represent? all basic mental faculties

Younger Teens: What does Joseph represent? spiritual principle

Older Teens: What is pictured by Joseph's being sold into Egypt? trying to relegate that principle to our external memory
