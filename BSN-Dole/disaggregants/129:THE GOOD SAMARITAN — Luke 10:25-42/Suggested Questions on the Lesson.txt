
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What is an apostle? one sent out

Younger Children: How many apostles were there? twelve

Younger Teens: When the Lord sent out the apostles, what were they to preach? kingdom of God

Younger Teens: How were they to be cared for? by those they served

Younger Children: How did the Lord help the apostles to feed the multitude? multiplied loaves and fish

Younger Teens: What question did the Lord ask of the apostles? who do people say I am?

Younger Teens: What was Peter's answer? the Christ of God

Younger Children: Who were the three apostles closest to the Lord? Peter, James, John

Younger Children: What vision were they allowed to see? transfiguration

Older Children: In our lesson for today what does the lawyer ask the Lord? how may I inherit eternal life?

Younger Children: To what does the Lord refer him for his answer? Scriptures

Younger Teens: What answer does the lawyer find? love Lord and neighbor

Younger Children: What question does the lawyer ask to "justify" himself? who is my neighbor?

Younger Children: With what parable does the Lord answer him? Good Samaritan

Younger Children: Where was the man going when he fell among thieves? Jerusalem to Jericho

Younger Children: What two men passed him by without helping him? priest, Levite

Younger Children: Who did help him? Samaritan

Younger Teens: Who were the Samaritans? people Assyrians had brought in

Younger Teens: What did this parable teach the lawyer? to help anyone in need

Younger Teens: What was the difference between Martha and Mary? first served, latter listened

Younger Children: Which one did the Lord say had chosen the better part? Mary

Older Teens: Why are we given this story immediately after the parable of the Good Samaritan? to help us learn how to love the neighbor
