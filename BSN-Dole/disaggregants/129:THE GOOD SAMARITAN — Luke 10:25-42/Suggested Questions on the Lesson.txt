
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What is an apostle? one sent out

Primary: How many apostles were there? twelve

Intermediate: When the Lord sent out the apostles, what were they to preach? kingdom of God

Intermediate: How were they to be cared for? by those they served

Primary: How did the Lord help the apostles to feed the multitude? multiplied loaves and fish

Intermediate: What question did the Lord ask of the apostles? who do people say I am? 

Intermediate: What was Peter's answer? the Christ of God

Primary: Who were the three apostles closest to the Lord? Peter, James, John

Primary: What vision were they allowed to see? transfiguration

Junior: In our lesson for today what does the lawyer ask the Lord? how may I inherit eternal life?

Primary: To what does the Lord refer him for his answer? Scriptures

Intermediate: What answer does the lawyer find? love Lord and neighbor

Primary: What question does the lawyer ask to "justify" himself? who is my neighbor?

Primary: With what parable does the Lord answer him? Good Samaritan

Primary: Where was the man going when he fell among thieves? Jerusalem to Jericho

Primary: What two men passed him by without helping him? priest, Levite

Primary: Who did help him? Samaritan

Intermediate: Who were the Samaritans? people Assyrians had brought in

Intermediate: What did this parable teach the lawyer? to help anyone in need

Intermediate: What was the difference between Martha and Mary? first served, latter listened

Primary: Which one did the Lord say had chosen the better part? Mary

Senior: Why are we given this story immediately after the parable of the Good Samaritan? to help us learn how to love the neighbor
