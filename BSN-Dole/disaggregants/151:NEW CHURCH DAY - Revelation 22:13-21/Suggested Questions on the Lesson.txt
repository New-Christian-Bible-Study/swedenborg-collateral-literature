
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What special day do we celebrate today? New Church Day 

Senior: Why do we call it that? @TCR 791

Primary: What is the first thing the Lord says about Himself in our lesson for today? I am alpha and omega

Primary: What are alpha and omega? first and last letters of Greek alphabet

Primary: How do we know it is Jesus Christ who says this? John recognized Him

Primary: Where do we often hear the last verse of our lesson for today? benediction in church

Intermediate: Why did the Lord have to make a Second Coming? men needed new insights into divine truth

Junior: Why must the Lord always leave us free to choose whether or not we will do right? love for the Lord and for what is good cannot be forced

Intermediate: Why should we try to remember this even when we are children? helps prepare us for heaven

Intermediate: Why do men say that we are living in a new age? great scientific advances

Intermediate: What false conclusion do we sometimes draw from this? Biblical laws "outdated"

Intermediate: What laws will never change? the Lord's laws

Senior: What has the Lord done to meet our need in this new age? opened inner sense of Word

Senior: What obligation does this lay upon us? regular study and continued effort to live up to the new truth
