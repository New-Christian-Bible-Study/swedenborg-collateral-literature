
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who were the first four disciples the Lord chose? Peter, Andrew, James, John

Younger Children: Who were the three who saw Him transfigured? Peter, James, John

Younger Children: What does transfigure mean? change of face

Younger Children: Where did they go with the Lord to see Him transfigured? high mountain

Younger Children: Who were seen talking with Him? Moses, Elijah

Older Children: What did Peter want to do? build three tents

Older Children: What happened which frightened the disciples? bright cloud, voice

Younger Children: Whom did they see afterward? Jesus only

Older Children: What did the Lord tell them about what they had seen? Don't tell others

Older Children: What question did they ask Him? Why do scribes say, "Elijah must come first"?

Older Children: What did He tell them? John the Baptist fulfilled that prophecy

Younger Teens: Why were Moses and Elijah seen with the Lord? to show He embodies the Law and the Prophets

Younger Teens: Why is John the Baptist brought into this story? taught repentance (first step)

Older Teens: What is the great lesson of the transfiguration story? it is possible for everyone who believes to see the Lord in His Word
