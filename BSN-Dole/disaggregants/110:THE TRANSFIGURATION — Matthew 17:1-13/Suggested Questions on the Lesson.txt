
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who were the first four disciples the Lord chose? Peter, Andrew, James, John

Primary: Who were the three who saw Him transfigured? Peter, James, John

Primary: What does transfigure mean? change of face

Primary: Where did they go with the Lord to see Him transfigured? high mountain

Primary: Who were seen talking with Him? Moses, Elijah

Junior: What did Peter want to do? build three tents

Junior: What happened which frightened the disciples? bright cloud, voice

Primary: Whom did they see afterward? Jesus only

Junior: What did the Lord tell them about what they had seen? Don't tell others

Junior: What question did they ask Him? Why do scribes say, "Elijah must come first"?

Junior: What did He tell them? John the Baptist fulfilled that prophecy

Intermediate: Why were Moses and Elijah seen with the Lord? to show He embodies the Law and the Prophets

Intermediate: Why is John the Baptist brought into this story? taught repentance (first step)

Senior: What is the great lesson of the transfiguration story? it is possible for everyone who believes to see the Lord in His Word
