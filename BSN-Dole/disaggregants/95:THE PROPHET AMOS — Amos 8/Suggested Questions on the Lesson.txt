
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many "Minor Prophets" are there? +
*A:* twelve

*Q:* (Older Children): Why are they called "Minor"? +
*A:* short books

*Q:* (Older Children): When did the earliest of them live? +
*A:* about 800 B.C.

*Q:* (Older Children): What is the period of the last three? +
*A:* about 400 B.C.

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which one in the series is Amos? +
*A:* third

*Q:* (Older Children): When did he live? +
*A:* time of Uzziah (about 800 B.C.)

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was his home? +
*A:* Tekoa

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was his occupation? +
*A:* shepherd

*Q:* (Older Children): To whom did he prophesy? +
*A:* Israel (also Edom, Ammon, Moab)

*Q:* (Younger Children): With what vision does our chapter begin? +
*A:* basket of fruit

*Q:* (Younger Children): What bad things were the Israelites doing? +
*A:* cheating, stealing

*Q:* (Older Children): What does the Lord say is to happen to them? +
*A:* nation will end

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Of what kind of hunger and thirst does verse 11 speak? +
*A:* of hearing the Word

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Which of the Blessings speaks of the same thing? +
*A:* fourth

*Q:* (Older Teens): How can we satisfy this kind of hunger and thirst? +
*A:* keep hearts pure, minds open to hear the Word
