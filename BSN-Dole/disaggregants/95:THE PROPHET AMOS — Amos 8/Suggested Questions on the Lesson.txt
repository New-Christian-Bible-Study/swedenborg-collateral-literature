
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: How many "Minor Prophets" are there? twelve

Older Children: Why are they called "Minor"? short books

Older Children: When did the earliest of them live? about 800 B.C.

Older Children: What is the period of the last three? about 400 B.C.

Younger Children: Which one in the series is Amos? third

Older Children: When did he live? time of Uzziah (about 800 B.C.)

Older Children: Where was his home? Tekoa

Younger Children: What was his occupation? shepherd

Older Children: To whom did he prophesy? Israel (also Edom, Ammon, Moab)

Younger Children: With what vision does our chapter begin? basket of fruit

Younger Children: What bad things were the Israelites doing? cheating, stealing

Older Children: What does the Lord say is to happen to them? nation will end

Younger Teens: Of what kind of hunger and thirst does verse 11 speak? of hearing the Word

Younger Teens: Which of the Blessings speaks of the same thing? fourth

Older Teens: How can we satisfy this kind of hunger and thirst? keep hearts pure, minds open to hear the Word
