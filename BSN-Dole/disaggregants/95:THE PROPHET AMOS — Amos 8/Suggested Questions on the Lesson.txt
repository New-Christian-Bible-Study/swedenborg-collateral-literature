
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: How many "Minor Prophets" are there? twelve

Junior: Why are they called "Minor"? short books

Junior: When did the earliest of them live? about 800 B.C.

Junior: What is the period of the last three? about 400 B.C.

Primary: Which one in the series is Amos? third

Junior: When did he live? time of Uzziah (about 800 B.C.)

Junior: Where was his home? Tekoa

Primary: What was his occupation? shepherd

Junior: To whom did he prophesy? Israel (also Edom, Ammon, Moab)

Primary: With what vision does our chapter begin? basket of fruit

Primary: What bad things were the Israelites doing? cheating, stealing

Junior: What does the Lord say is to happen to them? nation will end

Intermediate: Of what kind of hunger and thirst does verse 11 speak? of hearing the Word

Intermediate: Which of the Blessings speaks of the same thing? fourth

Senior: How can we satisfy this kind of hunger and thirst? keep hearts pure, minds open to hear the Word
