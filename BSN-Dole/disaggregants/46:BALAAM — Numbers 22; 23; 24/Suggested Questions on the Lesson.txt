
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Do you remember what led the children of Israel through the wilderness? +
*A:* pillar of cloud/fire

*Q:* (Older Children): How long did it keep them wandering there? +
*A:* forty years

*Q:* (Older Children): What was their last encampment in the wilderness? +
*A:* plains of Moab

*Q:* (Younger Children): What king was afraid of them? +
*A:* Balak (king of Moab)

*Q:* (Younger Children): To whom did he send for help? +
*A:* Balaam

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he want Balaam to do? +
*A:* curse Israel

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did Balaam get his power? +
*A:* from God

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord first tell Balaam? +
*A:* not to go

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell him when Balak sent for him the second time? +
*A:* speak only My word

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to Balaam on the way? +
*A:* angel frightened his donkey

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What preparations did Balaam have Balak make? +
*A:* seven altars, oxen, rams

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How did Balaam know what should be done? +
*A:* knowledge of correspondences

*Q:* (Older Children): How many times did Balaam try to curse Israel? +
*A:* three

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened each time? +
*A:* became a blessing

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why could he not curse Israel? +
*A:* no real power in evil intentions

*Q:* (Younger Children): What special prophecy did Balaam speak? +
*A:* Star

*Q:* (Older Children): How was he able to injure Israel? +
*A:* taught Balak how to tempt people

*Q:* (Older Children): What finally happened to Balaam? +
*A:* killed in battle between Israel and Midian

*A:* truly orderly life, with worship of the Lord at the center
