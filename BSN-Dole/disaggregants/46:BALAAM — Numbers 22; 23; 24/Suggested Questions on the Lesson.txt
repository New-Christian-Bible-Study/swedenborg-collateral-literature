
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Do you remember what led the children of Israel through the wilderness? pillar of cloud/fire

Older Children: How long did it keep them wandering there? forty years

Older Children: What was their last encampment in the wilderness? plains of Moab

Younger Children: What king was afraid of them? Balak (king of Moab)

Younger Children: To whom did he send for help? Balaam

Younger Children: What did he want Balaam to do? curse Israel

Younger Children: Where did Balaam get his power? from God

Older Children: What did the Lord first tell Balaam? not to go

Older Children: What did He tell him when Balak sent for him the second time? speak only My word

Younger Children: What happened to Balaam on the way? angel frightened his donkey

Younger Teens: What preparations did Balaam have Balak make? seven altars, oxen, rams

Younger Teens: How did Balaam know what should be done? knowledge of correspondences

Older Children: How many times did Balaam try to curse Israel? three

Older Children: What happened each time? became a blessing

Younger Teens: Why could he not curse Israel? no real power in evil intentions

Younger Children: What special prophecy did Balaam speak? Star

Older Children: How was he able to injure Israel? taught Balak how to tempt people

Older Children: What finally happened to Balaam? killed in battle between Israel and Midian

Older Teens: What does the encampment of Israel picture? truly orderly life, with worship of the Lord at the center
