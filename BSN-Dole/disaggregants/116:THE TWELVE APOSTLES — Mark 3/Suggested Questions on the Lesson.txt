
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What did the Lord do first for the man who was sick of the palsy? forgave sins

Older Children: What did the man have to do himself? rise, pick up bed, walk

Older Children: What disciple was called in our last lesson? Matthew (Levi)

Older Children: What faults did the scribes and Pharisees find with the Lord? ate with sinners, disciples didn't fast, broke sabbath

Younger Children: What did the Lord say about new wine? needs new containers

Older Children: What did He mean? old ideas and new truth don't go together

Older Children: What did He say about the sabbath? made for man

Younger Children: In our lesson today what miracle of healing was performed? withered hand

Older Children: Why did the scribes and Pharisees object to it? sabbath, challenged their authority

Older Children: With whom did they plot against the Lord? Herodians

Younger Children: Where did the Lord go to talk to the multitudes? seashore

Younger Children: Where did He go teach His disciples? mountain

Younger Children: How many did He choose to be apostles? twelve

Older Children: What powers did He give them? preach, cast out demons

Older Children: Name as many of the apostles as you can.

Older Children: What did the Lord's family think about what He was doing? thought He was crazy

Older Children: Where did the scribes say His power came from? the devil (Beelzebub)

Older Teens: What is the "unforgivable sin"? rejecting truth that condemns our evils

Older Teens: Why? makes it impossible for the Lord to help us

Younger Teens: What did the Lord say when He was told that His mother and His brethren were looking for Him? Whoever does the will of God is my mother and brother ...

Younger Teens: Why did He seem to disown them? because He knew why they came
