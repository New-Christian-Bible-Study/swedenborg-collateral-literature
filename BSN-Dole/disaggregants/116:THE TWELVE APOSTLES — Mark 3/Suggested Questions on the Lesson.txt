
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What did the Lord do first for the man who was sick of the palsy? forgave sins

Junior: What did the man have to do himself? rise, pick up bed, walk

Junior: What disciple was called in our last lesson? Matthew (Levi)

Junior: What faults did the scribes and Pharisees find with the Lord? ate with sinners, disciples didn't fast, broke sabbath

Primary: What did the Lord say about new wine? needs new containers

Junior: What did He mean? old ideas and new truth don't go together

Junior: What did He say about the sabbath? made for man

Primary: In our lesson today what miracle of healing was performed? withered hand

Junior: Why did the scribes and Pharisees object to it? sabbath, challenged their authority

Junior: With whom did they plot against the Lord? Herodians

Primary: Where did the Lord go to talk to the multitudes? seashore

Primary: Where did He go teach His disciples? mountain

Primary: How many did He choose to be apostles? twelve

Junior: What powers did He give them? preach, cast out demons

Junior: Name as many of the apostles as you can.

Junior: What did the Lord's family think about what He was doing? thought He was crazy

Junior: Where did the scribes say His power came from? the devil (Beelzebub)

Senior: What is the "unforgivable sin"? rejecting truth that condemns our evils

Senior: Why? makes it impossible for the Lord to help us

Intermediate: What did the Lord say when He was told that His mother and His brethren were looking for Him? Whoever does the will of God is my mother and brother ...

Intermediate: Why did He seem to disown them? because He knew why they came
