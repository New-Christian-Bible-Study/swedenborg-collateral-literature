
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord do first for the man who was sick of the palsy? +
*A:* forgave sins

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the man have to do himself? +
*A:* rise, pick up bed, walk

*Q:* (Older Children): What disciple was called in our last lesson? +
*A:* Matthew (Levi)

*Q:* (Older Children): What faults did the scribes and Pharisees find with the Lord? +
*A:* ate with sinners, disciples didn't fast, broke sabbath

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord say about new wine? +
*A:* needs new containers

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He mean? +
*A:* old ideas and new truth don't go together

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He say about the sabbath? +
*A:* made for man

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our lesson today what miracle of healing was performed? +
*A:* withered hand

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the scribes and Pharisees object to it? +
*A:* sabbath, challenged their authority

*Q:* (Older Children): With whom did they plot against the Lord? +
*A:* Herodians

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did the Lord go to talk to the multitudes? +
*A:* seashore

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did He go teach His disciples? +
*A:* mountain

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many did He choose to be apostles? +
*A:* twelve

*Q:* (Older Children): What powers did He give them? +
*A:* preach, cast out demons

Older Children: Name as many of the apostles as you can.

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord's family think about what He was doing? +
*A:* thought He was crazy

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the scribes say His power came from? +
*A:* the devil (Beelzebub)

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is the "unforgivable sin"? +
*A:* rejecting truth that condemns our evils

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why? +
*A:* makes it impossible for the Lord to help us

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord say when He was told that His mother and His brethren were looking for Him? +
*A:* Whoever does the will of God is my mother and brother ...

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did He seem to disown them? +
*A:* because He knew why they came
