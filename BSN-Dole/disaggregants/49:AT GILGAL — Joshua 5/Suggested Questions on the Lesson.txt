
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How long did the Israelites wander in the wilderness? forty years

Older Children: Where did Moses die? Mount Nebo

Younger Children: Who succeeded him as leader? Joshua

Younger Children: How did the Israelites cross the Jordan? miracle

Younger Children: What did they take from the bed of the river? twelve stones

Younger Children: What was done with these twelve stones? made monument

Younger Children: What was their first encampment in the Holy Land? Gilgal

Older Children: What does Gilgal mean? rolling

Older Children: Why was it called that? "I have rolled away the reproach of Egypt"

Older Children: What rite was Joshua commanded to observe at Gilgal? circumcision

Younger Children: What feast did they celebrate there? Passover

Younger Children: What is said about the manna? it stopped

Older Children: Why did they no longer need it? ample food there

Older Children: What vision did Joshua see? angel with sword

Older Children: What did the angel say of himself? captain of God's army

Older Children: What did he tell Joshua to do? take shoes off

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the wandering in the wilderness? reformation (reordering life)

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the entrance into the Holy Land? beginning of real spiritual living

Older Teens: What is the difference in representation between (1) Moses, and (2) Joshua? (1) truth obeyed through fear, (2) truth fighting in our lives

Older Teens: What two things were done at Gilgal which prefigure (1) baptism, and (2) the Holy Supper? (1) circumcision (purification rite) (2) Passover (memorial of salvation)
