
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: How long did the Israelites wander in the wilderness? forty years

Junior: Where did Moses die? Mount Nebo

Primary: Who succeeded him as leader? Joshua

Primary: How did the Israelites cross the Jordan? miracle

Primary: What did they take from the bed of the river? twelve stones

Primary: What was done with these twelve stones? made monument

Primary: What was their first encampment in the Holy Land? Gilgal

Junior: What does Gilgal mean? rolling

Junior: Why was it called that? "I have rolled away the reproach of Egypt"

Junior: What rite was Joshua commanded to observe at Gilgal? circumcision

Primary: What feast did they celebrate there? Passover

Primary: What is said about the manna? it stopped

Junior: Why did they no longer need it? ample food there

Junior: What vision did Joshua see? angel with sword

Junior: What did the angel say of himself? captain of God's army

Junior: What did he tell Joshua to do? take shoes off

Intermediate: What is pictured by the wandering in the wilderness? reformation (reordering life)

Intermediate: What is pictured by the entrance into the Holy Land? beginning of real spiritual living

Senior: What is the difference in representation between (1) Moses, and (2) Joshua? (1) truth obeyed through fear, (2) truth fighting in our lives

Senior: What two things were done at Gilgal which prefigure (1) baptism, and (2) the Holy Supper? (1) circumcision (purification rite) (2) Passover (memorial of salvation)
