
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): How long did the Israelites wander in the wilderness? +
*A:* forty years

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Moses die? +
*A:* Mount Nebo

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who succeeded him as leader? +
*A:* Joshua

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the Israelites cross the Jordan? +
*A:* miracle

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they take from the bed of the river? +
*A:* twelve stones

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was done with these twelve stones? +
*A:* made monument

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was their first encampment in the Holy Land? +
*A:* Gilgal

*Q:* (Older Children): What does Gilgal mean? +
*A:* rolling

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was it called that? +
*A:* "I have rolled away the reproach of Egypt"

*Q:* (Older Children): What rite was Joshua commanded to observe at Gilgal? +
*A:* circumcision

*Q:* (Younger Children): What feast did they celebrate there? +
*A:* Passover

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is said about the manna? +
*A:* it stopped

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did they no longer need it? +
*A:* ample food there

*Q:* (Older Children): What vision did Joshua see? +
*A:* angel with sword

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the angel say of himself? +
*A:* captain of God's army

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he tell Joshua to do? +
*A:* take shoes off

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by the wandering in the wilderness? +
*A:* reformation (reordering life)

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by the entrance into the Holy Land? +
*A:* beginning of real spiritual living

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is the difference in representation between (1) Moses, and (2) Joshua? +
*A:* (1) truth obeyed through fear, (2) truth fighting in our lives

*Q:* (Older Teens): What two things were done at Gilgal which prefigure (1) baptism, and (2) the Holy Supper? +
*A:* (1) circumcision (purification rite) (2) Passover (memorial of salvation)
