
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Where was the pool of Bethesda? Jerusalem

Younger Children: How many porches did it have? five

Younger Children: Who lay in the porches? sick people

Younger Children: Why did they lie there? hoping to be healed

Older Children: How long had one man been sick? thirty-eight years

Younger Children: What did the Lord ask him? do you want to be well?

Older Children: What did he answer? no one helps me

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him to do? rise, take bed, walk

Younger Children: What happened when he obeyed? healed

Older Children: What did the Lord tell the man later? sin no more

Younger Children: In our story today how long had the man been blind? since birth

Younger Teens: What did the disciples ask the Lord about him? who sinned?

Older Children: What did the Lord tell them? no one

Younger Children: How was the man's sight restored? clay, washing in pool

Younger Children: What was the first objection to the miracle? done on the sabbath

Older Children: What did their questions first lead the man to decide about the one who had healed him? a prophet

Older Children: Why were the man's parents afraid to praise the Lord openly? fear of being cast out

Older Children: When they could not deny the facts, what did his enemies say about the Lord? he is a sinner

Older Children: What did the man answer? "Never since the world began ... "

Younger Children: How was he punished? cast out

Older Children: What did the Lord then tell him? He was Son of God

Younger Children: How did the man respond? worshiped Him

Older Children: Why did the Lord say He came into the world? for judgment

Older Children: What did the Pharisees ask? are we blind?

Older Children: What did He tell them? if you were ... no guilt

Younger Teens: What does blindness represent? ignorance

Older Teens: What vital choice in life must each one of us make? whether to believe the Lord or not
