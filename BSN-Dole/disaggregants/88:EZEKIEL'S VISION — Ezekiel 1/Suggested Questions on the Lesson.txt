
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Who are the four Major Prophets? Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

Older Children: Where did Isaiah and Jeremiah prophesy? Judah

Older Children: Where did Ezekiel and Daniel prophesy? Babylon

Older Children: What office besides prophet did Ezekiel hold? priest

Older Children: After he was taken captive, where did he live? by the river Chebar

Younger Children: How was he first shown that he was to be a prophet? vision

Younger Children: How many living creatures did he see? four

Younger Children: How many faces did each one have? four

Younger Children: What were the faces? man, lion, ox, eagle

Older Children: What were their feet like? "straight," soles like calves' hoofs

Older Children: What did they have under their wings? human hands

Younger Children: What went on the earth beside them? wheels

Older Children: How did they move? with the creatures

Older Children: What did the noise of their wings sound like? rushing water, thunder, army

Younger Children: What did Ezekiel see above the living creatures? throne

Younger Children: Who sat on the throne? the Lord

Older Children: By what other name did Ezekiel afterward call the living creatures? cherubim

Younger Children: Where have we heard of cherubim before? Garden of Eden, ark of the covenant

Older Teens: What do cherubim represent? divine providence protecting holy things from profanation
