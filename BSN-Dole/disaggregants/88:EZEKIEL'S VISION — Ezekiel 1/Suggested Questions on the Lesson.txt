
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Who are the four Major Prophets? Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

Junior: Where did Isaiah and Jeremiah prophesy? Judah

Junior: Where did Ezekiel and Daniel prophesy? Babylon

Junior: What office besides prophet did Ezekiel hold? priest

Junior: After he was taken captive, where did he live? by the river Chebar

Primary: How was he first shown that he was to be a prophet? vision

Primary: How many living creatures did he see? four

Primary: How many faces did each one have? four

Primary: What were the faces? man, lion, ox, eagle

Junior: What were their feet like? "straight," soles like calves' hoofs

Junior: What did they have under their wings? human hands

Primary: What went on the earth beside them? wheels

Junior: How did they move? with the creatures

Junior: What did the noise of their wings sound like? rushing water, thunder, army

Primary: What did Ezekiel see above the living creatures? throne

Primary: Who sat on the throne? the Lord

Junior: By what other name did Ezekiel afterward call the living creatures? cherubim

Primary: Where have we heard of cherubim before? Garden of Eden, ark of the covenant

Senior: What do cherubim represent? divine providence protecting holy things from profanation
