
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the Lord go after His baptism? +
*A:* wilderness

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to Him there? +
*A:* tempted

*Q:* (Older Children): How did He answer the devil each time? +
*A:* "It is written ... "

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is our lesson today about? +
*A:* sermon on the mount

*Q:* (Younger Children): With what does the sermon on the mount begin? +
*A:* Blessings (Beatitudes)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does blessed mean? +
*A:* happy

*Q:* (Older Children): Are the things the Lord calls blessed just what we would expect? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): What do they teach is the source of happiness? +
*A:* the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many chapters does the sermon on the mount take up? +
*A:* three

*Q:* (Older Children): With what parable does the sermon on the mount end? +
*A:* house built on rock/sand

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is meant by our "house"? +
*A:* our character

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is the "rock" on which it is safe to build? +
*A:* truth from the Word

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why are the opinions of men like sand? +
*A:* shifting, unstable, no unifying element

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why could the Lord speak with authority? +
