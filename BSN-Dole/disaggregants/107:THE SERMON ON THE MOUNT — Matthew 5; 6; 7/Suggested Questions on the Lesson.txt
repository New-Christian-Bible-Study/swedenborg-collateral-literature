
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What happened to Him there? tempted

Older Children: How did He answer the devil each time? "It is written ... "

Younger Children: What is our lesson today about? sermon on the mount

Younger Children: With what does the sermon on the mount begin? Blessings (Beatitudes)

Younger Children: What does blessed mean? happy

Older Children: Are the things the Lord calls blessed just what we would expect? no

Older Children: What do they teach is the source of happiness? the Lord
