
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What did the Lord say about little children? of such is kingdom of heaven

Junior: What did the rich young man ask? what must I do to inherit eternal life?

Junior: What did the Lord tell him to do as the first thing necessary? obey commandments

Primary: What was the young man not willing to do? give up his riches

Junior: What did the Lord say about [trust in] riches? makes it hard to enter heaven

Primary: What did James and John want? seats of honor

Junior: What did the Lord say true greatness is? being servant to all

Junior: When the Lord was at supper in Bethany, what did a woman do for Him? anointed Him

Junior: What did some of the disciples say? this was wasteful

Junior: What did the Lord tell them? she has done a beautiful thing

Primary: How did the Lord tell the two disciples to find the house where they should prepare the Passover feast? follow man with water jar

Primary: Where was the room in the house? upstairs

Junior: What did the Passover commemorate? release from Egyptian slavery

Primary: When the Lord told the disciples that one of them would betray Him, what did each one ask? "Is it I?"

Primary: What did the Lord do with the bread and wine as they were eating? blessed, gave to disciples

Primary: Of what Christian sacrament was this the beginning? Holy Supper

Intermediate: What do the bread and wine represent? divine goodness and truth

Junior: Where did the Lord go after supper? Gethsemane

Junior: What was His prayer there? to "remove cup"

Junior: Who betrayed the Lord? Judas Iscariot

Junior: How did he betray Him? with a kiss

Junior: Where was the Lord taken to be tried? before the high priest

Senior: What accusation did the high priest make against Him? Why? blasphemy, claimed to be the Christ

Junior: What did Peter do which he had been sure he would not do? denied the Lord three times

Senior: What do we commemorate in the Holy Supper? our redemption from power of evil
