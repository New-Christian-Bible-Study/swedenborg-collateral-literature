
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What king of Judah is our lesson about today? Hezekiah

Older Children: What happened to Israel early in his reign? captured by Assyria

Older Children: How was Judah saved from Assyria? angel of the Lord

Younger Children: Who was the prophet in Judah in Hezekiah's time? Isaiah

Younger Children: When Hezekiah fell sick, what did Isaiah tell him? you will die

Younger Children: What did Hezekiah do? prayed

Younger Children: What new message from the Lord did Isaiah bring him? he would live fifteen more years

Younger Children: What was the matter with Hezekiah? boil (ulcer)

Younger Children: How did Isaiah cure him? figs

Older Children: What sign did Hezekiah ask? shadow go back ten degrees

Older Children: What king sent messengers to Hezekiah? Babylon

Older Children: What did Hezekiah show them? all his treasures

Older Children: What did Isaiah tell him? that was foolish

Younger Teens: How did the captivity of Israel affect Judah? left it unprotected on north

Older Teens: What do (1) Assyria, and (2) Babylon, represent? (1) worldly, naturalistic thinking (2) love of dominion from love of self
