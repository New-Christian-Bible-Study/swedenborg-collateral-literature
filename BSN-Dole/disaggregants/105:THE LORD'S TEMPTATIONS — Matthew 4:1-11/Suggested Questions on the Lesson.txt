
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who came before the Lord to prepare His way? John the Baptist

Older Children: Where did John the Baptist live? wilderness of Judea

Younger Children: What can you tell about his clothing and food? camel hair; locusts, wild honey

Older Children: What was his message? Repent!

Older Children: What did he say when the lord came to him to be baptized? I need to be baptized by you

Older Children: What did the Lord answer? it is fitting for us to fulfill all righteousness

Younger Children: What came down from heaven when the Lord was baptized? a dove

Older Children: What did the voice from heaven say? This is my beloved Son

Older Children: Where did the Lord go after He was baptized? wilderness

Younger Children: How long was He in the wilderness? forty days

Younger Children: Who tempted Him there? "the devil"

Younger Children: What was the first temptation? stones to bread

Older Children: What was the second temptation? throw self down

Older Children: What was the third temptation? worship devil

Younger Children: How did the Lord answer each time? "It is written ... "

Younger Children: What four words can we say whenever we are tempted to do wrong? "Get thee hence, Satan"

Younger Children: Who came and ministered to the Lord when His temptations were over? angels

Younger Teens: What do the three temptations mean?
(1) to be satisfied with natural idea of good,
(2) to expect the Lord to save us in spite of our folly,
(3) to give up the effort to learn and do the Lord's will

Older Teens: Why was the Lord tempted? to glorify His humanity
