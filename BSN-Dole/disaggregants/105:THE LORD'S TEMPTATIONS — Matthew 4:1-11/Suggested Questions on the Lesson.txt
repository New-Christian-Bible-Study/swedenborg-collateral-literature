
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who came before the Lord to prepare His way? John the Baptist

Junior: Where did John the Baptist live? wilderness of Judea

Primary: What can you tell about his clothing and food? camel hair; locusts, wild honey

Junior: What was his message? Repent!

Junior: What did he say when the lord came to him to be baptized? I need to be baptized by you

Junior: What did the Lord answer? it is fitting for us to fulfill all righteousness

Primary: What came down from heaven when the Lord was baptized? a dove

Junior: What did the voice from heaven say? This is my beloved Son

Junior: Where did the Lord go after He was baptized? wilderness

Primary: How long was He in the wilderness? forty days

Primary: Who tempted Him there? "the devil"

Primary: What was the first temptation? stones to bread

Junior: What was the second temptation? throw self down

Junior: What was the third temptation? worship devil

Primary: How did the Lord answer each time? "It is written ... "

Primary: What four words can we say whenever we are tempted to do wrong? "Get thee hence, Satan"

Primary: Who came and ministered to the Lord when His temptations were over? angels

Intermediate: What do the three temptations mean?
(1) to be satisfied with natural idea of good,
(2) to expect the Lord to save us in spite of our folly,
(3) to give up the effort to learn and do the Lord's will

Senior: Why was the Lord tempted? to glorify His humanity
