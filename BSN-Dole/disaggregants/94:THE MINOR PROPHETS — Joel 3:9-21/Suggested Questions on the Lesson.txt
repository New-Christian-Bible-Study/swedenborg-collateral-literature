
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Who were the four Major Prophets? Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

Younger Children: How many Minor Prophets were there? twelve

Older Children: Why are they called "Minor"? wrote shorter books

Younger Children: Where do their books come in the Bible? end of old Testament

Older Children: How do we know they were inspired? all claim to be the Word of the Lord

Younger Children: Which is the first of the Minor Prophets? Hosea

Younger Children: Which is the last? Malachi

Older Children: Which one is Joel? second

Older Children: How many chapters are there in his book? three

Younger Children: How does he describe the evils that work in our hearts? destructive insects

Older Children: What does he call the judgment day? day of the Lord

Older Children: What does the judgment day mean for us? when we die

Older Children: What is the "valley of decision"? our everyday life

Older Children: What does the day of the Lord bring to the evil? hell

Older Children: What does it bring to the good? heaven

Younger Teens: What would you say is the most important thing we learn from Joel? every day all of us are forming ourselves into the kinds of persons we will be forever
