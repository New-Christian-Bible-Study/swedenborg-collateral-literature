
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were the four Major Prophets? +
*A:* Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many Minor Prophets were there? +
*A:* twelve

*Q:* (Older Children): Why are they called "Minor"? +
*A:* wrote shorter books

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where do their books come in the Bible? +
*A:* end of old Testament

*Q:* (Older Children): How do we know they were inspired? +
*A:* all claim to be the Word of the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which is the first of the Minor Prophets? +
*A:* Hosea

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which is the last? +
*A:* Malachi

*Q:* (Older Children): Which one is Joel? +
*A:* second

*Q:* (Older Children): How many chapters are there in his book? +
*A:* three

*Q:* (Younger Children): How does he describe the evils that work in our hearts? +
*A:* destructive insects

*Q:* (Older Children): What does he call the judgment day? +
*A:* day of the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): What does the judgment day mean for us? +
*A:* when we die

*Q:* (Older Children): What is the "valley of decision"? +
*A:* our everyday life

*Q:* (Older Children): What does the day of the Lord bring to the evil? +
*A:* hell

*Q:* (Older Children): What does it bring to the good? +
*A:* heaven

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What would you say is the most important thing we learn from Joel? +
*A:* every day all of us are forming ourselves into the kinds of persons we will be forever
