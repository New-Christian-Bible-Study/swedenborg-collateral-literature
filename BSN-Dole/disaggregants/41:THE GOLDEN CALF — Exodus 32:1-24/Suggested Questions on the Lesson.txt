
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): How long did the Israelites camp at Sinai? +
*A:* almost a year

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did all the people hear the Lord speak from the top of Sinai? +
*A:* commandments

*Q:* (Older Children): What other instructions did the Lord give Moses for the people? +
*A:* laws of conduct, worship

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did Moses go to speak with the Lord? +
*A:* top of mount

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our lesson today, how long had he been gone? +
*A:* forty days

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the people think? +
*A:* gone forever

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they demand of Aaron? +
*A:* gods

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Aaron tell them to give him? +
*A:* gold earrings

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he make for them? +
*A:* calf

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Moses have in his hands when he came down from the mount? +
*A:* two tablets

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he do when he saw the people worshiping the golden calf? +
*A:* broke them

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did he do with the calf? +
*A:* melted, ground, sprinkled on water

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Aaron try to excuse himself? +
*A:* threw gold in fire, calf came out

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by the golden calf? +
*A:* love of worldly success and pleasure

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is the difference between (1) the first tables of stone, and (2) those hewn our later from the bottom of the mount? +
*A:* (1) high, spiritual form of law and (2) law adapted to state of worldly men
