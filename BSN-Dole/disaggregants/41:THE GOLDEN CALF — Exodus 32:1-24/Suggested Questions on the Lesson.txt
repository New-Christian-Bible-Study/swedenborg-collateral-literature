
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How long did the Israelites camp at Sinai? almost a year

Younger Children: What did all the people hear the Lord speak from the top of Sinai? commandments

Older Children: What other instructions did the Lord give Moses for the people? laws of conduct, worship

Younger Children: Where did Moses go to speak with the Lord? top of mount

Younger Children: In our lesson today, how long had he been gone? forty days

Older Children: What did the people think? gone forever

Older Children: What did they demand of Aaron? gods

Younger Children: What did Aaron tell them to give him? gold earrings

Younger Children: What did he make for them? calf

Younger Children: What did Moses have in his hands when he came down from the mount? two tablets

Younger Children: What did he do when he saw the people worshiping the golden calf? broke them

Younger Teens: What did he do with the calf? melted, ground, sprinkled on water

Older Children: How did Aaron try to excuse himself? threw gold in fire, calf came out

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the golden calf? love of worldly success and pleasure

Older Teens: What is the difference between (1) the first tables of stone, and (2) those hewn our later from the bottom of the mount? (1) high, spiritual form of law and (2) law adapted to state of worldly men
