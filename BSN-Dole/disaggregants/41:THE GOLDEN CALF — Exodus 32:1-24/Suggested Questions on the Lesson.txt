
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: How long did the Israelites camp at Sinai? almost a year

Primary: What did all the people hear the Lord speak from the top of Sinai? commandments

Junior: What other instructions did the Lord give Moses for the people? laws of conduct, worship

Primary: Where did Moses go to speak with the Lord? top of mount

Primary: In our lesson today, how long had he been gone? forty days

Junior: What did the people think? gone forever

Junior: What did they demand of Aaron? gods

Primary: What did Aaron tell them to give him? gold earrings

Primary: What did he make for them? calf

Primary: What did Moses have in his hands when he came down from the mount? two tablets

Primary: What did he do when he saw the people worshiping the golden calf? broke them

Intermediate: What did he do with the calf? melted, ground, sprinkled on water

Junior: How did Aaron try to excuse himself? threw gold in fire, calf came out

Intermediate: What is pictured by the golden calf? love of worldly success and pleasure

Senior: What is the difference between (1) the first tables of stone, and (2) those hewn our later from the bottom of the mount? (1) high, spiritual form of law and (2) law adapted to state of worldly men
