
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the second of the three great kings before the division of the land? David

Older Children: What special talent did David have? musician and poet

Younger Children: What book of the Bible was written in part through David? Psalms

Older Children: What did David say about what he wrote? it was the Word of the Lord

Younger Children: What is a psalm? a song

Younger Children: How did the Jews use the Psalms? hymns

Younger Teens: What did the Lord tell His disciples about them? they were about Him

Older Children: Can you repeat the first Psalm? "Blessed is the man ... "

Younger Children: What does blessed mean? happy

Older Children: What three things are we told not to do if we want to be happy? walk with ungodly, stand with sinners, sit with scornful

Older Children: What are we told to do? delight in the law of the Lord

Younger Children: To what is a good man likened in the first Psalm? a tree

Younger Children: To what is an ungodly man likened? chaff

Younger Teens: What does the Lord do for us in the Psalms? expresses our spiritual states and needs

Older Teens: How does the first Psalm set the pattern for the whole book? If we wish to be happy we should delight in the law of the Lord, and meditate daily on the Word.
