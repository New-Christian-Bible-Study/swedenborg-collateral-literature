
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: After men were created, where were they placed? Eden

Intermediate: Why were they not satisfied? wanted independence

Intermediate: How did the Lord meet their new need? showed them how to use self-hood constructively

Primary: Why couldn't men always live in the beautiful Garden of Eden? disobeyed

Junior: Why did they become worse as they went on? one bad deed led to another

Junior: What finally happened to most of them? destroyed by own evil

Primary: Who was saved? Noah

Primary: How was Noah saved? ark

Primary: How long did the rain last? forty days

Primary: Where was the window which Noah opened when the rain stopped? top

Primary: What two birds did he send out to see if the waters had gone down? raven, dove

Primary: What did the raven do? nothing

Primary: How many times did Noah send out the dove? three

Primary: What happened the first time? came back

Primary: What did the dove bring back the second time? olive leaf

Junior: What happened the third time? did not return

Junior: What was the first thing Noah did when he came out of the ark? built altar

Junior: What did the Lord promise? no more floods

Intermediate. What is meant by the flood? false ideas

Intermediate: What are meant by the raven and the dove? false and true thoughts

Senior: To what does the olive leaf correspond? knowledge of how to serve Lord

Senior: What change did the Lord make in man's constitution at the beginning of the Ancient Church? Separated will and understanding; people were no longer led by perception, but now were led by conscience

Junior: What does mythology call the civilization described by Noah? Silver Age

Junior: What does Swedenborg call it? Ancient Church
