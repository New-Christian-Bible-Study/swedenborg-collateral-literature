
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): After men were created, where were they placed? +
*A:* Eden

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why were they not satisfied? +
*A:* wanted independence

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How did the Lord meet their new need? +
*A:* showed them how to use self-hood constructively

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why couldn't men always live in the beautiful Garden of Eden? +
*A:* disobeyed

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did they become worse as they went on? +
*A:* one bad deed led to another

*Q:* (Older Children): What finally happened to most of them? +
*A:* destroyed by own evil

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was saved? +
*A:* Noah

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was Noah saved? +
*A:* ark

*Q:* (Younger Children): How long did the rain last? +
*A:* forty days

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was the window which Noah opened when the rain stopped? +
*A:* top

*Q:* (Younger Children): What two birds did he send out to see if the waters had gone down? +
*A:* raven, dove

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the raven do? +
*A:* nothing

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many times did Noah send out the dove? +
*A:* three

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened the first time? +
*A:* came back

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the dove bring back the second time? +
*A:* olive leaf

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened the third time? +
*A:* did not return

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the first thing Noah did when he came out of the ark? +
*A:* built altar

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord promise? +
*A:* no more floods

Younger Teens. What is meant by the flood? false ideas

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What are meant by the raven and the dove? +
*A:* false and true thoughts

*Q:* (Older Teens): To what does the olive leaf correspond? +
*A:* knowledge of how to serve Lord

*Q:* (Older Teens): What change did the Lord make in man's constitution at the beginning of the Ancient Church? +
*A:* Separated will and understanding; people were no longer led by perception, but now were led by conscience

*Q:* (Older Children): What does mythology call the civilization described by Noah? +
*A:* Silver Age

*Q:* (Older Children): What does Swedenborg call it? +
*A:* Ancient Church
