
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Whom did the Lord meet at Jericho on His way up to Jerusalem? Zacchaeus

Older Children: Can you tell the story of Zacchaeus? (see Luke 19)

Older Children: Why did the Pharisees object to the Lord's going to the house of Zacchaeus? he was a hated tax collector

Older Children: What parable did the Lord tell them? pounds

Younger Teens: What does this parable teach? do our best with what abilities we have

Younger Children: How did the Lord enter Jerusalem on Palm Sunday? riding a donkey

Older Children: Why did He ride on a donkey? kings and judges rode them

Older Children: How did the people receive Him? as a king

Younger Children: Where does Palm Sunday get its name? branches spread on road

Older Children: What did the Lord do in the temple after He entered the city? cleansed it

Older Children: Where did He lodge during the week? Bethany

Older Children: What did He do on Thursday evening? ate Passover

Older Children: What new feast did He institute? Holy Supper

Older Children: Who betrayed the Lord? Judas Iscariot

Older Children: How was the Lord put to death? crucified

Younger Children: On what day did the crucifixion take place? Friday

Younger Children: What was done with the Lord's body? put in new stone tomb

Younger Children: Who came to the tomb on Sunday morning? women

Younger Children: What did they bring? spices

Younger Children: What did they find? stone rolled away

Younger Children: What did the angels tell them? He is risen

Older Children: What saying of Jesus did they remind them of? that He would rise the third day

Older Children: How did the apostles receive the news? did not believe

Older Children: What did Peter do? ran and looked

Older Teens: What does the resurrection teach us? the Lord has power over death
