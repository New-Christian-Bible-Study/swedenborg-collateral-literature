
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whom did the Lord meet at Jericho on His way up to Jerusalem? +
*A:* Zacchaeus

*Q:* (Older Children): Can you tell the story of Zacchaeus? +
*A:* (see Luke 19)

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the Pharisees object to the Lord's going to the house of Zacchaeus? +
*A:* he was a hated tax collector

*Q:* (Older Children): What parable did the Lord tell them? +
*A:* pounds

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does this parable teach? +
*A:* do our best with what abilities we have

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the Lord enter Jerusalem on Palm Sunday? +
*A:* riding a donkey

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did He ride on a donkey? +
*A:* kings and judges rode them

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the people receive Him? +
*A:* as a king

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where does Palm Sunday get its name? +
*A:* branches spread on road

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord do in the temple after He entered the city? +
*A:* cleansed it

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did He lodge during the week? +
*A:* Bethany

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He do on Thursday evening? +
*A:* ate Passover

*Q:* (Older Children): What new feast did He institute? +
*A:* Holy Supper

*Q:* (Older Children): Who betrayed the Lord? +
*A:* Judas Iscariot

*Q:* (Older Children): How was the Lord put to death? +
*A:* crucified

*Q:* (Younger Children): On what day did the crucifixion take place? +
*A:* Friday

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was done with the Lord's body? +
*A:* put in new stone tomb

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who came to the tomb on Sunday morning? +
*A:* women

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they bring? +
*A:* spices

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they find? +
*A:* stone rolled away

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the angels tell them? +
*A:* He is risen

*Q:* (Older Children): What saying of Jesus did they remind them of? +
*A:* that He would rise the third day

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the apostles receive the news? +
*A:* did not believe

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Peter do? +
*A:* ran and looked

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the resurrection teach us? +
*A:* the Lord has power over death
