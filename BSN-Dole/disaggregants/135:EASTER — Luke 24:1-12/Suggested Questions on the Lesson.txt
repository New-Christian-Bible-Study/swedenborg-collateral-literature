
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Whom did the Lord meet at Jericho on His way up to Jerusalem? Zacchaeus

Junior: Can you tell the story of Zacchaeus? (see Luke 19)

Junior: Why did the Pharisees object to the Lord's going to the house of Zacchaeus? he was a hated tax collector

Junior: What parable did the Lord tell them? pounds

Intermediate: What does this parable teach? do our best with what abilities we have

Primary: How did the Lord enter Jerusalem on Palm Sunday? riding a donkey

Junior: Why did He ride on a donkey? kings and judges rode them

Junior: How did the people receive Him? as a king

Primary: Where does Palm Sunday get its name? branches spread on road

Junior: What did the Lord do in the temple after He entered the city? cleansed it

Junior: Where did He lodge during the week? Bethany

Junior: What did He do on Thursday evening? ate Passover

Junior: What new feast did He institute? Holy Supper

Junior: Who betrayed the Lord? Judas Iscariot

Junior: How was the Lord put to death? crucified

Primary: On what day did the crucifixion take place? Friday

Primary: What was done with the Lord's body? put in new stone tomb

Primary: Who came to the tomb on Sunday morning? women

Primary: What did they bring? spices

Primary: What did they find? stone rolled away

Primary: What did the angels tell them? He is risen

Junior: What saying of Jesus did they remind them of? that He would rise the third day

Junior: How did the apostles receive the news? did not believe

Junior: What did Peter do? ran and looked

Senior: What does the resurrection teach us? the Lord has power over death
