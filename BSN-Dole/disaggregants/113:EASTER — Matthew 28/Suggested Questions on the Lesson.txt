
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What day is today? Easter Sunday

Younger Children: Why do we observe Easter? to remember Christ's resurrection

Older Children: Why did the people turn against the Lord so soon after they welcomed Him as king? they listened to the jealous religious leaders

Older Children: Why did the Lord come into the city again on Thursday evening? to eat the Passover

Older Children: On what day of the week was the Lord crucified? Friday

Older Children: What do we call that day in Holy Week? Good Friday ["Good" in the sense of, "a day or season observed as holy by the church." -OED]

Older Children: What was done with the Lord's body after the crucifixion? laid in a new tomb

Younger Children: Who came to the sepulchre on the first Easter morning? the women

Younger Children: What did they find? open tomb

Younger Children: What did the angel tell them? He is risen

Older Children: Where did the angel tell them the Lord would meet the disciples? Galilee

Younger Children: As they went away, who met them? Jesus

Older Children: How did the religious leaders try to discredit the fact of the resurrection? bribed guards to lie

Younger Children: What does the Lord's resurrection teach us? after death we will live again

Older Children: When Jesus met the disciples in Galilee, what did He first say about Himself? "All power is given unto me ... "

Older Children: What did He tell them to do? make disciples, baptize

Older Children: What was His final encouragement to them? "I am with you always"

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord tell the disciples to meet Him on a mountain in Galilee? Galilee = outer, everyday life, mountain = high state of thinking

Older Teens: Why were the women the first to learn of the resurrection? our affections for truth persist even when external evidence would seem to demand doubt
