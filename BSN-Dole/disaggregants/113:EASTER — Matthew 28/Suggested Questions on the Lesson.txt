
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What day is today? +
*A:* Easter Sunday

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why do we observe Easter? +
*A:* to remember Christ's resurrection

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the people turn against the Lord so soon after they welcomed Him as king? +
*A:* they listened to the jealous religious leaders

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the Lord come into the city again on Thursday evening? +
*A:* to eat the Passover

*Q:* (Older Children): On what day of the week was the Lord crucified? +
*A:* Friday

*Q:* (Older Children): What do we call that day in Holy Week? +
*A:* Good Friday ["Good" in the sense of, "a day or season observed as holy by the church." -OED]

*Q:* (Older Children): What was done with the Lord's body after the crucifixion? +
*A:* laid in a new tomb

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who came to the sepulchre on the first Easter morning? +
*A:* the women

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they find? +
*A:* open tomb

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the angel tell them? +
*A:* He is risen

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the angel tell them the Lord would meet the disciples? +
*A:* Galilee

*Q:* (Younger Children): As they went away, who met them? +
*A:* Jesus

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the religious leaders try to discredit the fact of the resurrection? +
*A:* bribed guards to lie

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does the Lord's resurrection teach us? +
*A:* after death we will live again

*Q:* (Older Children): When Jesus met the disciples in Galilee, what did He first say about Himself? +
*A:* "All power is given unto me ... "

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell them to do? +
*A:* make disciples, baptize

*Q:* (Older Children): What was His final encouragement to them? +
*A:* "I am with you always"

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did the Lord tell the disciples to meet Him on a mountain in Galilee? +
*A:* Galilee = outer, everyday life, mountain = high state of thinking

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why were the women the first to learn of the resurrection? +
*A:* our affections for truth persist even when external evidence would seem to demand doubt
