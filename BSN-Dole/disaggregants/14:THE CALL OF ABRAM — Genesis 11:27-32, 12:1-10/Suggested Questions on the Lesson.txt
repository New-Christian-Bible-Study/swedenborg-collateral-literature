
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Teens: Are the first eleven chapters of the Bible historical fact? no

Younger Teens: Who was the first real person mentioned in the Bible? Eber

Younger Teens: Who was chosen by the Lord to begin the establishment of a new church on earth? Abram

Younger Teens: Who was Abram's father? Who were his brothers? Terah; Haran, Nahor

Older Children: Who was his wife? Sarai

Younger Children: Where did they live? Ur, Chaldea

Younger Children: Where did the Lord tell them to go? Canaan

Younger Teens: Where did they stop first? Haran (city of Nahor)

Younger Children: Who went on from Haran? Who was Lot? Lot, Abram's nephew

Older Children: What did the Lord promise Abram? to be father of nation

Younger Teens: Where did he stop first in the land of Canaan? Shechem (Sichem)

Younger Teens: What was his second stop? near Bethel

Younger Children: What did he build in both places? altar

Younger Teens: Where did he then go, and why? Egypt, famine

Younger Teens: What does the land of Canaan represent? heavenly way of life

Younger Teens: What does Abram's journey represent? leave inherited selfish tendencies ...

Older Teens: What does going into Egypt represent? gaining needed truth from the Word

Older Teens: What does this teach us about our own lives? we will repeatedly need to learn more truth
