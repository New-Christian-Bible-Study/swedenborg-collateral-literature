
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What story teaches us about the people of the first church on earth? +
*A:* Eden

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What was that church called? +
*A:* Most Ancient

*Q:* (Older Children): How did evil come into the world? +
*A:* man's disobedience

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What story tells about the saving of the good remnant? +
*A:* flood

*Q:* (Younger Children): How long did the flood last? +
*A:* almost a year (cf. 7:11, 8:13)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the first thing Noah did when he came out of the ark? +
*A:* built altar

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord promise? +
*A:* no more floods

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the sign or token of this covenant? +
*A:* rainbow

*Q:* (Older Children): How is a rainbow formed? +
*A:* sunlight on drops of water

*Q:* (Older Children): When do we usually see one? +
*A:* end of storm

Younger Teens. To what does water correspond? truth

*A:* knowledges in mind
