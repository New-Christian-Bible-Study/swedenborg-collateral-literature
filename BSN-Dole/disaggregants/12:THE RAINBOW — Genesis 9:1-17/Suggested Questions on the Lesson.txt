
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Teens: What story teaches us about the people of the first church on earth? Eden

Younger Teens: What was that church called? Most Ancient

Older Children: How did evil come into the world? man's disobedience

Younger Teens: What story tells about the saving of the good remnant? flood

Younger Children: How long did the flood last? almost a year (cf. 7:11, 8:13)

Younger Children: What was the first thing Noah did when he came out of the ark? built altar

Older Children: What did the Lord promise? no more floods

Younger Children: What was the sign or token of this covenant? rainbow

Older Children: How is a rainbow formed? sunlight on drops of water

Older Children: When do we usually see one? end of storm

Intermediate. To what does water correspond? truth

Older Teens: To what do the drops of water in the cloud correspond? knowledges in mind

Younger Teens: How did the Lord tell the Most Ancient people what was right? perception

Intermediate. How does He tell us? conscience
