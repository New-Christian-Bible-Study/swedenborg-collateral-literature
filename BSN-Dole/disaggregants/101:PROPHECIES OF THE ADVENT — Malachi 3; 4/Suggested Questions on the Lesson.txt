
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: How many Minor Prophets are there? twelve

Older Children: When did the last three prophesy? after return from Babylon

Younger Children: Who was the last one of all? Malachi

Younger Children: What does Malachi mean? my messenger

Older Children: What bad things were the people doing? disobeying the law of the tithe

Younger Children: What great event did Malachi foretell? coming of the Lord

Younger Children: What did he say it would be like? the sun of righteousness

Older Children: How did he say it would affect the wicked? would tread them down

Older Children: How would it affect the good? bring them healing

Younger Children: Who would be sent to prepare the way of the Lord? "Elijah"

Younger Teens: Why is the Lord's coming likened to the sunrise? brings warmth and light

Older Teens: Why were no prophets sent after Malachi until John the Baptist? church fully perverted

Older Teens: Why would the people need to be prepared by someone for the Lord's coming? the full effect of His truth coming suddenly would have driven people to self-destruction
