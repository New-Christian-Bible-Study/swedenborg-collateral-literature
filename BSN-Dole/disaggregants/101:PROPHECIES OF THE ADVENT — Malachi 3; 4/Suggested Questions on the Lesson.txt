
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: How many Minor Prophets are there? twelve

Junior: When did the last three prophesy? after return from Babylon

Primary: Who was the last one of all? Malachi

Primary: What does Malachi mean? my messenger

Junior: What bad things were the people doing? disobeying the law of the tithe

Primary: What great event did Malachi foretell? coming of the Lord

Primary: What did he say it would be like? the sun of righteousness

Junior: How did he say it would affect the wicked? would tread them down

Junior: How would it affect the good? bring them healing

Primary: Who would be sent to prepare the way of the Lord? "Elijah"

Intermediate: Why is the Lord's coming likened to the sunrise? brings warmth and light

Senior: Why were no prophets sent after Malachi until John the Baptist? church fully perverted

Senior: Why would the people need to be prepared by someone for the Lord's coming? the full effect of His truth coming suddenly would have driven people to self-destruction
