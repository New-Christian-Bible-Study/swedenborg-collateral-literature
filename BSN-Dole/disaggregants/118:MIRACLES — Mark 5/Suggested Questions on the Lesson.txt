
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What is a miracle? a wonderful act of the Lord's to teach us a lesson

Junior: What miracles can you remember that we have studied in Mark? palsied man, man with withered hand

Primary: How many miracles are described in our lesson for today? three

Junior: Where was the first one done? across the Jordan

Junior: What was the matter with the man? possessed by demons

Junior: How did he show his faith in the Lord? ran and worshiped Him

Junior: What did the devil say his name was? Legion (many)

Intermediate: What does this mean? no temptation ever comes alone

Primary: What favor did the devil ask? to go into herd of swine

Primary: What happened to the swine? ran down deep bank into sea and drowned

Junior: After the Lord went back to Galilee, who came to Him for help? Jairus

Primary: What did Jairus want? healing of his daughter who was near death

Primary: What did the messengers from his home tell him? she is dead

Junior: What miracle was performed on the way to the home of Jairus? woman healed

Junior: How was the woman healed? touched Jesus' garment

Primary: At the home of Jairus what did the Lord tell the mourners? she is sleeping

Primary: How did He heal the child? took hand, said, "Arise!"

Primary: What did He tell her parents to do for her? give her something to eat

Senior: Why did the Lord perform miracles? to teach us what he can do for our souls
