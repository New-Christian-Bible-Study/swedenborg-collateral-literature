
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What is a miracle? a wonderful act of the Lord's to teach us a lesson

Older Children: What miracles can you remember that we have studied in Mark? palsied man, man with withered hand

Younger Children: How many miracles are described in our lesson for today? three

Older Children: Where was the first one done? across the Jordan

Older Children: What was the matter with the man? possessed by demons

Older Children: How did he show his faith in the Lord? ran and worshiped Him

Older Children: What did the devil say his name was? Legion (many)

Younger Teens: What does this mean? no temptation ever comes alone

Younger Children: What favor did the devil ask? to go into herd of swine

Younger Children: What happened to the swine? ran down deep bank into sea and drowned

Older Children: After the Lord went back to Galilee, who came to Him for help? Jairus

Younger Children: What did Jairus want? healing of his daughter who was near death

Younger Children: What did the messengers from his home tell him? she is dead

Older Children: What miracle was performed on the way to the home of Jairus? woman healed

Older Children: How was the woman healed? touched Jesus' garment

Younger Children: At the home of Jairus what did the Lord tell the mourners? she is sleeping

Younger Children: How did He heal the child? took hand, said, "Arise!"

Younger Children: What did He tell her parents to do for her? give her something to eat

Older Teens: Why did the Lord perform miracles? to teach us what he can do for our souls
