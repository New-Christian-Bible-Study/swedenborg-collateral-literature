
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Whom did Isaac marry? Rebekah

Younger Children: Where did she come from? Haran

Younger Children: What two sons did Isaac and Rebekah have? Esau and Jacob

Younger Children: Which was the firstborn? Esau

Younger Children: How were they different? Esau hairy and red; Jacob smooth

Younger Children: What did Esau sell to Jacob? birthright

Younger Children: What did he get for it? "mess of pottage"

Younger Children: Which son was Isaac's favorite? Esau

Younger Children: Which was Rebekah's favorite? Jacob

Older Children: When Isaac was old, how did Rebekah help Jacob to deceive him? prepared meat, put goat's hair on Jacob

Younger Children: What did Isaac give Jacob that Esau should have had? blessing of firstborn

Younger Children: How did Esau feel about it? wanted to kill Jacob

Younger Teens: What do Esau and Jacob represent? natural will, natural understanding

Older Teens: Why did the Lord permit Jacob to supplant Esau? to show that we must not be led by selfish will
