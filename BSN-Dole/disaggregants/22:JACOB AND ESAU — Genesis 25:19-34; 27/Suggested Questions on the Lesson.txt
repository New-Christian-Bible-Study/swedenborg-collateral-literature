
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Whom did Isaac marry? Rebekah

Primary: Where did she come from? Haran

Primary: What two sons did Isaac and Rebekah have? Esau and Jacob

Primary: Which was the firstborn? Esau

Primary: How were they different? Esau hairy and red; Jacob smooth

Primary: What did Esau sell to Jacob? birthright

Primary: What did he get for it? "mess of pottage"

Primary: Which son was Isaac's favorite? Esau

Primary: Which was Rebekah's favorite? Jacob

Junior: When Isaac was old, how did Rebekah help Jacob to deceive him? prepared meat, put goat's hair on Jacob

Primary: What did Isaac give Jacob that Esau should have had? blessing of firstborn

Primary: How did Esau feel about it? wanted to kill Jacob

Intermediate: What do Esau and Jacob represent? natural will, natural understanding

Senior: Why did the Lord permit Jacob to supplant Esau? to show that we must not be led by selfish will
