
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What prophecy did John the Baptist fulfill? +
*A:* Malachi's regarding Elijah

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were his father and mother? +
*A:* Zacharias and Elisabeth

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who announced his coming? +
*A:* the angel Gabriel

*Q:* (Younger Children): What sign did the angel give Zacharias? +
*A:* that he be dumb till John's birth

*Q:* (Younger Children): What other announcement did the same angel make? +
*A:* that the Lord would be born

*Q:* (Younger Children): To whom was it made? +
*A:* Mary

*Q:* (Older Children): What two familiar selections do we find in the first chapter of Luke? +
*A:* Benedictus, Magnificat

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What was the message of John the Baptist? +
*A:* Repent!

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he do for the people who believed him? +
*A:* baptized them

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why was the Lord baptized? +
*A:* to show us what we ought to do

*Q:* (Older Children): What is the first miracle described in our lesson for today? +
*A:* healing centurion's slave

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is the second? +
*A:* raising the widow's son

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why could the Lord bring the dead man back to life? +
*A:* He has power over life

*Q:* (Older Children): What did John want to ask the Lord? +
*A:* "Are you he who is to come?"

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell John's messengers? +
*A:* tell him what you have seen and heard

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did He tell the people John was? +
*A:* a prophet

*Q:* (Older Teens): Who did He say was greater than John? +
*A:* he who is least in the kingdom of God
