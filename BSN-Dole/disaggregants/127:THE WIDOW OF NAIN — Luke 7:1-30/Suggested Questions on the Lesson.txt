
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What prophecy did John the Baptist fulfill? Malachi's regarding Elijah

Older Children: Who were his father and mother? Zacharias and Elisabeth

Younger Children: Who announced his coming? the angel Gabriel

Younger Children: What sign did the angel give Zacharias? that he be dumb till John's birth

Younger Children: What other announcement did the same angel make? that the Lord would be born

Younger Children: To whom was it made? Mary

Older Children: What two familiar selections do we find in the first chapter of Luke? Benedictus, Magnificat

Younger Teens: What was the message of John the Baptist? Repent!

Older Children: What did he do for the people who believed him? baptized them

Younger Children: Why was the Lord baptized? to show us what we ought to do

Older Children: What is the first miracle described in our lesson for today? healing centurion's slave

Younger Children: What is the second? raising the widow's son

Older Teens: Why could the Lord bring the dead man back to life? He has power over life

Older Children: What did John want to ask the Lord? "Are you he who is to come?"

Older Children: What did the Lord tell John's messengers? tell him what you have seen and heard

Younger Teens: What did He tell the people John was? a prophet

Older Teens: Who did He say was greater than John? he who is least in the kingdom of God
