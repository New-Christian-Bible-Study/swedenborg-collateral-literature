
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What two men is our lesson about today? +
*A:* Abram, Lot

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Abram and Lot come from? +
*A:* Ur, Chaldea

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did they leave Ur? +
*A:* the Lord told them to

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Abram do at the first two places where he stopped in Canaan? +
*A:* built altars

*Q:* (Older Children): To what other country did he and Lot go before they settled in Canaan? +
*A:* Egypt

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened in Egypt? +
*A:* they became rich

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did they go first when they came back? +
*A:* Bethel

*Q:* (Older Children): What difficulty came up between them? +
*A:* herdsmen quarreled

*Q:* (Older Children): What offer did Abram make to Lot? +
*A:* choice of land

*Q:* (Younger Children): What part of the country did Lot choose? +
*A:* valley

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did he choose it? +
*A:* good pasture

*Q:* (Older Children): What danger was there which he did not think about? +
*A:* Sodom

*Q:* (Older Children): Into what trouble did he fall? +
*A:* captured in war

*Q:* (Older Children): How was he saved? +
*A:* Abram

*Q:* (Younger Teens): In our lives what does Lot's choice picture? +
*A:* doing whatever pleases us at the moment

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What are meant by the cities of Sodom and Gomorrah? +
*A:* selfishness, worldly thinking

*Q:* (Older Teens): Does this mean that we should avoid all the pleasures of the senses? +
*A:* no, but should be kept in proper perspective

*Q:* (Older Teens): How can we tell what pleasures are innocent? +
*A:* not harmful, and in harmony with goals of spiritual life
