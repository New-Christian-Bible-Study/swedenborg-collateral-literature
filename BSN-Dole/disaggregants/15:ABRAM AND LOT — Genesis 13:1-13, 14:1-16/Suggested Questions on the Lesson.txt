
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What two men is our lesson about today? Abram, Lot

Older Children: Where did Abram and Lot come from? Ur, Chaldea

Older Children: Why did they leave Ur? the Lord told them to

Younger Children: What did Abram do at the first two places where he stopped in Canaan? built altars

Older Children: To what other country did he and Lot go before they settled in Canaan? Egypt

Younger Children: What happened in Egypt? they became rich

Older Children: Where did they go first when they came back? Bethel

Older Children: What difficulty came up between them? herdsmen quarreled

Older Children: What offer did Abram make to Lot? choice of land

Younger Children: What part of the country did Lot choose? valley

Older Children: Why did he choose it? good pasture

Older Children: What danger was there which he did not think about? Sodom

Older Children: Into what trouble did he fall? captured in war

Older Children: How was he saved? Abram

Younger Teens: In our lives what does Lot's choice picture? doing whatever pleases us at the moment

Younger Teens: What are meant by the cities of Sodom and Gomorrah? selfishness, worldly thinking

Older Teens: Does this mean that we should avoid all the pleasures of the senses? no, but should be kept in proper perspective

Older Teens: How can we tell what pleasures are innocent? not harmful, and in harmony with goals of spiritual life
