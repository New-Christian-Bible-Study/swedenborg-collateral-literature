
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What two men is our lesson about today? Abram, Lot

Junior: Where did Abram and Lot come from? Ur, Chaldea

Junior: Why did they leave Ur? the Lord told them to

Primary: What did Abram do at the first two places where he stopped in Canaan? built altars

Junior: To what other country did he and Lot go before they settled in Canaan? Egypt

Primary: What happened in Egypt? they became rich

Junior: Where did they go first when they came back? Bethel

Junior: What difficulty came up between them? herdsmen quarreled

Junior: What offer did Abram make to Lot? choice of land

Primary: What part of the country did Lot choose? valley

Junior: Why did he choose it? good pasture

Junior: What danger was there which he did not think about? Sodom

Junior: Into what trouble did he fall? captured in war

Junior: How was he saved? Abram

Intermediate: In our lives what does Lot's choice picture? doing whatever pleases us at the moment

Intermediate: What are meant by the cities of Sodom and Gomorrah? selfishness, worldly thinking

Senior: Does this mean that we should avoid all the pleasures of the senses? no, but should be kept in proper perspective

Senior: How can we tell what pleasures are innocent? not harmful, and in harmony with goals of spiritual life
