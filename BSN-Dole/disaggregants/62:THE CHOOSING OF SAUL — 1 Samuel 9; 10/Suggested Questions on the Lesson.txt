
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why, when Samuel was old, did the Israelites ask for a king? +
*A:* his sons were evil

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How did Samuel feel about it? +
*A:* rejected

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord tell him? +
*A:* they were rejecting Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the first king the Lord chose? +
*A:* Saul

*Q:* (Older Children): What was Saul doing when he first met Samuel? +
*A:* seeking lost donkeys

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Samuel prove to him that the Lord had chosen him? +
*A:* anointed him (see also 1 Samuel 10:2-6)

*Q:* (Older Children): What does anointing mean? +
*A:* pouring oil on head

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the Lord call the people together? +
*A:* Mizpah

*Q:* (Older Children): How was Saul chosen there? +
*A:* by casting lots

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who really governed the choice by lot? +
*A:* the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why were the people pleased to have Saul for their king? +
*A:* tall and strong

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does a king represent? +
*A:* truth ruling
