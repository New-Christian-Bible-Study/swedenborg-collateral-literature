
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Teens: Why, when Samuel was old, did the Israelites ask for a king? his sons were evil

Younger Teens: How did Samuel feel about it? rejected

Younger Teens: What did the Lord tell him? they were rejecting Him

Younger Children: Who was the first king the Lord chose? Saul

Older Children: What was Saul doing when he first met Samuel? seeking lost donkeys

Older Children: How did Samuel prove to him that the Lord had chosen him? anointed him (see also 1 Samuel 10:2-6)

Older Children: What does anointing mean? pouring oil on head

Older Children: Where did the Lord call the people together? Mizpah

Older Children: How was Saul chosen there? by casting lots

Younger Children: Who really governed the choice by lot? the Lord

Younger Children: Why were the people pleased to have Saul for their king? tall and strong

Younger Teens: What does a king represent? truth ruling
