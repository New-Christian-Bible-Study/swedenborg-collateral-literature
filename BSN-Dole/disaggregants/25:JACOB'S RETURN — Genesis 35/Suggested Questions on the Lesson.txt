
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Why did Jacob leave home and go to Haran? afraid of brother

Younger Children: How long did he stay there? twenty years

Younger Children: What two wives did he marry? Leah, Rachel

Younger Children: How many sons were born to him in Haran? eleven

Younger Children: Who was the youngest of these? Joseph

Younger Children: Where was his twelfth son born? near Bethlehem

Younger Children: What was his name? Benjamin

Older Children: Why did Jacob go to Bethel when he returned from Haran? to worship God

Older Children: Before he reached Bethel at what place did he stop? Shechem

Older Children: What did he make the people do at Shechem? give up gods, earrings

Older Children: Who was buried at Bethel? Deborah

Younger Children: What did Jacob do at Bethel? built stone altar

Older Children: What did the Lord promise him there? to father great nation

Older Children: Where did Jacob find Isaac? Hebron (Mamre)

Older Children: Where did Jacob and Esau bury Isaac? cave of Machpelah

Younger Teens: What does Rebekah's nurse represent? hereditary selfishness

Older Teens: Why is her death and burial mentioned at this point in the story of Jacob? when we return voluntarily to spiritual living we must get rid of selfish motivation
