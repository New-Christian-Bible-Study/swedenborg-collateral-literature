
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did Jacob leave home and go to Haran? +
*A:* afraid of brother

*Q:* (Younger Children): How long did he stay there? +
*A:* twenty years

*Q:* (Younger Children): What two wives did he marry? +
*A:* Leah, Rachel

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many sons were born to him in Haran? +
*A:* eleven

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the youngest of these? +
*A:* Joseph

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was his twelfth son born? +
*A:* near Bethlehem

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was his name? +
*A:* Benjamin

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did Jacob go to Bethel when he returned from Haran? +
*A:* to worship God

*Q:* (Older Children): Before he reached Bethel at what place did he stop? +
*A:* Shechem

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he make the people do at Shechem? +
*A:* give up gods, earrings

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was buried at Bethel? +
*A:* Deborah

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Jacob do at Bethel? +
*A:* built stone altar

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord promise him there? +
*A:* to father great nation

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Jacob find Isaac? +
*A:* Hebron (Mamre)

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did Jacob and Esau bury Isaac? +
*A:* cave of Machpelah

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Rebekah's nurse represent? +
*A:* hereditary selfishness

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why is her death and burial mentioned at this point in the story of Jacob? +
*A:* when we return voluntarily to spiritual living we must get rid of selfish motivation
