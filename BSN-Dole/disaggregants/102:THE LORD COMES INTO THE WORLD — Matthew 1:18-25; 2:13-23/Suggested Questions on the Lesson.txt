
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Into what two main parts is the Bible divided? +
*A:* Old, New Testament

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which part have we been studying? +
*A:* Old Testament

*Q:* (Older Children): With the history of what nation is the old Testament in its letter principally concerned? +
*A:* the Hebrew nation

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many tribes of the Israelites were there? +
*A:* twelve

*Q:* (Older Children): In what two divisions of the land were they finally settled? +
*A:* Israel, Judah

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to the people of the division called Israel? +
*A:* exiled by Assyria

*Q:* (Older Children): What nation took the people of Judah captive? +
*A:* Babylon

*Q:* (Older Children): How long were they held in Babylon? +
*A:* seventy years

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why was it necessary that some of them come back to the Holy Land? +

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they rebuild? +
*A:* the temple

*Q:* (Older Children): Into what three parts is the Holy Land divided in the time of the New Testament? +
*A:* Galilee, Samaria, Judea

*Q:* (Older Children): With what great event does the New Testament story begin? +
*A:* birth of the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was the Lord Jesus born? +
*A:* Bethlehem

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was His mother? +
*A:* Mary

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was His father? +
*A:* God

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the name of Mary's husband? +
*A:* Joseph

*Q:* (Younger Children): In Joseph's dream, what did the Lord tell him the baby would be called? +
*A:* Jesus

*Q:* (Older Children): who was king in Judea at this time? +
*A:* Herod

*Q:* (Younger Children): After the baby was born, what did God tell Joseph in a dream? +
*A:* flee to Egypt

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did Herod want to destroy the Lord? +
*A:* fear for his throne

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he try to do it? +
*A:* killed boy babies at Bethlehem

*Q:* (Older Children): When was Joseph told it was safe to bring Jesus back? +
*A:* after Herod's death

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did they go to live? +
*A:* Nazareth

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by the Lord's going down into Egypt? +
*A:* his learning of Scripture

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did the Lord have to be born in the Holy Land and of a Jewish mother? +
*A:* to be in a true correspondential setting and in order to take on the appropriate maternal heredity
