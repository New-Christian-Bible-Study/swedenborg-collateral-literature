
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What book do we study in Sunday School? +
*A:* Bible

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who is its Author? +
*A:* God

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is the first book in the Bible? +
*A:* Genesis

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does Genesis mean? +
*A:* the beginning

*Q:* (Younger Children): Can you repeat the first verse of the Bible? +
*A:* In the beginning ...

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was given to the world on the first day of creation? +
*A:* light

*Q:* (Older Children): In how many days was creation finished? +
*A:* six

*Q:* (Older Children): Does day mean our day of twenty-four hours? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the Lord's final creation? +
*A:* man

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How is man different from the animals? +
*A:* image of God

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What dominion or power was given to man? +
*A:* all earth

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord say of creation when He had finished it? +
*A:* it was good

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did He do on the seventh day? +
*A:* rested

*Q:* (Older Children): How did He set the seventh day apart from other days? +
*A:* blessed and sanctified it

*Q:* (Older Children): What does blessed mean? +
*A:* made happy

*Q:* (Older Children): What does sanctified or hallowed mean? +
*A:* holy

*Q:* (Older Children): When the Lord was on earth, what did He say it is lawful to do on the sabbath? +
*A:* good

*Q:* (Older Children): What things did He Himself do on the sabbath? +
*A:* worshiped, taught, healed, ate with friends

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do we mean by (1) celestial, and (2) spiritual states? +
*A:* (1) doing right spontaneously from love (2) doing right according to truth

*Q:* (Older Teens): Spiritually, what is the rest of the Sabbath Day? +
*A:* rest from struggle with temptation
