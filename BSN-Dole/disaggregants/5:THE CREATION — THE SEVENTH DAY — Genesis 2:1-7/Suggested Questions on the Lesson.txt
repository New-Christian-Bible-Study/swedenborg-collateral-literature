
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What book do we study in Sunday School? Bible

Primary: Who is its Author? God

Primary: What is the first book in the Bible? Genesis

Primary: What does Genesis mean? the beginning

Primary: Can you repeat the first verse of the Bible? In the beginning ...

Primary: What was given to the world on the first day of creation? light

Junior: In how many days was creation finished? six

Junior: Does day mean our day of twenty-four hours? no

Junior: What was the Lord's final creation? man

Intermediate: How is man different from the animals? image of God

Intermediate: What dominion or power was given to man? all earth

Junior: What did the Lord say of creation when He had finished it? it was good

Primary: What did He do on the seventh day? rested

Junior: How did He set the seventh day apart from other days? blessed and sanctified it

Junior: What does blessed mean? made happy

Junior: What does sanctified or hallowed mean? holy

Junior: When the Lord was on earth, what did He say it is lawful to do on the sabbath? good

Junior: What things did He Himself do on the sabbath? worshiped, taught, healed, ate with friends

Intermediate: What do we mean by (1) celestial, and (2) spiritual states? (1) doing right spontaneously from love (2) doing right according to truth

Senior: Spiritually, what is the rest of the Sabbath Day? rest from struggle with temptation
