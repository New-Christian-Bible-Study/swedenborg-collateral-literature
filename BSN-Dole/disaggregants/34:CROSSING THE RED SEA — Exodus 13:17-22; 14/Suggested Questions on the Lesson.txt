
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Moses and Aaron finally persuade Pharaoh to let the Hebrews go? +
*A:* plagues

*Q:* (Older Children): What great feast did they celebrate on the eve of their departure? +
*A:* Passover

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Egyptians give them? +
*A:* jewels of gold and silver, clothing

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the Lord lead them? +
*A:* pillar of cloud/fire

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Pharaoh do after they left? +
*A:* pursued

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did he catch up with them? +
*A:* Red Sea

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord protect them through the night? +
*A:* pillar of cloud between

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did they cross the Red Sea in the morning? +
*A:* waters parted miraculously

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to the Egyptians when they tried to follow? +
*A:* drowned

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by the children of Israel's leaving Egypt? +
*A:* deciding to stop being merely worldly and to seek spiritual way of life

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the Red Sea represent? +
*A:* accumulation of falsity from evil

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by the destruction of the Egyptians? +
*A:* permanent removal of slavery to worldliness
