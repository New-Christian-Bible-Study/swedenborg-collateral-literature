
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How did Moses and Aaron finally persuade Pharaoh to let the Hebrews go? plagues

Older Children: What great feast did they celebrate on the eve of their departure? Passover

Younger Children: What did the Egyptians give them? jewels of gold and silver, clothing

Younger Children: How did the Lord lead them? pillar of cloud/fire

Older Children: What did Pharaoh do after they left? pursued

Younger Children: Where did he catch up with them? Red Sea

Older Children: How did the Lord protect them through the night? pillar of cloud between

Younger Children: How did they cross the Red Sea in the morning? waters parted miraculously

Younger Children: What happened to the Egyptians when they tried to follow? drowned

Older Teens: What is pictured by the children of Israel's leaving Egypt? deciding to stop being merely worldly and to seek spiritual way of life

Younger Teens: What does the Red Sea represent? accumulation of falsity from evil

Older Teens: What is pictured by the destruction of the Egyptians? permanent removal of slavery to worldliness
