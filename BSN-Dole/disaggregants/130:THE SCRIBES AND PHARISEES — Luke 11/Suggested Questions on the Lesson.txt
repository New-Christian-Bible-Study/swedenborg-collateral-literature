
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How does the form of the Lord's Prayer in Luke differ from that in Matthew? [see Older Children notes]

Older Children: Who were the scribes? men who copied the Scriptures

Older Children: Who were the Pharisees? a strict sect who took pride in knowing the law

Older Children: What law did the scribes study? Scriptures

Younger Children: Where did they say the Lord's power to work miracles came from? Beelzebub (devil)

Older Children: What did the Lord tell them? a house divided against itself falls

Older Children: What sign did the Lord say would be given to the Jews? that of Jonah

Older Children: To what two stories of the Old Testament did He refer? Jonah, queen of Sheba

Younger Children: What did the Lord say about a lamp? put it on a stand

Younger Children: What does this mean? it is foolish to light lamp and then hide it

Older Children: What is meant by the eye's being single? clear or pure

Older Children: What is meant by the eye's being evil? blinded by evil

Younger Children: With what great fault did the Lord charge the scribes and Pharisees? hypocrisy

Older Children: What is a hypocrite? one who only pretends to be good

Younger Teens: What were some of the ways in which the scribes and Pharisees were hypocrites? prayed to be seen of men, acted very pious, judged others

Older Teens: Why did the scribes and Pharisees not want to recognize the Lord as the Messiah? His presence and teachings threatened their power
