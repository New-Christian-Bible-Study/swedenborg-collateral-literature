
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: From what book of the Bible is our lesson for today? Judges

Junior: Who were the Judges? temporary local leaders

Junior: In what way were the Judges different from Moses and Joshua as leaders? not great leaders of entire people

Junior: What was the trouble with the people in the period of the Judges? forgot God

Primary: Which Judge is our lesson about today? Gideon

Junior: From what tribe did he come? Manasseh

Primary: What enemy was attacking Israel? Midian (also Amalek)

Junior: What was the first thing Gideon did after he was called? humbled himself

Junior: What assurance did he ask of the Lord? fleece

Junior: From what tribes did he gather his army? Asher, Zebulun, Naphtali, Manasseh

Primary: Why did the army have to be reduced in numbers? God to give victory

Primary: Who were first sent home? fearful

Primary: What was the second test? water drinking

Primary: How large was the army finally? three hundred

Primary: What did they have instead of weapons? lamps, pitchers, trumpets

Junior: How was the attack made? surrounded enemy, broke jars, shouted

Junior: What was the result? confusion, victory

Intermediate: What do the Judges represent? particular truths needed in time of trouble

Senior: What do you think are the important lessons taught in this story? trust the Lord, get rid of fearful thoughts, be prepared to face ridicule, ask the Lord what is right
