
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What mistake did the children of Israel make after Joseph's death? stayed in Egypt

Junior: Why did the Egyptians become afraid of them? multiplied

Primary: What did the Pharaohs do to them? made them slaves

Primary: Whom did the Lord raise up to lead them out of Egypt? Moses

Junior: What power did the Lord give Moses? to plague Egypt

Junior: What sign did he give him to use to prove his power? rod > serpent

Primary: Who was chosen to help him? Aaron

Primary: What did Moses ask of Pharaoh? let people go

Junior: How did the Lord tell them to try to convince him? threaten plagues

Primary: How many plagues were there? ten

Primary: What was the first plague? water > blood

Primary: What was the second? frogs

Junior: What was the last one? firstborn died

Junior: How many of the plagues could the magicians imitate? first two

Intermediate: What does Egypt represent in our lives? plane of worldly knowledge

Intermediate: What is pictured by the captivity of the Israelites in Egypt? our higher nature enslaved by worldliness

Senior: What in general is represented by the ten plagues? successive steps of degeneration of a stubbornly worldly person
