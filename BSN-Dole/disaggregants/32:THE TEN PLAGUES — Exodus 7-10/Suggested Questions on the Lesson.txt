
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What mistake did the children of Israel make after Joseph's death? +
*A:* stayed in Egypt

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the Egyptians become afraid of them? +
*A:* multiplied

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Pharaohs do to them? +
*A:* made them slaves

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whom did the Lord raise up to lead them out of Egypt? +
*A:* Moses

*Q:* (Older Children): What power did the Lord give Moses? +
*A:* to plague Egypt

*Q:* (Older Children): What sign did he give him to use to prove his power? +
*A:* rod > serpent

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was chosen to help him? +
*A:* Aaron

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Moses ask of Pharaoh? +
*A:* let people go

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord tell them to try to convince him? +
*A:* threaten plagues

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many plagues were there? +
*A:* ten

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the first plague? +
*A:* water > blood

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the second? +
*A:* frogs

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the last one? +
*A:* firstborn died

*Q:* (Older Children): How many of the plagues could the magicians imitate? +
*A:* first two

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Egypt represent in our lives? +
*A:* plane of worldly knowledge

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by the captivity of the Israelites in Egypt? +
*A:* our higher nature enslaved by worldliness

*Q:* (Older Teens): What in general is represented by the ten plagues? +
*A:* successive steps of degeneration of a stubbornly worldly person
