
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What mistake did the children of Israel make after Joseph's death? stayed in Egypt

Older Children: Why did the Egyptians become afraid of them? multiplied

Younger Children: What did the Pharaohs do to them? made them slaves

Younger Children: Whom did the Lord raise up to lead them out of Egypt? Moses

Older Children: What power did the Lord give Moses? to plague Egypt

Older Children: What sign did he give him to use to prove his power? rod > serpent

Younger Children: Who was chosen to help him? Aaron

Younger Children: What did Moses ask of Pharaoh? let people go

Older Children: How did the Lord tell them to try to convince him? threaten plagues

Younger Children: How many plagues were there? ten

Younger Children: What was the first plague? water > blood

Younger Children: What was the second? frogs

Older Children: What was the last one? firstborn died

Older Children: How many of the plagues could the magicians imitate? first two

Younger Teens: What does Egypt represent in our lives? plane of worldly knowledge

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the captivity of the Israelites in Egypt? our higher nature enslaved by worldliness

Older Teens: What in general is represented by the ten plagues? successive steps of degeneration of a stubbornly worldly person
