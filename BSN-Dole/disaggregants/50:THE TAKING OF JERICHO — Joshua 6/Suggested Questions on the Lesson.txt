
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What river separated the Israelites from the Holy Land? Jordan

Junior: How did they cross it? miracle

Junior: Where was their first camp in the Holy Land? Gilgal

Primary: What feast did they celebrate there? Passover

Junior: What change was made in their food? manna ceased

Primary: What was the first strong city they had to conquer? Jericho

Primary: What did the Lord tell them to carry around the city? the ark

Primary: How many priests were to go before the ark? seven

Junior: What were they to carry? ram's horn trumpets

Primary: How many times were they to march around the city each day for six days? once

Primary: How many times were they to march around it on the seventh day? seven

Primary: What happened when they finally blew the trumpets and shouted? walls fell

Junior: What did they save out of the city? Rahab and family, all metals

Junior: What did they do with everything else in it? destroyed it

Junior: What curse was pronounced against Jericho? rebuilder will lose two sons

Intermediate: What does entrance into the Holy Land represent? beginning regenerate life

Senior: What does Jericho represent? natural self-confidence

Senior: Why did the Lord overcome Jericho in this way? to show that such tendencies can only be conquered by the Lord's power
