
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What river separated the Israelites from the Holy Land? +
*A:* Jordan

*Q:* (Older Children): How did they cross it? +
*A:* miracle

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was their first camp in the Holy Land? +
*A:* Gilgal

*Q:* (Younger Children): What feast did they celebrate there? +
*A:* Passover

*Q:* (Older Children): What change was made in their food? +
*A:* manna ceased

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the first strong city they had to conquer? +
*A:* Jericho

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord tell them to carry around the city? +
*A:* the ark

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many priests were to go before the ark? +
*A:* seven

*Q:* (Older Children): What were they to carry? +
*A:* ram's horn trumpets

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many times were they to march around the city each day for six days? +
*A:* once

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many times were they to march around it on the seventh day? +
*A:* seven

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened when they finally blew the trumpets and shouted? +
*A:* walls fell

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they save out of the city? +
*A:* Rahab and family, all metals

*Q:* (Older Children): What did they do with everything else in it? +
*A:* destroyed it

*Q:* (Older Children): What curse was pronounced against Jericho? +
*A:* rebuilder will lose two sons

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does entrance into the Holy Land represent? +
*A:* beginning regenerate life

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does Jericho represent? +
*A:* natural self-confidence

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did the Lord overcome Jericho in this way? +
*A:* to show that such tendencies can only be conquered by the Lord's power
