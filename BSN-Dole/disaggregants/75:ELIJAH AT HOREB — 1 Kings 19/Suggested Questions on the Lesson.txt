
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Teens: What three buildings did Solomon erect? temple, two palaces

Younger Teens: What happened to the kingdom after Solomon died? divided

Younger Teens: What had Solomon done which brought this about? idolatry, heavy taxes

Younger Teens: What were the two divisions of the land called? Israel, Judah

Younger Teens: What do these divisions picture? division of one's heart and mind

Younger Teens: What worship was set up in Israel, and where? gold calves at Dan and Bethel

Younger Children: In our lesson for today, who is king of Israel? Ahab

Younger Children: What was his wife's name? Jezebel

Younger Children: Who was the Lord's prophet? Elijah

Younger Children: Why did Jezebel want to kill Elijah? he had killed prophets of Baal

Younger Teens: When Elijah fled, where did he go first? Beersheba

Younger Teens: How did the Lord encourage him in the wilderness? an angel fed him

Younger Teens: Then where did Elijah go? Horeb

Younger Children: What else was Horeb called, and for what do we remember it? Sinai, ten commandments

Younger Teens: What happened when Elijah was in the cave on the mountain? Lord spoke, gave signs

Younger Children: Was the Lord in the wind, the earthquake, and the fire? no

Younger Children: What came after them? still small voice

Younger Teens: What did the Lord tell Elijah which encouraged him? seven thousand still true to the Lord

Younger Teens: What did the Lord tell Elijah to do? anoint new kings, choose his successor

Younger Teens: What was Elisha doing when Elijah found him? plowing

Older Teens: What is pictured by the Lord's speaking in the still small voice? times of quiet meditation after temptations, when we hear the Lord speaking to us through our conscience
