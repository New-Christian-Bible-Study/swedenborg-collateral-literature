
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What three buildings did Solomon erect? +
*A:* temple, two palaces

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What happened to the kingdom after Solomon died? +
*A:* divided

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What had Solomon done which brought this about? +
*A:* idolatry, heavy taxes

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What were the two divisions of the land called? +
*A:* Israel, Judah

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do these divisions picture? +
*A:* division of one's heart and mind

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What worship was set up in Israel, and where? +
*A:* gold calves at Dan and Bethel

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our lesson for today, who is king of Israel? +
*A:* Ahab

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was his wife's name? +
*A:* Jezebel

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the Lord's prophet? +
*A:* Elijah

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did Jezebel want to kill Elijah? +
*A:* he had killed prophets of Baal

*Q:* (Younger Teens): When Elijah fled, where did he go first? +
*A:* Beersheba

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How did the Lord encourage him in the wilderness? +
*A:* an angel fed him

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Then where did Elijah go? +
*A:* Horeb

*Q:* (Younger Children): What else was Horeb called, and for what do we remember it? +
*A:* Sinai, ten commandments

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What happened when Elijah was in the cave on the mountain? +
*A:* Lord spoke, gave signs

*Q:* (Younger Children): Was the Lord in the wind, the earthquake, and the fire? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): What came after them? +
*A:* still small voice

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord tell Elijah which encouraged him? +
*A:* seven thousand still true to the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord tell Elijah to do? +
*A:* anoint new kings, choose his successor

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What was Elisha doing when Elijah found him? +
*A:* plowing

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by the Lord's speaking in the still small voice? +
*A:* times of quiet meditation after temptations, when we hear the Lord speaking to us through our conscience
