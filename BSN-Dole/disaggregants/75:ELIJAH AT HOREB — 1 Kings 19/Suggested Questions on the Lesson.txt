
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Intermediate: What three buildings did Solomon erect? temple, two palaces 

Intermediate: What happened to the kingdom after Solomon died? divided

Intermediate: What had Solomon done which brought this about? idolatry, heavy taxes

Intermediate: What were the two divisions of the land called? Israel, Judah

Intermediate: What do these divisions picture? division of one's heart and mind

Intermediate: What worship was set up in Israel, and where? gold calves at Dan and Bethel

Primary: In our lesson for today, who is king of Israel? Ahab

Primary: What was his wife's name? Jezebel

Primary: Who was the Lord's prophet? Elijah

Primary: Why did Jezebel want to kill Elijah? he had killed prophets of Baal

Intermediate: When Elijah fled, where did he go first? Beersheba

Intermediate: How did the Lord encourage him in the wilderness? an angel fed him

Intermediate: Then where did Elijah go? Horeb

Primary: What else was Horeb called, and for what do we remember it? Sinai, ten commandments

Intermediate: What happened when Elijah was in the cave on the mountain? Lord spoke, gave signs

Primary: Was the Lord in the wind, the earthquake, and the fire? no

Primary: What came after them? still small voice

Intermediate: What did the Lord tell Elijah which encouraged him? seven thousand still true to the Lord

Intermediate: What did the Lord tell Elijah to do? anoint new kings, choose his successor

Intermediate: What was Elisha doing when Elijah found him? plowing

Senior: What is pictured by the Lord's speaking in the still small voice? times of quiet meditation after temptations, when we hear the Lord speaking to us through our conscience
