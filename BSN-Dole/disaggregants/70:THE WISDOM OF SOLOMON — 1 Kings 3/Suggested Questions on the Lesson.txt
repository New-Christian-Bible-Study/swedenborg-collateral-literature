
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: How was David different from Saul? obeyed direct orders of the Lord

Younger Children: What special talents did David have? musician, poet

Older Children: What book in the Bible was given in part through David? Psalms

Older Children: What was accomplished in David's reign? peace

Older Children: Where did David set up the ark? Jerusalem

Younger Children: Who was the third king of Israel? Solomon

Younger Children: Whose son was he? David and Bathsheba

Older Children: What does his name mean? peaceful

Older Children: Where did Solomon go to worship the Lord? Gibeon

Older Children: What was at Gibeon? tabernacle

Younger Children: Who spoke to Solomon at Gibeon in a dream? the Lord

Younger Children: What did the Lord ask Solomon to do? "Ask what I shall give you"

Younger Children: What did Solomon ask the Lord to give him? an understanding heart

Older Children: What did the Lord tell him? he would have it, plus riches and honor

Older Children: What did Solomon build at Jerusalem? temple

Younger Children: How long did Solomon reign? forty years

Younger Teens: What is represented by Solomon? loving to live the Lord's will

Older Teens: Why did Solomon and not David build the temple? heavenly character comes only when one is at peace with God
