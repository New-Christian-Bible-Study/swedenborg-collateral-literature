
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: How was David different from Saul? obeyed direct orders of the Lord

Primary: What special talents did David have? musician, poet

Junior: What book in the Bible was given in part through David? Psalms

Junior: What was accomplished in David's reign? peace

Junior: Where did David set up the ark? Jerusalem

Primary: Who was the third king of Israel? Solomon

Primary: Whose son was he? David and Bathsheba

Junior: What does his name mean? peaceful

Junior: Where did Solomon go to worship the Lord? Gibeon

Junior: What was at Gibeon? tabernacle

Primary: Who spoke to Solomon at Gibeon in a dream? the Lord

Primary: What did the Lord ask Solomon to do? "Ask what I shall give you"

Primary: What did Solomon ask the Lord to give him? an understanding heart

Junior: What did the Lord tell him? he would have it, plus riches and honor

Junior: What did Solomon build at Jerusalem? temple

Primary: How long did Solomon reign? forty years

Intermediate: What is represented by Solomon? loving to live the Lord's will

Senior: Why did Solomon and not David build the temple? heavenly character comes only when one is at peace with God
