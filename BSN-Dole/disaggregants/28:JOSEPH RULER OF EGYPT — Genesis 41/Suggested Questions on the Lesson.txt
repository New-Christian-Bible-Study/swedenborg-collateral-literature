
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Joseph get into prison in Egypt? +
*A:* Potiphar's wife's lies

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he do there for the king's butler and baker? +
*A:* interpreted dreams

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did Pharaoh send for him? +
*A:* butler remembered him when Pharaoh dreamed

*Q:* (Older Children): What were Pharaoh's two dreams? +
*A:* seven fat/thin cattle, seven fat/thin ears of grain

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Joseph tell him they meant? +
*A:* seven years plenty/famine

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he advise Pharaoh to do? +
*A:* appoint wise leader, store food

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whom did Pharaoh choose to carry out this advice? +
*A:* Joseph

*Q:* (Older Children): What powers did he give Joseph? +
*A:* control of whole country

*Q:* (Older Children): Whom did he give him for his wife? +
*A:* Asenath

*Q:* (Older Children): who were Joseph's two sons? +
*A:* Manasseh and Ephraim

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Joseph prepare for the years of famine? +
*A:* stored grain all through land

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does Joseph represent? +
*A:* our religious sensitivity

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Egypt represent? +
*A:* state of acquiring memory-knowledge

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is meant by the famine? +
*A:* "too busy" to learn new truth

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by Joseph's being made ruler of Egypt? +
*A:* putting our everyday life under control of spiritual principles
