
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How did Joseph get into prison in Egypt? Potiphar's wife's lies

Younger Children: What did he do there for the king's butler and baker? interpreted dreams

Younger Children: Why did Pharaoh send for him? butler remembered him when Pharaoh dreamed

Older Children: What were Pharaoh's two dreams? seven fat/thin cattle, seven fat/thin ears of grain

Older Children: What did Joseph tell him they meant? seven years plenty/famine

Older Children: What did he advise Pharaoh to do? appoint wise leader, store food

Younger Children: Whom did Pharaoh choose to carry out this advice? Joseph

Older Children: What powers did he give Joseph? control of whole country

Older Children: Whom did he give him for his wife? Asenath

Older Children: who were Joseph's two sons? Manasseh and Ephraim

Older Children: How did Joseph prepare for the years of famine? stored grain all through land

Older Teens: What does Joseph represent? our religious sensitivity

Younger Teens: What does Egypt represent? state of acquiring memory-knowledge

Younger Teens: What is meant by the famine? "too busy" to learn new truth

Older Teens: What is pictured by Joseph's being made ruler of Egypt? putting our everyday life under control of spiritual principles
