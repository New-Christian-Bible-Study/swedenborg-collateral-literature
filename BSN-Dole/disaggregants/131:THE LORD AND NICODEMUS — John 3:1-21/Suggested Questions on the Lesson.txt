
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Who were the (1) scribes, and (2) Pharisees? (1) men who copied the Scriptures, (2) a strict religious sect

Junior: What had they done to the Scriptures? added many of their own ideas

Junior: What did the Lord call them? hypocrites

Junior: Why were they opposed to the Lord? He exposed their hypocrisy

Primary: What is the name of the man in our lesson for today? Nicodemus

Junior: What was he besides being a Pharisee? a ruler (member of Sanhedrin)

Primary: Why did he come to the Lord by night? afraid of ridicule

Junior: Why did he believe in the Lord? miracles

Junior: What did the Lord see he wanted to ask? way to heaven

Primary: What did the Lord tell him? you must be born again

Intermediate: What did the Lord mean by being "born again"? repent, follow His teachings

Junior: What did the Lord say about the brazen serpent in the wilderness? ... so must Son of man be lifted up

Junior: Why did the Lord say He came into the world? to save it

Junior: Why does He say some people are condemned? will not believe

Junior: Why should we stop to think every time we find ourselves trying to cover up our thoughts or feelings or actions? probably means we just want our own way

Intermediate: What does it mean to be "born of water"? outer life cleansed

Senior: What does it mean to be "born of the Spirit"? inner life purified

Senior: How is this related to the work of (1) John the Baptist, and (2) the Lord? (1) cleansing outer life (2) purifying thoughts, replacing self-love with love of the Lord and the neighbor
