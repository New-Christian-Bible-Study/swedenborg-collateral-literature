
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Older Children: What enemy attacked Israel while Samuel was still a child? Philistia

Younger Children: How did the Israelites try to win the victory? took ark to battlefield

Younger Children: What happened to the two priests who took the ark out of the tabernacle? killed

Older Children: What happened to the ark? captured

Younger Children: What was its effect on the Philistines' god? it fell and broke

Older Children: What was its effect on the Philistines themselves? fear, plague

Younger Children: What did the Philistines decide to do? send it back

Older Children: Who told them how to send the ark back? priests, diviners

Younger Children: How was it sent back? new cart

Younger Children: What was sent with it? five gold tumors, mice

Older Children: To what place did it come? Bethshemesh

Younger Teens: What do the Philistines represent? knowledge without obedience

Younger Teens: Why could they not keep the ark? knowledge not obeyed plagues one

Older Teens: What are pictured by the new cart and the milk cows? new attitude, kindly affection

Older Teens: How did the Philistine diviners know the way in which the ark should be returned to Israel? knowledge of correspondences
