
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Junior: What enemy attacked Israel while Samuel was still a child? Philistia

Primary: How did the Israelites try to win the victory? took ark to battlefield

Primary: What happened to the two priests who took the ark out of the tabernacle? killed

Junior: What happened to the ark? captured

Primary: What was its effect on the Philistines' god? it fell and broke

Junior: What was its effect on the Philistines themselves? fear, plague

Primary: What did the Philistines decide to do? send it back

Junior: Who told them how to send the ark back? priests, diviners

Primary: How was it sent back? new cart

Primary: What was sent with it? five gold tumors, mice

Junior: To what place did it come? Bethshemesh

Intermediate: What do the Philistines represent? knowledge without obedience

Intermediate: Why could they not keep the ark? knowledge not obeyed plagues one

Senior: What are pictured by the new cart and the milk cows? new attitude, kindly affection

Senior: How did the Philistine diviners know the way in which the ark should be returned to Israel? knowledge of correspondences
