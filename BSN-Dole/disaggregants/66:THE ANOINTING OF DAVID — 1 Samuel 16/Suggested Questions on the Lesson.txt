
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell Samuel about Saul and his line? +
*A:* to lose kingdom

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did the Lord send Samuel to find a new king? +
*A:* Bethlehem

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who else was born in Bethlehem? +
*A:* Jesus, also Benjamin

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Samuel think when he saw Jesse's eldest son? +
*A:* here was the one

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord tell him? +
*A:* not this one

*Q:* (Older Children): How many sons of Jesse did the Lord reject? +
*A:* seven

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was David doing when they sent for him? +
*A:* tending sheep

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to David when he was anointed? +
*A:* spirit of Lord came on him

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to Saul at the same time? +
*A:* evil spirit troubled him

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did David look like? +
*A:* ruddy, handsome

*Q:* (Older Children): What other qualities did he have? +
*A:* warrior, poet, obedient to God

*Q:* (Younger Children): On what instrument did David play? +
*A:* lyre (harp)

*Q:* (Older Children): How did David come to Saul's attention? +
*A:* soothed with music

*Q:* (Older Children): What position did Saul give him? +
*A:* armor-bearer

*Q:* (Older Children): What book in the Bible was written in part through David? +
*A:* Psalms

*Q:* (Younger Teens): To what does David correspond? +
*A:* truth understood spiritually ruling in life

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by Saul's continuing on the throne after David was anointed? +
*A:* old habits hard to change
