
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What did the Lord tell Samuel about Saul and his line? to lose kingdom

Younger Children: Where did the Lord send Samuel to find a new king? Bethlehem

Younger Children: Who else was born in Bethlehem? Jesus, also Benjamin

Younger Children: What did Samuel think when he saw Jesse's eldest son? here was the one

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him? not this one

Older Children: How many sons of Jesse did the Lord reject? seven

Younger Children: What was David doing when they sent for him? tending sheep

Older Children: What happened to David when he was anointed? spirit of Lord came on him

Older Children: What happened to Saul at the same time? evil spirit troubled him

Younger Children: What did David look like? ruddy, handsome

Older Children: What other qualities did he have? warrior, poet, obedient to God

Younger Children: On what instrument did David play? lyre (harp)

Older Children: How did David come to Saul's attention? soothed with music

Older Children: What position did Saul give him? armor-bearer

Older Children: What book in the Bible was written in part through David? Psalms

Younger Teens: To what does David correspond? truth understood spiritually ruling in life

Older Teens: What is pictured by Saul's continuing on the throne after David was anointed? old habits hard to change
