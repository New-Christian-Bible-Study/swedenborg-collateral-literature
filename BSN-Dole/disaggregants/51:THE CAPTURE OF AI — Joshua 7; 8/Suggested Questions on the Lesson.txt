
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What was the first city the Israelites had to conquer? Jericho

Younger Children: How did the Lord help them to take it? made walls fall

Younger Children: What command did the Lord give them as to the spoils of Jericho? not to take any for themselves

Younger Children: What was the next city they had to take? Ai

Older Children: How did Joshua at first try to take it? small army

Older Children: What did he do when his army fled? fell on face, tore clothes, blamed God

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him? someone had sinned

Younger Children: How did they find the offender? drew lots

Younger Children: Who was he? Achan

Older Children: What had he done? taken garment, gold, silver

Older Children: How was he punished? stoned, burned

Older Children: How much of the army did Joshua use in the second attack on Ai? all

Older Children: What did he do with part of his men? hid as ambush

Older Children: What was done with the spoils of Ai? people could take

Younger Children: What did they do at Shechem? built altar, wrote and read laws

Younger Teens: What does the sin of Achan picture? feeling some of our faults are excusable

Younger Teens: What lesson does it teach? don't blame God, or others, when you get into trouble

Older Teens: What is the meaning of the ambush? using reserve strength we have from our knowledge of spiritual things
