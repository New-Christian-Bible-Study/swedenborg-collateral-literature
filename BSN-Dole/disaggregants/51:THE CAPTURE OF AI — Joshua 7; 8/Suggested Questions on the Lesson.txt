
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What was the first city the Israelites had to conquer? Jericho

Primary: How did the Lord help them to take it? made walls fall

Primary: What command did the Lord give them as to the spoils of Jericho? not to take any for themselves

Primary: What was the next city they had to take? Ai

Junior: How did Joshua at first try to take it? small army

Junior: What did he do when his army fled? fell on face, tore clothes, blamed God

Primary: What did the Lord tell him? someone had sinned

Primary: How did they find the offender? drew lots

Primary: Who was he? Achan

Junior: What had he done? taken garment, gold, silver

Junior: How was he punished? stoned, burned

Junior: How much of the army did Joshua use in the second attack on Ai? all

Junior: What did he do with part of his men? hid as ambush

Junior: What was done with the spoils of Ai? people could take

Primary: What did they do at Shechem? built altar, wrote and read laws

Intermediate: What does the sin of Achan picture? feeling some of our faults are excusable

Intermediate: What lesson does it teach? don't blame God, or others, when you get into trouble

Senior: What is the meaning of the ambush? using reserve strength we have from our knowledge of spiritual things

The teacher should be prepared to tell briefly the story of the fall of Jericho and the capture of Ai. The older pupils may be able to tell these stories themselves.
