
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Who was John? the beloved apostle

Older Children: What island was he exiled to in his old age? Patmos

Younger Children: What did the Lord do for him there? gave him visions

Older Children: What was the first vision given him? the glorified Lord

Older Children: What does the whole book of Revelation prophesy? the Second Coming

Older Children: How were the evils which grew up in the first Christian Church disclosed? opening of seals

Younger Children: In our chapter for today what does John first see? woman clothed with the sun

Older Children: Can you describe this part of the vision? moon under feet, crown of twelve stars ...

Younger Children: What next appeared to threaten the woman and her child? dragon

Younger Children: What can you tell about the dragon? seven heads, ten horns, seven crowns

Younger Children: How was the child saved? caught up to heaven

Older Children: Who fought against the dragon? Michael and his angels

Older Children: How was the woman saved? eagle wings, fled to wilderness

Younger Teens: What do the woman and her child represent? New Church and truth of that church

Older Teens: What does the dragon represent? doctrine of "faith alone"
