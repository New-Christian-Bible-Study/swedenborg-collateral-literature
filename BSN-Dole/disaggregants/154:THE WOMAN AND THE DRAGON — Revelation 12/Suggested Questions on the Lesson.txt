
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was John? +
*A:* the beloved apostle

*Q:* (Older Children): What island was he exiled to in his old age? +
*A:* Patmos

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord do for him there? +
*A:* gave him visions

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the first vision given him? +
*A:* the glorified Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): What does the whole book of Revelation prophesy? +
*A:* the Second Coming

*Q:* (Older Children): How were the evils which grew up in the first Christian Church disclosed? +
*A:* opening of seals

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our chapter for today what does John first see? +
*A:* woman clothed with the sun

*Q:* (Older Children): Can you describe this part of the vision? +
*A:* moon under feet, crown of twelve stars ...

*Q:* (Younger Children): What next appeared to threaten the woman and her child? +
*A:* dragon

*Q:* (Younger Children): What can you tell about the dragon? +
*A:* seven heads, ten horns, seven crowns

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was the child saved? +
*A:* caught up to heaven

*Q:* (Older Children): Who fought against the dragon? +
*A:* Michael and his angels

*Q:* (Older Children): How was the woman saved? +
*A:* eagle wings, fled to wilderness

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do the woman and her child represent? +
*A:* New Church and truth of that church

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the dragon represent? +
*A:* doctrine of "faith alone"
