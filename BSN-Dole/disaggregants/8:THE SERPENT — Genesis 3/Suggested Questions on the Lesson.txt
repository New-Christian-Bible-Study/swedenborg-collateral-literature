
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Can you repeat the first verse of the Bible? +
*A:* In the beginning ...

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What are the heavens and the earth in us? +
*A:* higher and lower natures

*Q:* (Older Teens): Of what does the first chapter of Genesis treat in its spiritual sense? +
*A:* steps in regeneration

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the difference between a man and an animal? +
*A:* man image of God

*Q:* (Older Children): What were the first people told to do? +
*A:* rule "earth"

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the first church developed on earth called? +
*A:* Most Ancient

*Q:* (Older Children): How is the beautiful state of the first church described? +
*A:* Eden

*Q:* (Younger Children): After chapter 1, what names are used to tell about the first men and women? +
*A:* Adam and Eve

*Q:* (Younger Children): What two special trees are mentioned? +
*A:* life, knowledge

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the tree of life signify? +
*A:* principle that all life is from God

*Q:* (Younger Children): In the beginning of the story, where was this tree in the garden? +
*A:* center

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were the first people told not to do? +
*A:* eat of tree of knowledge

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does this mean? +
*A:* not to decide for yourself what is good or evil

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the serpent? +
*A:* symbol of affection for sense pleasures

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the serpent tell Eve? +
*A:* wouldn't die

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Eve and Adam do? +
*A:* ate fruit

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why were they afterward ashamed? +
*A:* guilt for disobedience

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did they try to cover their nakedness? +
*A:* fig leaves

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was their punishment for disobedience? +
*A:* driven out of garden

*Q:* (Older Children): How was the garden protected? +
*A:* cherubim

*Q:* (Older Teens): What was the first prophecy of the Lord's Advent? +
*A:* verse 15 - "it shall bruise thy head ... "
