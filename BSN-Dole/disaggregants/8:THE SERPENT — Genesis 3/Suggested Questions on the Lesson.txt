
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Can you repeat the first verse of the Bible? In the beginning ...

Intermediate: What are the heavens and the earth in us? higher and lower natures

Senior: Of what does the first chapter of Genesis treat in its spiritual sense? steps in regeneration

Intermediate: What is the difference between a man and an animal? man image of God

Junior: What were the first people told to do? rule "earth"

Intermediate: What is the first church developed on earth called? Most Ancient

Junior: How is the beautiful state of the first church described? Eden

Primary: After chapter 1, what names are used to tell about the first men and women? Adam and Eve

Primary: What two special trees are mentioned? life, knowledge

Intermediate: What does the tree of life signify? principle that all life is from God

Primary: In the beginning of the story, where was this tree in the garden? center

Primary: What were the first people told not to do? eat of tree of knowledge

Senior: What does this mean? not to decide for yourself what is good or evil

Intermediate: What is the serpent? symbol of affection for sense pleasures

Junior: What did the serpent tell Eve? wouldn't die

Primary: What did Eve and Adam do? ate fruit

Primary: Why were they afterward ashamed? guilt for disobedience

Primary: How did they try to cover their nakedness? fig leaves

Primary: What was their punishment for disobedience? driven out of garden

Junior: How was the garden protected? cherubim

Senior: What was the first prophecy of the Lord's Advent? verse 15 - "it shall bruise thy head ... "
