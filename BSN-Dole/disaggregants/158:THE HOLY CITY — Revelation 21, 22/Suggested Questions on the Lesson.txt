
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What did all the Lord's messages point out to the seven churches? +
*A:* good and bad points, warning, promise

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the church of Laodicea receive the severest condemnation? +
*A:* hardest problem to overcome

*Q:* (Younger Children): What has been named for this city (Revelation 21:2)? +
*A:* our church

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many gates did the city have? +
*A:* twelve

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the angel measure it with? +
*A:* gold reed

*Q:* (Older Children): What was its shape found to be? +
*A:* foursquare

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were seen in its foundations? +
*A:* precious stones

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were its gates made of? +
*A:* pearl

*Q:* (Older Children): How was it lighted? +
*A:* the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): Who can enter it? +
*A:* all who will to

*Q:* (Older Children): What was seen proceeding out of the throne of God? +
*A:* river of water of life

*Q:* (Younger Children): What grew beside the river? +
*A:* tree of life

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where have we heard about the river and the tree of life before? +
*A:* Eden

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the angel tell John when John would have worshiped him? +
*A:* worship God

*Q:* (Older Children): How do we regularly use the last verse of the Bible? +
*A:* benediction

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the holy city represent? +
*A:* true system of doctrine found in opened Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): What should the name of our church mean to us? +
*A:* a challenge to live up to all it implies
