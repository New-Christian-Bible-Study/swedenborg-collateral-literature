
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What did all the Lord's messages point out to the seven churches? good and bad points, warning, promise

Older Children: Why did the church of Laodicea receive the severest condemnation? hardest problem to overcome

Younger Children: What has been named for this city (Revelation 21:2)? our church

Younger Children: How many gates did the city have? twelve

Younger Children: What did the angel measure it with? gold reed

Older Children: What was its shape found to be? foursquare

Younger Children: What were seen in its foundations? precious stones

Younger Children: What were its gates made of? pearl

Older Children: How was it lighted? the Lord

Older Children: Who can enter it? all who will to

Older Children: What was seen proceeding out of the throne of God? river of water of life

Younger Children: What grew beside the river? tree of life

Younger Children: Where have we heard about the river and the tree of life before? Eden

Older Children: What did the angel tell John when John would have worshiped him? worship God

Older Children: How do we regularly use the last verse of the Bible? benediction

Younger Teens: What does the holy city represent? true system of doctrine found in opened Word

Older Teens: What should the name of our church mean to us? a challenge to live up to all it implies
