
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did Joseph's older brothers dislike him? +
*A:* favorite, dreams

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was he taken by the Ishmaelite caravan? +
*A:* Egypt

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he come to be put in prison? +
*A:* Potiphar's wife's lies

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who else were in prison with him? +
*A:* Pharaoh's butler and baker

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he do for them? +
*A:* interpreted dreams

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the butler's dream? +
*A:* wine into cup

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the baker's dream? +
*A:* birds eating bread

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to the butler and the baker? +
*A:* butler restored, baker hanged

*Q:* (Younger Children): Did the butler remember Joseph? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is Joseph in our lives? +
*A:* desire to be good

*Q:* (Older Teens): How do we sometimes "put him in prison in Egypt"? +
*A:* try to forget him when we have selfish desires

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is meant by his being able to interpret dreams? +
*A:* to understand our experiences

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why could the Lord always take care of Joseph? +
*A:* wanted to do right
