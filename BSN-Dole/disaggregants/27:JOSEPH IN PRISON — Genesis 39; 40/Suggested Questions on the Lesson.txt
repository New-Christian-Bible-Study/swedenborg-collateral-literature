
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Why did Joseph's older brothers dislike him? favorite, dreams

Younger Children: Where was he taken by the Ishmaelite caravan? Egypt

Older Children: How did he come to be put in prison? Potiphar's wife's lies

Younger Children: Who else were in prison with him? Pharaoh's butler and baker

Younger Children: What did he do for them? interpreted dreams

Older Children: What was the butler's dream? wine into cup

Younger Children: What was the baker's dream? birds eating bread

Older Children: What happened to the butler and the baker? butler restored, baker hanged

Younger Children: Did the butler remember Joseph? no

Younger Teens: What is Joseph in our lives? desire to be good

Older Teens: How do we sometimes "put him in prison in Egypt"? try to forget him when we have selfish desires

Older Teens: What is meant by his being able to interpret dreams? to understand our experiences

Younger Teens: Why could the Lord always take care of Joseph? wanted to do right
