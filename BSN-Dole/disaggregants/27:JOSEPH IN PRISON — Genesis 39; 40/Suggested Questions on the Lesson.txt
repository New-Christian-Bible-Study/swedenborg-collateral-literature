
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Why did Joseph's older brothers dislike him? favorite, dreams

Primary: Where was he taken by the Ishmaelite caravan? Egypt

Junior: How did he come to be put in prison? Potiphar's wife's lies

Primary: Who else were in prison with him? Pharaoh's butler and baker

Primary: What did he do for them? interpreted dreams

Junior: What was the butler's dream? wine into cup

Primary: What was the baker's dream? birds eating bread

Junior: What happened to the butler and the baker? butler restored, baker hanged

Primary: Did the butler remember Joseph? no

Intermediate: What is Joseph in our lives? desire to be good

Senior: How do we sometimes "put him in prison in Egypt"? try to forget him when we have selfish desires

Senior: What is meant by his being able to interpret dreams? to understand our experiences

Intermediate: Why could the Lord always take care of Joseph? wanted to do right
