
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Where did David live? Bethlehem

Primary: What was his occupation? shepherd

Junior: Where were the armies of Saul and the Philistines facing each other? Valley of Elah

Primary: Whom had the Philistines chosen to be their champion? Goliath

Junior: What can you tell about Goliath? nine feet tall, heavily armored

Junior: What challenge did he shout to Israel? "Give me a man ... "

Junior: How had David happened to come down to the army? to bring food to brothers

Primary: What offer did he make to Saul? "I will fight Goliath"

Primary: Why did he think he could overcome Goliath? trusted God

Junior: Why could David not use Saul's armor? had not "proved" it

Primary: What were David's weapons? sling, five smooth stones

Junior: How did he kill Goliath? sling and stone

Junior: With what did he cut off Goliath's head? Goliath's sword

Senior: What do the Philistines represent? thinking we are better than others because of our superior knowledge

Intermediate: What does Goliath stand for? inherited pride

Intermediate: What are the smooth stones from the brook? truths from the Word we have tried and found to be effective
