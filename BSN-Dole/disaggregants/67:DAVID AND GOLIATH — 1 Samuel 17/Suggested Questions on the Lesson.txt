
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Where did David live? Bethlehem

Younger Children: What was his occupation? shepherd

Older Children: Where were the armies of Saul and the Philistines facing each other? Valley of Elah

Younger Children: Whom had the Philistines chosen to be their champion? Goliath

Older Children: What can you tell about Goliath? nine feet tall, heavily armored

Older Children: What challenge did he shout to Israel? "Give me a man ... "

Older Children: How had David happened to come down to the army? to bring food to brothers

Younger Children: What offer did he make to Saul? "I will fight Goliath"

Younger Children: Why did he think he could overcome Goliath? trusted God

Older Children: Why could David not use Saul's armor? had not "proved" it

Younger Children: What were David's weapons? sling, five smooth stones

Older Children: How did he kill Goliath? sling and stone

Older Children: With what did he cut off Goliath's head? Goliath's sword

Older Teens: What do the Philistines represent? thinking we are better than others because of our superior knowledge

Younger Teens: What does Goliath stand for? inherited pride

Younger Teens: What are the smooth stones from the brook? truths from the Word we have tried and found to be effective
