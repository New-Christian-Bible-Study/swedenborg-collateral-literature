
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: In what book is our lesson today? Psalms

Younger Children: What is a psalm? a song

Older Children: How do we know that the book of Psalms is part of the inspired Word? Luke 24:44

Younger Teens: Why do you think everyone loves the Psalms? appeal to heart

Older Children: In Psalm 91 what does the Lord promise the man who lives close to Him? protection

Younger Teens: Of what two general classes of dangers does the Psalm speak? of night, of day

Older Children: Is the Lord talking about dangers to our bodies? no

Younger Children: What does He say our armor against these dangers is? obeying His Word

Younger Children: What does He tell us about the angels? will take care of us

Older Children: How can we use this Psalm to help us? use against bad thoughts and feelings

Older Teens: What is the difference between the dangers by day and those by night? "day" = deliberate evils, "night" = errors of ignorance
