
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: In what book is our lesson today? Psalms

Primary: What is a psalm? a song

Junior: How do we know that the book of Psalms is part of the inspired Word? Luke 24:44

Intermediate: Why do you think everyone loves the Psalms? appeal to heart

Junior: In Psalm 91 what does the Lord promise the man who lives close to Him? protection

Intermediate: Of what two general classes of dangers does the Psalm speak? of night, of day

Junior: Is the Lord talking about dangers to our bodies? no

Primary: What does He say our armor against these dangers is? obeying His Word

Primary: What does He tell us about the angels? will take care of us

Junior: How can we use this Psalm to help us? use against bad thoughts and feelings

Senior: What is the difference between the dangers by day and those by night? "day" = deliberate evils, "night" = errors of ignorance
