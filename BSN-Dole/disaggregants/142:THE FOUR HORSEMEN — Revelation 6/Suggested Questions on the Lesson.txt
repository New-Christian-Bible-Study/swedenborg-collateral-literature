
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What is the name of the last book in the Bible? Revelation

Primary: To whom was this revelation given? the apostle John

Junior: Do you remember what was the first thing John saw? the risen Lord

Junior: For whom was he given messages? seven churches

Junior: What great symbol of judgment did he see? throne

Junior: Who were seated around the throne? twenty-four elders

Primary: Who sat upon the throne? the Lord (the lamb)

Junior: What other creatures appeared in the vision? cherubim

Primary: What was in the right hand of the one who sat on the throne? a scroll or book

Primary: What was this book? the Word

Primary: With how many seals was it sealed? seven

Junior: Who could open it? the Lord (lion of Judah, lamb)

Primary: What did John see when the first seal was opened? white horse

Primary: What did he see when each of the next three seals was opened? red, black, pale horses

Junior: Who were seen when the fifth seal was opened? those slain for the Word

Junior: What happened when the sixth seal was opened? earthquake, etc.

Senior: What in general is pictured by the four horses? different ways of regarding the Word

Intermediate: What does the opening of the seals mean? opening of inner sense of Word to our understanding
