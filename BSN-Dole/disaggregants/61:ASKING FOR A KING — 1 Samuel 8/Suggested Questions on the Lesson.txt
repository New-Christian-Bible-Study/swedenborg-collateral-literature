
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Older Children: Where was he brought up? Shiloh

Older Children: Who was the high priest when Samuel was a child? Eli

Older Children: How did he and all the people learn that Samuel was to be a prophet? Lord spoke to Samuel

Older Children: What was the difference between Samuel and all the earlier Judges? religious leader, accepted by all

Older Children: Where was Samuel's home? Ramah

Older Children: In his old age what did he do which made the people dissatisfied? let his sons judge

Younger Children: What did they ask him to give them? a king

Younger Children: Was Samuel willing? no

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him? give them a king

Older Children: What did Samuel tell the people a king would be like? harsh

Older Children: Did they change their minds? no

Younger Teens: What does a king represent? truth ruling in our lives

Older Teens: What does Samuel represent? the Word as a whole restoring order to our lives
