
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the last of the Judges? Samuel

Junior: Where was he brought up? Shiloh

Junior: Who was the high priest when Samuel was a child? Eli

Junior: How did he and all the people learn that Samuel was to be a prophet? Lord spoke to Samuel

Junior: What was the difference between Samuel and all the earlier Judges? religious leader, accepted by all

Junior: Where was Samuel's home? Ramah

Junior: In his old age what did he do which made the people dissatisfied? let his sons judge

Primary: What did they ask him to give them? a king

Primary: Was Samuel willing? no

Primary: What did the Lord tell him? give them a king

Junior: What did Samuel tell the people a king would be like? harsh

Junior: Did they change their minds? no

Intermediate: What does a king represent? truth ruling in our lives

Senior: What does Samuel represent? the Word as a whole restoring order to our lives
