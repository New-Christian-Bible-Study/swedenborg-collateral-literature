
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the last of the Judges? +
*A:* Samuel

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was he brought up? +
*A:* Shiloh

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was the high priest when Samuel was a child? +
*A:* Eli

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he and all the people learn that Samuel was to be a prophet? +
*A:* Lord spoke to Samuel

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the difference between Samuel and all the earlier Judges? +
*A:* religious leader, accepted by all

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was Samuel's home? +
*A:* Ramah

*Q:* (Older Children): In his old age what did he do which made the people dissatisfied? +
*A:* let his sons judge

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they ask him to give them? +
*A:* a king

*Q:* (Younger Children): Was Samuel willing? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord tell him? +
*A:* give them a king

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Samuel tell the people a king would be like? +
*A:* harsh

*Q:* (Older Children): Did they change their minds? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does a king represent? +
*A:* truth ruling in our lives

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does Samuel represent? +
*A:* the Word as a whole restoring order to our lives
