
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Near what sea did the Lord make His home during His ministry? Galilee

Junior: What was the name of the place where He lived? Capernaum

Primary: Who were the first four disciples He called? Peter, Andrew, James, John

Primary: What was their occupation? fishermen

Junior: What were they doing when they were called? working with their nets

Primary: What words did the Lord use when He called them? Follow me!

Primary: What did they do immediately when He said, "Follow me"? followed Him

Junior: Of what three types of work did the Lord's ministry consist? teaching, preaching, healing

Junior: What kinds of ailments are mentioned in our lesson? possession, lunacy, palsy

Junior: Where did the great numbers of people come from who followed the Lord? all over

Intermediate: What does the sea represent? knowledge gathered in the memory

Intermediate: What do fish represent? affection for such knowledge

Senior: What is meant by being "fishers of men"? to draw others into the Lord's kingdom
