
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Near what sea did the Lord make His home during His ministry? Galilee

Older Children: What was the name of the place where He lived? Capernaum

Younger Children: Who were the first four disciples He called? Peter, Andrew, James, John

Younger Children: What was their occupation? fishermen

Older Children: What were they doing when they were called? working with their nets

Younger Children: What words did the Lord use when He called them? Follow me!

Younger Children: What did they do immediately when He said, "Follow me"? followed Him

Older Children: Of what three types of work did the Lord's ministry consist? teaching, preaching, healing

Older Children: What kinds of ailments are mentioned in our lesson? possession, lunacy, palsy

Older Children: Where did the great numbers of people come from who followed the Lord? all over

Younger Teens: What does the sea represent? knowledge gathered in the memory

Younger Teens: What do fish represent? affection for such knowledge

Older Teens: What is meant by being "fishers of men"? to draw others into the Lord's kingdom
