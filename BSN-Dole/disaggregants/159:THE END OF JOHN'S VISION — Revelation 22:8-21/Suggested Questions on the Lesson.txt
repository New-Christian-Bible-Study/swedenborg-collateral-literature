
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): In our lesson today what did John think about the angel who showed him the vision? +
*A:* that he should be worshiped

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the angel tell him? +
*A:* worship God

*Q:* (Older Children): What do alpha and omega mean? +
*A:* first, last

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who have right to the tree of life and the right to enter the holy city? +
*A:* those who obey

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who is the Lord God? +
*A:* Jesus Christ

*Q:* (Older Children): What invitation does He give us? +
*A:* come unto me

*Q:* (Older Children): What does He warn us not to do? +
*A:* add to or take away from words of book

*Q:* (Younger Children): For what is our church named? +
*A:* holy city

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why is it so important for us to learn and do the Lord's will in this world? +
*A:* because we make our eternal choice here

*Q:* (Older Teens): What do (1) the river, and (2) the tree of life, represent? +
*A:* (1) truth of Word, (2) divine love

*Q:* (Older Children): Why is the last verse of the Bible so familiar to us? +
*A:* benediction
