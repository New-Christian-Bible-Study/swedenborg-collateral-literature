
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: In our lesson today what did John think about the angel who showed him the vision? that he should be worshiped

Younger Children: What did the angel tell him? worship God

Older Children: What do alpha and omega mean? first, last

Younger Children: Who have right to the tree of life and the right to enter the holy city? those who obey

Younger Children: Who is the Lord God? Jesus Christ

Older Children: What invitation does He give us? come unto me

Older Children: What does He warn us not to do? add to or take away from words of book

Younger Children: For what is our church named? holy city

Younger Teens: Why is it so important for us to learn and do the Lord's will in this world? because we make our eternal choice here

Older Teens: What do (1) the river, and (2) the tree of life, represent? (1) truth of Word, (2) divine love

Older Children: Why is the last verse of the Bible so familiar to us? benediction
