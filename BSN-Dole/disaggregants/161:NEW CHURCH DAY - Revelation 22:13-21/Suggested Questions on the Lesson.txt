
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What special day do we celebrate today? +
*A:* New Church Day

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why do we call it that? +
*A:* @TCR 791

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is the first thing the Lord says about Himself in our lesson for today? +
*A:* I am alpha and omega

*Q:* (Younger Children): What are alpha and omega? +
*A:* first and last letters of Greek alphabet

*Q:* (Younger Children): How do we know it is Jesus Christ who says this? +
*A:* John recognized Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where do we often hear the last verse of our lesson for today? +
*A:* benediction in church

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did the Lord have to make a Second Coming? +
*A:* men needed new insights into divine truth

*Q:* (Older Children): Why must the Lord always leave us free to choose whether or not we will do right? +
*A:* love for the Lord and for what is good cannot be forced

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why should we try to remember this even when we are children? +
*A:* helps prepare us for heaven

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why do men say that we are living in a new age? +
*A:* great scientific advances

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What false conclusion do we sometimes draw from this? +
*A:* Biblical laws "outdated"

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What laws will never change? +
*A:* the Lord's laws

*Q:* (Older Teens): What has the Lord done to meet our need in this new age? +
*A:* opened inner sense of Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): What obligation does this lay upon us? +
*A:* regular study and continued effort to live up to the new truth
