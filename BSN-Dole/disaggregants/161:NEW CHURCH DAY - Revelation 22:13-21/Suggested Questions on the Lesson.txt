
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What special day do we celebrate today? New Church Day

Older Teens: Why do we call it that? @TCR 791

Younger Children: What is the first thing the Lord says about Himself in our lesson for today? I am alpha and omega

Younger Children: What are alpha and omega? first and last letters of Greek alphabet

Younger Children: How do we know it is Jesus Christ who says this? John recognized Him

Younger Children: Where do we often hear the last verse of our lesson for today? benediction in church

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord have to make a Second Coming? men needed new insights into divine truth

Older Children: Why must the Lord always leave us free to choose whether or not we will do right? love for the Lord and for what is good cannot be forced

Younger Teens: Why should we try to remember this even when we are children? helps prepare us for heaven

Younger Teens: Why do men say that we are living in a new age? great scientific advances

Younger Teens: What false conclusion do we sometimes draw from this? Biblical laws "outdated"

Younger Teens: What laws will never change? the Lord's laws

Older Teens: What has the Lord done to meet our need in this new age? opened inner sense of Word

Older Teens: What obligation does this lay upon us? regular study and continued effort to live up to the new truth
