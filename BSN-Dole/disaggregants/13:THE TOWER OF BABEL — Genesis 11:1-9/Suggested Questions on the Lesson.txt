
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were Cain and Abel? +
*A:* sons of Adam and Eve

*Q:* (Older Children): What were their occupations? +
*A:* farmer, shepherd

*Q:* (Older Children): What offerings did they bring to the Lord? +
*A:* grain, lamb

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was Cain's offering rejected? +
*A:* insincere

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord preserve Cain? +
*A:* mark

*Q:* (Older Children): In our story for today, in which direction were the people journeying? +
*A:* from East

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did they stop? +
*A:* Shinar (Babylon)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they plan to build? +
*A:* tower

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the purpose of their building? +
*A:* reach heaven

*Q:* (Younger Children): What materials did they use? +
*A:* brick, tar

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord stop the building? +
*A:* confused language

*Q:* (Older Children): What became of the people? +
*A:* scattered

*Q:* (Older Children): The end of what church is described in this story? +
*A:* Ancient

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the tower called? +
*A:* Babel

*Q:* (Older Children): What other name in the Bible is the Greek form of the Hebrew word Babel? +
*A:* Babylon

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is pictured by the building of the Tower of Babel? +
*A:* building life on selfish premises

*Q:* (Older Teens): What was the difference between the Most Ancient and the Ancient Churches? +
*A:* perception — truth acquired by an inner way; and conscience — truth acquired by an outer way
