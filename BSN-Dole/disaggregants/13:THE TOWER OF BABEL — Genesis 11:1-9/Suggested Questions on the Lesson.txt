
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Who were Cain and Abel? sons of Adam and Eve

Junior: What were their occupations? farmer, shepherd

Junior: What offerings did they bring to the Lord? grain, lamb

Junior: Why was Cain's offering rejected? insincere

Junior: How did the Lord preserve Cain? mark

Junior: In our story for today, in which direction were the people journeying? from East

Primary: Where did they stop? Shinar (Babylon)

Primary: What did they plan to build? tower

Primary: What was the purpose of their building? reach heaven

Primary: What materials did they use? brick, tar

Junior: How did the Lord stop the building? confused language

Junior: What became of the people? scattered

Junior: The end of what church is described in this story? Ancient

Primary: What was the tower called? Babel

Junior: What other name in the Bible is the Greek form of the Hebrew word Babel? Babylon

Intermediate: What is pictured by the building of the Tower of Babel? building life on selfish premises

Senior: What was the difference between the Most Ancient and the Ancient Churches? perception — truth acquired by an inner way; and conscience — truth acquired by an outer way
