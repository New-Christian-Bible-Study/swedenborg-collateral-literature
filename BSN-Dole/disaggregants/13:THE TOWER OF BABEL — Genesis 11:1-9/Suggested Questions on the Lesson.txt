
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Who were Cain and Abel? sons of Adam and Eve

Older Children: What were their occupations? farmer, shepherd

Older Children: What offerings did they bring to the Lord? grain, lamb

Older Children: Why was Cain's offering rejected? insincere

Older Children: How did the Lord preserve Cain? mark

Older Children: In our story for today, in which direction were the people journeying? from East

Younger Children: Where did they stop? Shinar (Babylon)

Younger Children: What did they plan to build? tower

Younger Children: What was the purpose of their building? reach heaven

Younger Children: What materials did they use? brick, tar

Older Children: How did the Lord stop the building? confused language

Older Children: What became of the people? scattered

Older Children: The end of what church is described in this story? Ancient

Younger Children: What was the tower called? Babel

Older Children: What other name in the Bible is the Greek form of the Hebrew word Babel? Babylon

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the building of the Tower of Babel? building life on selfish premises

Older Teens: What was the difference between the Most Ancient and the Ancient Churches? perception — truth acquired by an inner way; and conscience — truth acquired by an outer way
