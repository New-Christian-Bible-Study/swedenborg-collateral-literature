
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the father of the third church? Abraham

Junior: In what place did he settle in the land of Canaan? South (Negeb)

Primary: What was his wife's name? Sarai

Primary: What one thing did Abram not have that he wanted? a son

Junior: How did Sarai try to supply this lack? gave Abraham Hagar as a wife

Primary: From what country did Hagar come? Egypt

Junior: What fault did she commit? despised her mistress

Junior: Why did she run away from her mistress Sarai? harsh treatment

Primary: Where did she go and where did the angel of the Lord find her? wilderness, spring

Primary: What did he tell her to do? go back and obey

Junior: What did he say about her son? to father great nation

Primary: What was her son's name? Ishmael
