
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the father of the third church? +
*A:* Abraham

*Q:* (Older Children): In what place did he settle in the land of Canaan? +
*A:* South (Negeb)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was his wife's name? +
*A:* Sarai

*Q:* (Younger Children): What one thing did Abram not have that he wanted? +
*A:* a son

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Sarai try to supply this lack? +
*A:* gave Abraham Hagar as a wife

*Q:* (Younger Children): From what country did Hagar come? +
*A:* Egypt

*Q:* (Older Children): What fault did she commit? +
*A:* despised her mistress

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did she run away from her mistress Sarai? +
*A:* harsh treatment

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did she go and where did the angel of the Lord find her? +
*A:* wilderness, spring

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he tell her to do? +
*A:* go back and obey

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he say about her son? +
*A:* to father great nation

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was her son's name? +
*A:* Ishmael
