
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the father of the third church? Abraham

Older Children: In what place did he settle in the land of Canaan? South (Negeb)

Younger Children: What was his wife's name? Sarai

Younger Children: What one thing did Abram not have that he wanted? a son

Older Children: How did Sarai try to supply this lack? gave Abraham Hagar as a wife

Younger Children: From what country did Hagar come? Egypt

Older Children: What fault did she commit? despised her mistress

Older Children: Why did she run away from her mistress Sarai? harsh treatment

Younger Children: Where did she go and where did the angel of the Lord find her? wilderness, spring

Younger Children: What did he tell her to do? go back and obey

Older Children: What did he say about her son? to father great nation

Younger Children: What was her son's name? Ishmael
