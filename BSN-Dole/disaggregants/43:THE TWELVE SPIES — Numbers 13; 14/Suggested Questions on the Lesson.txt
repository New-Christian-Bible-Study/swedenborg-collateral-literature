
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: When the Israelites left Sinai, to what place did they come which was near the Holy Land? Kadesh

Junior: Whom did they send out from there? twelve spies

Primary: What did the spies bring back from the Holy Land? grapes, pomegranates, figs

Primary: What did they report about the land? land of milk and honey

Junior: What did ten of the spies advise? people too strong for us

Junior: What two men reminded the people that the Lord would help them? Caleb and Joshua

Primary: Whose advice did the people take? that of ten cowards

Junior: What was their punishment? forty years wandering

Primary: Who were to be allowed to enter the Holy Land? those then under twenty, Caleb and Joshua

Junior: What happened to the ten spies who gave the bad advice? died of plague

Junior: How much longer did the people wander in the wilderness? approx. 38 years

Intermediate: What does the Holy Land picture? heaven, or the regenerate life

Senior: What is the meaning of the story of the spies? our desire to be reassured that the heavenly way of life is really happy and worthwhile
