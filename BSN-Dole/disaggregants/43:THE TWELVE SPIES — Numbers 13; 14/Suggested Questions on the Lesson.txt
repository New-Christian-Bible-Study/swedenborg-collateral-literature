
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): When the Israelites left Sinai, to what place did they come which was near the Holy Land? +
*A:* Kadesh

*Q:* (Older Children): Whom did they send out from there? +
*A:* twelve spies

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the spies bring back from the Holy Land? +
*A:* grapes, pomegranates, figs

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they report about the land? +
*A:* land of milk and honey

*Q:* (Older Children): What did ten of the spies advise? +
*A:* people too strong for us

*Q:* (Older Children): What two men reminded the people that the Lord would help them? +
*A:* Caleb and Joshua

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whose advice did the people take? +
*A:* that of ten cowards

*Q:* (Older Children): What was their punishment? +
*A:* forty years wandering

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who were to be allowed to enter the Holy Land? +
*A:* those then under twenty, Caleb and Joshua

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to the ten spies who gave the bad advice? +
*A:* died of plague

*Q:* (Older Children): How much longer did the people wander in the wilderness? +
*A:* approx. 38 years

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the Holy Land picture? +
*A:* heaven, or the regenerate life

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is the meaning of the story of the spies? +
*A:* our desire to be reassured that the heavenly way of life is really happy and worthwhile
