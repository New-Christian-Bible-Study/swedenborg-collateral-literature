
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Where was the Lord born? Bethlehem

Younger Children: Where was He taken to save Him from Herod? Egypt

Younger Children: Where was He brought up? Nazareth

Older Children: How old was He when He began His ministry? about thirty

Younger Children: Who was sent to prepare the way of the Lord? John the Baptist

Younger Children: How was John the Baptist dressed? camel hair garment, leather belt

Younger Children: What was his food? locusts, wild honey

Older Children: What was his message? Repent!

Older Children: Where did he baptize? Jordan

Older Children: What three kinds of baptism did he speak of? water, Holy Spirit, fire

Younger Children: Who came to John to be baptized? Jesus

Older Children: How did John feel about it? unworthy

Older Children: What did the Lord tell him? "It is fitting ... to fulfill all righteousness"

Younger Children: When the Lord was baptized, what was seen coming from heaven? dove

Younger Children: What did the voice from heaven say? "This is my beloved Son"

Younger Teens: What do the three kinds of baptism picture? repentance, reformation, regeneration

Older Teens: What are the three uses of baptism? (1) to fulfill righteousness, (2) to point way to repentance, (3) to establish Christian spiritual environment OR (1) that a person be called a Christian, (2) that he may know and follow the Lord, (3) that he may be regenerated
