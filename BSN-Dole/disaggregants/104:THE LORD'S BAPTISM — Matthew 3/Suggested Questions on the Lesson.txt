
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was the Lord born? +
*A:* Bethlehem

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was He taken to save Him from Herod? +
*A:* Egypt

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was He brought up? +
*A:* Nazareth

*Q:* (Older Children): How old was He when He began His ministry? +
*A:* about thirty

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was sent to prepare the way of the Lord? +
*A:* John the Baptist

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was John the Baptist dressed? +
*A:* camel hair garment, leather belt

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was his food? +
*A:* locusts, wild honey

*Q:* (Older Children): What was his message? +
*A:* Repent!

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did he baptize? +
*A:* Jordan

*Q:* (Older Children): What three kinds of baptism did he speak of? +
*A:* water, Holy Spirit, fire

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who came to John to be baptized? +
*A:* Jesus

*Q:* (Older Children): How did John feel about it? +
*A:* unworthy

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell him? +
*A:* "It is fitting ... to fulfill all righteousness"

*Q:* (Younger Children): When the Lord was baptized, what was seen coming from heaven? +
*A:* dove

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the voice from heaven say? +
*A:* "This is my beloved Son"

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do the three kinds of baptism picture? +
*A:* repentance, reformation, regeneration

*Q:* (Older Teens): What are the three uses of baptism? +
*A:* (1) to fulfill righteousness, (2) to point way to repentance, (3) to establish Christian spiritual environment OR (1) that a person be called a Christian, (2) that he may know and follow the Lord, (3) that he may be regenerated
