
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Where was the Lord born? Bethlehem

Primary: Where was He taken to save Him from Herod? Egypt

Primary: Where was He brought up? Nazareth

Junior: How old was He when He began His ministry? about thirty

Primary: Who was sent to prepare the way of the Lord? John the Baptist

Primary: How was John the Baptist dressed? camel hair garment, leather belt

Primary: What was his food? locusts, wild honey

Junior: What was his message? Repent!

Junior: Where did he baptize? Jordan

Junior: What three kinds of baptism did he speak of? water, Holy Spirit, fire

Primary: Who came to John to be baptized? Jesus

Junior: How did John feel about it? unworthy

Junior: What did the Lord tell him? "It is fitting ... to fulfill all righteousness"

Primary: When the Lord was baptized, what was seen coming from heaven? dove

Primary: What did the voice from heaven say? "This is my beloved Son"

Intermediate: What do the three kinds of baptism picture? repentance, reformation, regeneration

Senior: What are the three uses of baptism? (1) to fulfill righteousness, (2) to point way to repentance, (3) to establish Christian spiritual environment OR (1) that a person be called a Christian, (2) that he may know and follow the Lord, (3) that he may be regenerated
