
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Who were the four Major Prophets? Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

Primary: How many Minor Prophets were there? twelve

Junior: Why are they called "Minor"? wrote shorter books

Primary: Where do their books come in the Bible? end of old Testament

Junior: How do we know they were inspired? all claim to be the Word of the Lord

Primary: Which is the first of the Minor Prophets? Hosea

Primary: Which is the last? Malachi

Junior: Which one is Joel? second

Junior: How many chapters are there in his book? three

Primary: How does he describe the evils that work in our hearts? destructive insects

Junior: What does he call the judgment day? day of the Lord

Junior: What does the judgment day mean for us? when we die

Junior: What is the "valley of decision"? our everyday life

Junior: What does the day of the Lord bring to the evil? hell

Junior: What does it bring to the good? heaven

Intermediate: What would you say is the most important thing we learn from Joel? every day all of us are forming ourselves into the kinds of persons we will be forever
