
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What happened on Palm Sunday? +
*A:* the Lord's triumphal entry

*Q:* (Older Children): Why had the Lord come to Jerusalem? +
*A:* to observe Passover

*Q:* (Older Children): What new feast did the Lord establish as He ate His last Passover? +
*A:* Holy Supper

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did He say one of His twelve disciples would do? +
*A:* betray Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did each one ask? +
*A:* Is it I?

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which one betrayed the Lord? +
*A:* Judas Iscariot

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Judas betray Him? +
*A:* with a kiss

*Q:* (Older Children): What did all the disciples do when the Lord was captured? +
*A:* fled

*Q:* (Younger Children): What had the Lord told Peter he would do? +
*A:* deny Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Did Peter think he would deny the Lord? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): Did he do it? +

*Q:* (Younger Children): What made him remember what the Lord had said? +
*A:* cock crowed

*Q:* (Older Children): Before whom was the Lord first tried? +
*A:* Caiaphas, the high priest

*Q:* (Older Children): Who had to pronounce the final sentence? +
*A:* Pilate, the Roman governor

*Q:* (Older Children): What choice did Pilate give them? +
*A:* to free Jesus or Barabbas

*Q:* (Older Children): How was the Lord put to death? +
*A:* crucifixion

*Q:* (Older Children): What was done with His body? +
*A:* placed in a new tomb hewn in rock

*Q:* (Older Children): How did they try to guard against His resurrection? +
*A:* sealed tomb, set guard

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do the bread and wine in the Holy Supper represent? +
*A:* the Lord's divine love and wisdom

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the cross mean to us? +
*A:* a sign that evil and falsity have no real power against goodness and truth
