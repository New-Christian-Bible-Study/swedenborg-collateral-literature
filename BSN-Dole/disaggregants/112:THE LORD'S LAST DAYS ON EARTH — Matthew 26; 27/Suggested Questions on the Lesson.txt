
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Teens: What happened on Palm Sunday? the Lord's triumphal entry

Older Children: Why had the Lord come to Jerusalem? to observe Passover

Older Children: What new feast did the Lord establish as He ate His last Passover? Holy Supper

Younger Children: What did He say one of His twelve disciples would do? betray Him

Younger Children: What did each one ask? Is it I?

Younger Children: Which one betrayed the Lord? Judas Iscariot

Older Children: How did Judas betray Him? with a kiss

Older Children: What did all the disciples do when the Lord was captured? fled

Younger Children: What had the Lord told Peter he would do? deny Him

Younger Children: Did Peter think he would deny the Lord? no

Younger Children: Did he do it? yes â€” three times

Younger Children: What made him remember what the Lord had said? cock crowed

Older Children: Before whom was the Lord first tried? Caiaphas, the high priest

Older Children: Who had to pronounce the final sentence? Pilate, the Roman governor

Older Children: What choice did Pilate give them? to free Jesus or Barabbas

Older Children: How was the Lord put to death? crucifixion

Older Children: What was done with His body? placed in a new tomb hewn in rock

Older Children: How did they try to guard against His resurrection? sealed tomb, set guard

Younger Teens: What do the bread and wine in the Holy Supper represent? the Lord's divine love and wisdom

Older Teens: What does the cross mean to us? a sign that evil and falsity have no real power against goodness and truth
