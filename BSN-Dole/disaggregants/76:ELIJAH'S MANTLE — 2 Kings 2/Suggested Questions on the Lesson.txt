
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What happened to Solomon's kingdom after he died? divided

Younger Children: What were the two divisions of the kingdom called? Israel, Judah

Younger Children: Which remained faithful to the line of David? Judah

Younger Children: What two great prophets were sent to Israel? Elijah, Elisha

Younger Children: When Elijah's work was ended, what did Elisha ask? double portion of power

Younger Children: What did Elijah tell him? granted, if you see me go

Younger Children: What vision was granted to Elisha? fiery chariots and horses

Younger Children: What fell from Elijah as he was taken up to heaven? his mantle

Older Children: How did the sons of the prophets know that Elisha had become the successor of Elijah? saw him part waters of Jordan with mantle

Older Children: What miracle did Elisha perform for them? "healed" water

Older Children: What name did some children call Elisha? baldhead

Older Children: Why was this so bad? a sign of disgrace

Older Children: What happened to the children? mauled by bears

Younger Teens: What do the two parts of the divided kingdom represent? mind, heart

Younger Teens: What does the prophet's mantle represent? letter of Word

Older Teens: What is the difference in correspondence between (1) Elijah, and (2) Elisha? (1) Word studied from obedience (2) Word studied with awareness of inner sense
