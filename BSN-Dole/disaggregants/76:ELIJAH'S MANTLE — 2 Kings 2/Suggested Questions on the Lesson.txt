
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to Solomon's kingdom after he died? +
*A:* divided

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were the two divisions of the kingdom called? +
*A:* Israel, Judah

*Q:* (Younger Children): Which remained faithful to the line of David? +
*A:* Judah

*Q:* (Younger Children): What two great prophets were sent to Israel? +
*A:* Elijah, Elisha

*Q:* (Younger Children): When Elijah's work was ended, what did Elisha ask? +
*A:* double portion of power

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Elijah tell him? +
*A:* granted, if you see me go

*Q:* (Younger Children): What vision was granted to Elisha? +
*A:* fiery chariots and horses

*Q:* (Younger Children): What fell from Elijah as he was taken up to heaven? +
*A:* his mantle

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the sons of the prophets know that Elisha had become the successor of Elijah? +
*A:* saw him part waters of Jordan with mantle

*Q:* (Older Children): What miracle did Elisha perform for them? +
*A:* "healed" water

*Q:* (Older Children): What name did some children call Elisha? +
*A:* baldhead

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was this so bad? +
*A:* a sign of disgrace

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to the children? +
*A:* mauled by bears

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do the two parts of the divided kingdom represent? +
*A:* mind, heart

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the prophet's mantle represent? +
*A:* letter of Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is the difference in correspondence between (1) Elijah, and (2) Elisha? +
*A:* (1) Word studied from obedience (2) Word studied with awareness of inner sense
