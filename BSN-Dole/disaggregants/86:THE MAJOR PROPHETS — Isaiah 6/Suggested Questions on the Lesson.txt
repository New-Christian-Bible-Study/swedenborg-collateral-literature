
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What is a prophet? one who speaks for the Lord

Younger Children: About what great prophet to Israel have we had a story? Elijah

Older Children: What Judge was also a prophet? Samuel

Older Children: Into what three parts did the Hebrews divide their Bible? Law, Prophets, Writings

Older Children: What books did they include in their Prophets which we think of as historical books? Joshua, Judges, Samuel, Kings

Older Children: Into what two groups do we divide the Prophets? Major, Minor

Older Children: Why are they called Major and Minor? longer, shorter

Younger Children: How many Major Prophets are there? four

Younger Children: Can you name them? Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

Younger Children: About which one is our lesson today? Isaiah

Older Children: When and where did Isaiah prophesy? Judah, reigns of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, Hezekiah

Younger Children: Whom did Isaiah see in his vision? the Lord

Younger Children: What can you tell about the seraphim? six wings

Younger Children: Why was Isaiah frightened? "unclean" lips

Older Children: How did the Lord purify Isaiah's lips? live coal

Younger Children: What did the Lord ask? "Whom shall I send?"

Younger Children: What did Isaiah say? "Here am I; send me."

Younger Teens: What was the mission of the prophets? point out evils, call to repentance

Older Teens: What do we learn in this lesson are the qualities which we must have if we are to serve the Lord effectively? vision of Lord, humility, consecration, willingness to serve
