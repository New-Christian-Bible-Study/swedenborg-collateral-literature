
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is a prophet? +
*A:* one who speaks for the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): About what great prophet to Israel have we had a story? +
*A:* Elijah

*Q:* (Older Children): What Judge was also a prophet? +
*A:* Samuel

*Q:* (Older Children): Into what three parts did the Hebrews divide their Bible? +
*A:* Law, Prophets, Writings

*Q:* (Older Children): What books did they include in their Prophets which we think of as historical books? +
*A:* Joshua, Judges, Samuel, Kings

*Q:* (Older Children): Into what two groups do we divide the Prophets? +
*A:* Major, Minor

*Q:* (Older Children): Why are they called Major and Minor? +
*A:* longer, shorter

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many Major Prophets are there? +
*A:* four

*Q:* (Younger Children): Can you name them? +
*A:* Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

*Q:* (Younger Children): About which one is our lesson today? +
*A:* Isaiah

*Q:* (Older Children): When and where did Isaiah prophesy? +
*A:* Judah, reigns of Uzziah, Jotham, Ahaz, Hezekiah

*Q:* (Younger Children): Whom did Isaiah see in his vision? +
*A:* the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): What can you tell about the seraphim? +
*A:* six wings

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why was Isaiah frightened? +
*A:* "unclean" lips

*Q:* (Older Children): How did the Lord purify Isaiah's lips? +
*A:* live coal

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord ask? +
*A:* "Whom shall I send?"

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Isaiah say? +
*A:* "Here am I; send me."

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What was the mission of the prophets? +
*A:* point out evils, call to repentance

*Q:* (Older Teens): What do we learn in this lesson are the qualities which we must have if we are to serve the Lord effectively? +
*A:* vision of Lord, humility, consecration, willingness to serve
