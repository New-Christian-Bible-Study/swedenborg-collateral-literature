
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was Samaria? +
*A:* middle of land

*Q:* (Older Children): Who were the Samaritans? +
*A:* foreigners

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did the Lord stop to rest on His way from Jerusalem to Galilee? +
*A:* Jacob's well

*Q:* (Older Children): Why was the Samaritan woman surprised when He asked her to give Him a drink? +
*A:* Jews didn't talk to Samaritans

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell her about water? +
*A:* you should have asked ...

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell her about Himself? +
*A:* I am Christ

*Q:* (Older Children): What did He tell His disciples about His "food"? +
*A:* to do God's will

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the Samaritans receive Him? +
*A:* many believed

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was the pool of Bethesda? +
*A:* Jerusalem

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many porches did it have? +
*A:* five

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who waited in the porches? +
*A:* sick people

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were they waiting for? +
*A:* "troubling" of water

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened whenever the water was troubled? +
*A:* first to step in was healed

*Q:* (Older Children): How long had one man at the pool been sick? +
*A:* thirty-eight years

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord first ask him? +
*A:* do you want to be healed?

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he answer? +
*A:* no one to help me

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord tell him to do? +
*A:* rise, take bed, walk

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened when he tried? +
*A:* he was healed

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the religious leaders tell him? +
*A:* he was breaking the sabbath

*Q:* (Older Children): When the Lord met him again, what did He say to him? +
*A:* sin no more

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the leaders try to do when they learned who had healed the man? +
*A:* kill Jesus

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the pool of Bethesda in us? +
*A:* knowledge of God's way, stored in memory

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is meant by the words, "Rise, take up thy bed, and walk"? +
*A:* elevate your thoughts, take the doctrine on which you base your life, and move forward
