
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Where was Samaria? middle of land

Junior: Who were the Samaritans? foreigners

Primary: Where did the Lord stop to rest on His way from Jerusalem to Galilee? Jacob's well

Junior: Why was the Samaritan woman surprised when He asked her to give Him a drink? Jews didn't talk to Samaritans

Junior: What did He tell her about water? you should have asked ...

Junior: What did He tell her about Himself? I am Christ

Junior: What did He tell His disciples about His "food"? to do God's will

Primary: How did the Samaritans receive Him? many believed

Junior: Where was the pool of Bethesda? Jerusalem

Primary: How many porches did it have? five

Primary: Who waited in the porches? sick people

Primary: What were they waiting for? "troubling" of water

Primary: What happened whenever the water was troubled? first to step in was healed

Junior: How long had one man at the pool been sick? thirty-eight years

Junior: What did the Lord first ask him? do you want to be healed?

Primary: What did he answer? no one to help me

Primary: What did the Lord tell him to do? rise, take bed, walk

Primary: What happened when he tried? he was healed

Junior: What did the religious leaders tell him? he was breaking the sabbath

Junior: When the Lord met him again, what did He say to him? sin no more

Junior: What did the leaders try to do when they learned who had healed the man? kill Jesus

Intermediate: What is the pool of Bethesda in us? knowledge of God's way, stored in memory

Senior: What is meant by the words, "Rise, take up thy bed, and walk"? elevate your thoughts, take the doctrine on which you base your life, and move forward
