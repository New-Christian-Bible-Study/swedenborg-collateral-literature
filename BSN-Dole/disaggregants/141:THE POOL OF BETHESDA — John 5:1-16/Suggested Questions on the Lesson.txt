
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Where was Samaria? middle of land

Older Children: Who were the Samaritans? foreigners

Younger Children: Where did the Lord stop to rest on His way from Jerusalem to Galilee? Jacob's well

Older Children: Why was the Samaritan woman surprised when He asked her to give Him a drink? Jews didn't talk to Samaritans

Older Children: What did He tell her about water? you should have asked ...

Older Children: What did He tell her about Himself? I am Christ

Older Children: What did He tell His disciples about His "food"? to do God's will

Younger Children: How did the Samaritans receive Him? many believed

Older Children: Where was the pool of Bethesda? Jerusalem

Younger Children: How many porches did it have? five

Younger Children: Who waited in the porches? sick people

Younger Children: What were they waiting for? "troubling" of water

Younger Children: What happened whenever the water was troubled? first to step in was healed

Older Children: How long had one man at the pool been sick? thirty-eight years

Older Children: What did the Lord first ask him? do you want to be healed?

Younger Children: What did he answer? no one to help me

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him to do? rise, take bed, walk

Younger Children: What happened when he tried? he was healed

Older Children: What did the religious leaders tell him? he was breaking the sabbath

Older Children: When the Lord met him again, what did He say to him? sin no more

Older Children: What did the leaders try to do when they learned who had healed the man? kill Jesus

Younger Teens: What is the pool of Bethesda in us? knowledge of God's way, stored in memory

Older Teens: What is meant by the words, "Rise, take up thy bed, and walk"? elevate your thoughts, take the doctrine on which you base your life, and move forward
