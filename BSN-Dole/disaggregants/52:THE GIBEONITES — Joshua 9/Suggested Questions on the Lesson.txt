
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What did Joshua set up at Gilgal? twelve stones

Older Children: What feast did the Israelites celebrate there? Passover

Older Children: What change in their diet was made there? manna ceased

Younger Children: Which one of the enemies in the Holy Land wanted to make peace with Joshua? Gibeonites

Younger Children: How did they deceive him? pretended to be from far away

Older Children: In what way was Joshua careless? failed to consult the Lord

Older Children: What promise did he make to the Gibeonites? not to kill them

Younger Children: When he found out that they had deceived him, did he break his promise? no

Older Children: How did he decide they should be treated? made to cut wood, haul water

Older Children: What does this story teach us about promises? shouldn't make hasty ones

Younger Teens: What was the ancestry of the Gibeonites? Hivites (from Ham, son of Noah)

Older Teens: What do they represent? intellectual curiosity

Younger Teens: What is pictured by their (1) patched garments, and (2) moldy bread? (1) ideas of conduct (2) spirituality

Older Teens: What is pictured by their being made hewers of wood and drawers of water? using intellectual curiosity to get accurate knowledge of ways to benefit the world
