
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Whose Book is the Bible? God's Word

Younger Children: What is the name of the first book in the Bible? Genesis

Older Children: What does Genesis mean? the beginning

Older Children: What is the first chapter about? creation

Younger Children: In how many days was everything created? six

Older Children: What was the last thing created? man

Younger Teens: In whose image and likeness was man created? God's

Older Children: What did the Lord do on the seventh day? rested

Younger Children: Where did the Lord place the first people? Garden of Eden

Younger Children: What name is given to them in our chapter? Adam

Older Children: What does Adam mean? mankind

Older Children: What was Adam told to do in the Garden of Eden? dress it and keep it

Younger Children: What tree was in the midst of the garden? life

Younger Children: How was the garden watered? river

Younger Children: Into how many streams was the river divided? four

Older Children: Of what tree were the people told not to eat? knowledge of good and evil

Older Children: To what did Adam give names? animals, birds

Older Children: What did the Lord take from Adam while he slept? rib

Older Children: Into what did He make the rib? woman

Younger Teens: What does the Garden of Eden picture? state of nearness to and complete trust in the Lord

Younger Teens: What is symbolized by the river? truth as it comes from the Lord into our minds

Older Teens: What do the two trees represent?
- life = principle that we live from the Lord
- knowledge = ability to think of self as independent from God; hence that we can know what is good and wise without God's help
