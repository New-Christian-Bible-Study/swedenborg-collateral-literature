
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Whose Book is the Bible? God's Word

Primary: What is the name of the first book in the Bible? Genesis

Junior: What does Genesis mean? the beginning

Junior: What is the first chapter about? creation

Primary: In how many days was everything created? six

Junior: What was the last thing created? man

Intermediate: In whose image and likeness was man created? God's

Junior: What did the Lord do on the seventh day? rested

Primary: Where did the Lord place the first people? Garden of Eden

Primary: What name is given to them in our chapter? Adam

Junior: What does Adam mean? mankind

Junior: What was Adam told to do in the Garden of Eden? dress it and keep it

Primary: What tree was in the midst of the garden? life

Primary: How was the garden watered? river

Primary: Into how many streams was the river divided? four

Junior: Of what tree were the people told not to eat? knowledge of good and evil

Junior: To what did Adam give names? animals, birds

Junior: What did the Lord take from Adam while he slept? rib

Junior: Into what did He make the rib? woman

Intermediate: What does the Garden of Eden picture? state of nearness to and complete trust in the Lord

Intermediate: What is symbolized by the river? truth as it comes from the Lord into our minds

Senior: What do the two trees represent?
- life = principle that we live from the Lord
- knowledge = ability to think of self as independent from God; hence that we can know what is good and wise without God's help
