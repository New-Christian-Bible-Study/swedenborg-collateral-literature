
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Can you repeat the first verse of the Gospel of John?

*Q:* (Older Children): Why do we call the inspired Scripture the Word of God? +
*A:* it is God's Word to us

*Q:* (Older Children): Why does John call Jesus "the Word made flesh"? +
*A:* He is living truth

*Q:* (Older Children): What is meant by saying that "in him was life, and the life was the light of men"? +
*A:* His truth is alive, and enables us to live spiritually

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the Lord have to come into the world? +
*A:* to save us from our sins

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who bore witness to His being the Messiah? +
*A:* John the Baptist

*Q:* (Younger Children): What sign was given to John the Baptist? +
*A:* dove

*Q:* (Older Children): What disciple first met the Lord? +
*A:* Andrew

*Q:* (Older Children): What other disciples are mentioned in the first chapter of John? +
*A:* Peter, Philip, Nathanael (Bartholomew)

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was the Lord born? +
*A:* Bethlehem

*Q:* (Older Children): In what division of the Holy Land was Bethlehem? +
*A:* Judea

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did the Lord grow up? +
*A:* Nazareth

*Q:* (Older Children): In what division of the land was Nazareth? +
*A:* Galilee

*Q:* (Older Children): What division lay between Judea and Galilee? +
*A:* Samaria

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the Lord live during the three years of His ministry? +
*A:* Capernaum

*Q:* (Older Children): What is a miracle? +
*A:* a wonderful thing done by the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was the Lord's first miracle performed? +
*A:* Cana

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did He go to Cana? +
*A:* a wedding

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was lacking at the feast? +
*A:* wine

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who went to the Lord for help? +
*A:* Mary

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord first tell the servants to do? +
*A:* fill waterpots

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened? +
*A:* water turned into wine

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the governor of the feast say about this wine? +
*A:* it was best

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does marriage represent? +
*A:* uniting goodness and truth in life

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do (1) water, and (2) wine represent? +
*A:* (1) literal truth, (2) spiritual truth

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did the Lord perform miracles? +
*A:* to strengthen the faith of His followers, and to symbolize the things He can do for our souls
