
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What story in the Bible tells of the end of the second church on earth? Tower of Babel

Older Children: What happened to its people? scattered

Younger Children: What family was chosen by the Lord to begin a new church? Abram (Eber, Terah)

Younger Children: Where did they live? Ur, Chaldea

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell them to do? go to Canaan

Younger Children: Where did some of them stop? Haran

Younger Children: Who went on? Abram, Sarai and Lot

Younger Children: In what two places in Canaan did Abraham build altars? Shechem, Bethel

Younger Children: Why did he go down into Egypt? famine

Older Children: Where did he settle when he came back? Hebron

Older Children: What promise did the Lord make to Abraham about his descendants? to be as sand of sea

Younger Children: What one necessary thing did Abraham lack? son

Older Children: How did Sarah try to make up for this lack? gave Hagar as wife

Younger Children: What was the name of Hagar's son? Ishmael

Older Children: What did the Lord tell Abraham about Ishmael? not your heir

Younger Children: How old was Abraham when Sarah's own son was born? hundred years

Younger Children: What was Sarah's child named? Isaac

Younger Children: What did Ishmael do which displeased Sarah? mocked Isaac

Younger Children: What did Sarah tell Abraham to do? send them away

Older Children: What did the Lord tell him? do as Sarah said

Older Children: Where did Hagar and Ishmael go? desert

Older Children: What did the angel promise them? "I will make him a great nation"

Older Children: What did Ishmael become? archer

Older Children: From what country was his wife? Egypt

Younger Teens: What does Egypt represent? memory-knowledge

Older Teens: What do Ishmael and Isaac represent? (1) natural or first rational (2) spiritual or second rational
