
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What story in the Bible tells of the end of the second church on earth? Tower of Babel

Junior: What happened to its people? scattered

Primary: What family was chosen by the Lord to begin a new church? Abram (Eber, Terah)

Primary: Where did they live? Ur, Chaldea

Primary: What did the Lord tell them to do? go to Canaan

Primary: Where did some of them stop? Haran

Primary: Who went on? Abram, Sarai and Lot

Primary: In what two places in Canaan did Abraham build altars? Shechem, Bethel

Primary: Why did he go down into Egypt? famine

Junior: Where did he settle when he came back? Hebron

Junior: What promise did the Lord make to Abraham about his descendants? to be as sand of sea

Primary: What one necessary thing did Abraham lack? son

Junior: How did Sarah try to make up for this lack? gave Hagar as wife

Primary: What was the name of Hagar's son? Ishmael

Junior: What did the Lord tell Abraham about Ishmael? not your heir

Primary: How old was Abraham when Sarah's own son was born? hundred years

Primary: What was Sarah's child named? Isaac

Primary: What did Ishmael do which displeased Sarah? mocked Isaac

Primary: What did Sarah tell Abraham to do? send them away

Junior: What did the Lord tell him? do as Sarah said

Junior: Where did Hagar and Ishmael go? desert

Junior: What did the angel promise them? "I will make him a great nation"

Junior: What did Ishmael become? archer

Junior: From what country was his wife? Egypt

Intermediate: What does Egypt represent? memory-knowledge

Senior: What do Ishmael and Isaac represent? (1) natural or first rational (2) spiritual or second rational
