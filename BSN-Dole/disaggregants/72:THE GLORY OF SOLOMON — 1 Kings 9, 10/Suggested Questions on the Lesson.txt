
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How long was David's reign? forty years

Younger Children: What was the name of the king after David? Solomon

Younger Children: What does the name Solomon mean? peace

Older Children: What did Solomon ask of the Lord? wisdom

Older Children: What did the Lord promise him in addition? riches and honor

Older Children: What did Solomon build which David had been forbidden to build? temple

Older Children: What else did Solomon build? houses for self, wife

Older Children: Who furnished him cedar wood and skilled workmen? Hiram

Older Children: What did Solomon give Hiram? twenty cities

Older Children: Who was Solomon's first wife? Pharaoh's daughter

Older Children: What did Solomon have which the Israelites had never had before? navy

Younger Children: What queen came to visit Solomon and why did she come? Sheba, to prove him with hard questions

Younger Children: What did she say about him after she had talked with him? wiser than reported
