
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): How long was David's reign? +
*A:* forty years

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the name of the king after David? +
*A:* Solomon

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does the name Solomon mean? +
*A:* peace

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Solomon ask of the Lord? +
*A:* wisdom

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord promise him in addition? +
*A:* riches and honor

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Solomon build which David had been forbidden to build? +
*A:* temple

*Q:* (Older Children): What else did Solomon build? +
*A:* houses for self, wife

*Q:* (Older Children): Who furnished him cedar wood and skilled workmen? +
*A:* Hiram

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Solomon give Hiram? +
*A:* twenty cities

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was Solomon's first wife? +
*A:* Pharaoh's daughter

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Solomon have which the Israelites had never had before? +
*A:* navy

*Q:* (Younger Children): What queen came to visit Solomon and why did she come? +
*A:* Sheba, to prove him with hard questions

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did she say about him after she had talked with him? +
*A:* wiser than reported
