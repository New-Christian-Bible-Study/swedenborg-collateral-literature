
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Is the book of Jonah one of the Major or one of the Minor Prophets? +
*A:* Minor

*Q:* (Older Children): When and where did Jonah live? +
*A:* shortly after Elisha, in Israel

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did he try to run away from the Lord? +
*A:* too proud to warn Nineveh

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to him? +
*A:* thrown overboard, swallowed by great fish

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was Nineveh? +
*A:* Assyria

*Q:* (Older Children): When Jonah went to Nineveh, what did he tell the people? +
*A:* forty days till doom

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did his prophecy not come true? +
*A:* the people repented

*Q:* (Younger Children): Was Jonah pleased? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where did he go? +
*A:* edge of town

*Q:* (Younger Children): What grew up to give him shade? +
*A:* "gourd"

*Q:* (Younger Children): What came to destroy the gourd? +
*A:* worm

*Q:* (Younger Children): Was Jonah angry? +
*A:* yes

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell him? +
*A:* he should have as great pity for people

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the gourd represent? +
*A:* arrogant pride, sense of superiority

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the worm represent? +
*A:* false ideas associated with pride

*Q:* (Older Teens): Do people today ever behave as Jonah did? +
*A:* Explain. yes, every time we feel our ways and our ideas must be better than those of anyone who is "different"
