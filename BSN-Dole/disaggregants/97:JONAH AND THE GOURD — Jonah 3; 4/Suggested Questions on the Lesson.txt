
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Is the book of Jonah one of the Major or one of the Minor Prophets? Minor

Older Children: When and where did Jonah live? shortly after Elisha, in Israel

Older Children: Why did he try to run away from the Lord? too proud to warn Nineveh

Younger Children: What happened to him? thrown overboard, swallowed by great fish

Older Children: Where was Nineveh? Assyria

Older Children: When Jonah went to Nineveh, what did he tell the people? forty days till doom

Older Children: Why did his prophecy not come true? the people repented

Younger Children: Was Jonah pleased? no

Younger Children: Where did he go? edge of town

Younger Children: What grew up to give him shade? "gourd"

Younger Children: What came to destroy the gourd? worm

Younger Children: Was Jonah angry? yes

Older Children: What did the Lord tell him? he should have as great pity for people

Younger Teens: What does the gourd represent? arrogant pride, sense of superiority

Older Teens: What does the worm represent? false ideas associated with pride

Older Teens: Do people today ever behave as Jonah did? Explain. yes, every time we feel our ways and our ideas must be better than those of anyone who is "different"
