
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What became of the people of Judah? taken to Babylon

Primary: What young prince among the captives wrote a book of the Bible? Daniel

Junior: What were the names of his three companions? Shadrach, Meshach, Abednego

Junior: Why did these four young men attract special notice? refused rich food

Junior: Did their faithfulness to the Lord do them harm? no

Junior: Who was king of Babylon at the time of our story? Nebuchadnezzar

Primary: What did Nebuchadnezzar make? gold image

Primary: What command did he give? when you hear the music, bow down

Primary: What did Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego do? refused

Primary: What did Nebuchadnezzar do to them? threw them in a furnace

Primary: Were they burned? no

Primary: Who appeared in the fiery furnace with them? the Lord

Junior: What happened to the men who had cast them into the furnace? killed

Junior: What order did Nebuchadnezzar afterward give? no one speak against the Lord

Intermediate: What does Babylon represent? love of having one's own way

Senior: What does Nebuchadnezzar represent? self-praise and pride in our own wisdom
