
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What became of the people of Judah? +
*A:* taken to Babylon

*Q:* (Younger Children): What young prince among the captives wrote a book of the Bible? +
*A:* Daniel

*Q:* (Older Children): What were the names of his three companions? +
*A:* Shadrach, Meshach, Abednego

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did these four young men attract special notice? +
*A:* refused rich food

*Q:* (Older Children): Did their faithfulness to the Lord do them harm? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was king of Babylon at the time of our story? +
*A:* Nebuchadnezzar

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Nebuchadnezzar make? +
*A:* gold image

*Q:* (Younger Children): What command did he give? +
*A:* when you hear the music, bow down

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego do? +
*A:* refused

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Nebuchadnezzar do to them? +
*A:* threw them in a furnace

*Q:* (Younger Children): Were they burned? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who appeared in the fiery furnace with them? +
*A:* the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): What happened to the men who had cast them into the furnace? +
*A:* killed

*Q:* (Older Children): What order did Nebuchadnezzar afterward give? +
*A:* no one speak against the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Babylon represent? +
*A:* love of having one's own way

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does Nebuchadnezzar represent? +
*A:* self-praise and pride in our own wisdom
