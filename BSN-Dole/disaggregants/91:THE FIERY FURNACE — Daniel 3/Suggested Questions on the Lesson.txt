
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What became of the people of Judah? taken to Babylon

Younger Children: What young prince among the captives wrote a book of the Bible? Daniel

Older Children: What were the names of his three companions? Shadrach, Meshach, Abednego

Older Children: Why did these four young men attract special notice? refused rich food

Older Children: Did their faithfulness to the Lord do them harm? no

Older Children: Who was king of Babylon at the time of our story? Nebuchadnezzar

Younger Children: What did Nebuchadnezzar make? gold image

Younger Children: What command did he give? when you hear the music, bow down

Younger Children: What did Shadrach, Meshach, and Abednego do? refused

Younger Children: What did Nebuchadnezzar do to them? threw them in a furnace

Younger Children: Were they burned? no

Younger Children: Who appeared in the fiery furnace with them? the Lord

Older Children: What happened to the men who had cast them into the furnace? killed

Older Children: What order did Nebuchadnezzar afterward give? no one speak against the Lord

Younger Teens: What does Babylon represent? love of having one's own way

Older Teens: What does Nebuchadnezzar represent? self-praise and pride in our own wisdom
