
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: How did Jacob take Esau's place? bought birthright, stole blessing

Older Children: Why did Jacob have to leave home? Esau's threat

Younger Children: Where did he go? Haran

Older Children: With whom did he take up employment? Laban

Younger Children: What two daughters did Laban have? Leah and Rachel

Younger Children: Which one did Jacob love? Rachel

Older Children: Which one was given him first? Leah

Younger Children: How long did Jacob stay in Haran? twenty years

Younger Children: How many sons did he have? twelve

Older Children: Which ones were Rachel's children? Joseph and Benjamin

Younger Children: Which was the last one born in Haran? Joseph

Younger Children: Where was Benjamin born? near Bethlehem

Older Teens: What do Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob represent? will, thought, act

Younger Teens: What does the well at Haran represent? the Word

Older Teens: What do Leah and Rachel represent? exterior affection for truth, interior affection for truth

Older Teens: What do the twelve sons of Jacob represent? all aspects of good and truth
