
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: How did Jacob take Esau's place? bought birthright, stole blessing

Junior: Why did Jacob have to leave home? Esau's threat

Primary: Where did he go? Haran

Junior: With whom did he take up employment? Laban

Primary: What two daughters did Laban have? Leah and Rachel

Primary: Which one did Jacob love? Rachel

Junior: Which one was given him first? Leah

Primary: How long did Jacob stay in Haran? twenty years

Primary: How many sons did he have? twelve

Junior: Which ones were Rachel's children? Joseph and Benjamin

Primary: Which was the last one born in Haran? Joseph

Primary: Where was Benjamin born? near Bethlehem

Senior: What do Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob represent? will, thought, act

Intermediate: What does the well at Haran represent? the Word

Senior: What do Leah and Rachel represent? exterior affection for truth, interior affection for truth

Senior: What do the twelve sons of Jacob represent? all aspects of good and truth
