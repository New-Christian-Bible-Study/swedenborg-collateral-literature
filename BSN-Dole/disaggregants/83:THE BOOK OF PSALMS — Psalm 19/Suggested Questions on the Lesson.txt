
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What book of the Bible are we studying today? +
*A:* Psalms

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is a psalm? +
*A:* a song

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What did the Lord tell His disciples about the Psalms? +
*A:* they told about Him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Through whom were many of them written? +
*A:* David

*Q:* (Older Children): How were they used by the Hebrews? +
*A:* hymns

*Q:* (Older Children): What do they teach us? +
*A:* ways of expressing our feelings about the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is the first verse of Psalm 19? +
*A:* "The heavens declare ... "

*Q:* (Older Children): What do you think this means? +
*A:* refers to our awareness of the perfection of creation

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is said in this Psalm about the law of the Lord? +
*A:* " ... perfect, converting the soul"

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is meant by the "fear of the Lord"? +
*A:* desire not to displease Him

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the last part of the Psalm about? +
*A:* happiness in doing right, prayer to continue in this state

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does that mean for us? +
*A:* our need to ask the Lord's help to overcome our faults
