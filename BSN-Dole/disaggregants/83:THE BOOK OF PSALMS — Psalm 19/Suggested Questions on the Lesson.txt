
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What book of the Bible are we studying today? Psalms

Younger Children: What is a psalm? a song

Younger Teens: What did the Lord tell His disciples about the Psalms? they told about Him

Younger Children: Through whom were many of them written? David

Older Children: How were they used by the Hebrews? hymns

Older Children: What do they teach us? ways of expressing our feelings about the Lord

Younger Children: What is the first verse of Psalm 19? "The heavens declare ... "

Older Children: What do you think this means? refers to our awareness of the perfection of creation

Younger Teens: What is said in this Psalm about the law of the Lord? " ... perfect, converting the soul"

Older Teens: What is meant by the "fear of the Lord"? desire not to displease Him

Younger Teens: What is the last part of the Psalm about? happiness in doing right, prayer to continue in this state

Older Teens: What does that mean for us? our need to ask the Lord's help to overcome our faults
