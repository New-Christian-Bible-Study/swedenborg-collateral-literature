
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the condition of the Hebrews when they first settled in Egypt? +
*A:* honored guests

*Q:* (Older Children): How did it change as the years went on? +
*A:* became slaves

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the Pharaoh of our lesson today afraid of? +
*A:* numerical strength of Hebrews

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did he try to keep the Hebrews from increasing? +
*A:* ordered death of newborn sons

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the name of the baby boy who was saved? +
*A:* Moses

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did his mother do in order to save him? +
*A:* made "ark," hid in rushes

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who found him? +
*A:* Pharaoh's daughter

*Q:* (Older Children): What did she decide to do? +
*A:* adopt him

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was called to nurse Moses? +
*A:* Moses' mother

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Egypt represent? +
*A:* state of acquiring knowledge

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by the bondage in Egypt? +
*A:* becoming enslaved to worldly knowledge or principles: e.g., business or pleasure

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Moses represent? +
*A:* divine law
