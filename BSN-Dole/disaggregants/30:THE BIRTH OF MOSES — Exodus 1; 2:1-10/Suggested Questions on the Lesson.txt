
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What was the condition of the Hebrews when they first settled in Egypt? honored guests

Junior: How did it change as the years went on? became slaves

Primary: What was the Pharaoh of our lesson today afraid of? numerical strength of Hebrews

Primary: How did he try to keep the Hebrews from increasing? ordered death of newborn sons

Primary: What was the name of the baby boy who was saved? Moses

Primary: What did his mother do in order to save him? made "ark," hid in rushes

Primary: Who found him? Pharaoh's daughter

Junior: What did she decide to do? adopt him

Primary: Who was called to nurse Moses? Moses' mother

Intermediate: What does Egypt represent? state of acquiring knowledge

Senior: What is pictured by the bondage in Egypt? becoming enslaved to worldly knowledge or principles: e.g., business or pleasure

Intermediate: What does Moses represent? divine law
