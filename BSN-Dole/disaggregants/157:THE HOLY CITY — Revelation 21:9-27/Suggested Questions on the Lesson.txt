
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What is our lesson for today about? the holy city

Junior: Where did the city come from? God, out of heaven

Primary: What was its name? New Jerusalem

Primary: What was it made of? gold

Primary: How many gates did it have? twelve

Junior: What names were written on them? tribes of Israel

Junior: What was the wall of the city made of? jasper

Primary: How many foundations did it have? twelve

Junior: What were written on the foundations? apostles

Intermediate: What were they garnished with? jewels

Primary: What was the street of the city made of? gold

Junior: What is said about the temple in the city? the Lord is the temple

Junior: Where did its light come from? the glory of God

Junior: Who could not enter the city? evil people

Intermediate: Who could enter it? those written in Lamb's book of life

Intermediate: What does it mean to be written in the Lamb's book of life? to have lived a good life

Primary: What did the angel measure the city with? gold rod or reed

Junior: What were its proportions found to be? "four square"

Junior: Why was its measure called "the measure of a man, that is, of the angel"? good person is "four square"

Intermediate: Why should we in the New Church especially love this vision? it pictures the ideal church

Intermediate: What is pictured by the fact that the city and its street were of pure gold, like clear glass? enables one to walk in loving ways, clearly understood

Senior: What should we say when people ask to what church we belong? Church of the New Jerusalem

Senior: What should we say if they appear to be puzzled by the name? see Revelation 21-22
