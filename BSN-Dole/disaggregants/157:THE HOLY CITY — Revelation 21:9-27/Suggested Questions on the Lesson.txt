
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What is our lesson for today about? the holy city

Older Children: Where did the city come from? God, out of heaven

Younger Children: What was its name? New Jerusalem

Younger Children: What was it made of? gold

Younger Children: How many gates did it have? twelve

Older Children: What names were written on them? tribes of Israel

Older Children: What was the wall of the city made of? jasper

Younger Children: How many foundations did it have? twelve

Older Children: What were written on the foundations? apostles

Younger Teens: What were they garnished with? jewels

Younger Children: What was the street of the city made of? gold

Older Children: What is said about the temple in the city? the Lord is the temple

Older Children: Where did its light come from? the glory of God

Older Children: Who could not enter the city? evil people

Younger Teens: Who could enter it? those written in Lamb's book of life

Younger Teens: What does it mean to be written in the Lamb's book of life? to have lived a good life

Younger Children: What did the angel measure the city with? gold rod or reed

Older Children: What were its proportions found to be? "four square"

Older Children: Why was its measure called "the measure of a man, that is, of the angel"? good person is "four square"

Younger Teens: Why should we in the New Church especially love this vision? it pictures the ideal church

Younger Teens: What is pictured by the fact that the city and its street were of pure gold, like clear glass? enables one to walk in loving ways, clearly understood

Older Teens: What should we say when people ask to what church we belong? Church of the New Jerusalem

Older Teens: What should we say if they appear to be puzzled by the name? see Revelation 21-22
