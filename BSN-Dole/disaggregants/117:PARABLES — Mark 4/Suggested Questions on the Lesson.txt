
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What is a parable? a story with a hidden meaning

Younger Teens: How many parables are there in the Word? see footnote* in Intermediate section

Younger Teens: Why does the Lord teach us in parables? we can see truth better if it doesn't seem at first to apply to us

Younger Teens: What are three of the parables in our lesson about? seed

Younger Teens: What is the seed? truth from God's Word

Younger Teens: What are the different kinds of ground? different kinds of minds

Younger Children: What does the Lord say about a lamp? should be put on a stand

Younger Teens: What does light symbolize? truth which enlightens

Older Teens: How should we use the truth? to help see our own faults

Younger Teens: What happens if we do not use it? we lose it (lamp goes out)

Younger Teens: By what degrees does grain develop? first blade, then ear, then full grain

Younger Children: Who makes it grow? God

Younger Children: What does the Lord say of a grain of mustard seed? smallest of seeds

Younger Teens: What does it become? great bush or tree

Older Teens: How is this like the development of the truths we learn from the Word? at first some truths don't mean much to us, but later we may see real meaning
