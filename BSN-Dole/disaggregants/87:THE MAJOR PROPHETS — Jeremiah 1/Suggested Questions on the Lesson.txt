
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Where did the prophets get their messages? from the Lord, in visions or dreams

Older Children: What work were they called to do? warn people about their evils, exhort them to do good

Older Children: Can you name the four Major Prophets? Isaiah, Jeremiah, Ezekiel, Daniel

Older Children: In what time did Isaiah prophesy? reign of Hezekiah

Older Children: When did Jeremiah begin to prophesy? Josiah's reign

Younger Children: What did he say when the Lord called him? I cannot speak: for I am a child

Younger Children: What did the Lord tell him? I will put my words in your mouth

Older Children: What two kinds of work was he to do? tear down, build up

Younger Children: What was the first vision he had? "rod" of almond

Older Children: What did the Lord tell him it meant? "I shall hasten to perform my work"

Younger Children: What was his second vision? boiling pot in the north

Older Children: What did it mean? evil was to come from the north

Older Children: What great tragedy did he witness? Babylonian invasion

Older Children: What is the book of Lamentations about? mourning the fall of Jerusalem

Older Children: In what country did Jeremiah do most of his work? Judah

Older Children: In what other country did he later prophesy? Egypt

Older Children: How did he happen to go to Egypt? taken against his will by rebellious people

Older Children: Where did Ezekiel and Daniel prophesy? Babylon

Older Children: How did they get to Babylon? taken with captives
