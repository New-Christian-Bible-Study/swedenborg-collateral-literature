
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What evil did Jeroboam commit? +
*A:* set up two gold calves

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did he set up the golden calves? +
*A:* afraid people would return to Rehoboam

*Q:* (Older Children): Where were they set up? +
*A:* Bethel and Dan

*Q:* (Younger Children): What two great prophets did the Lord send to Israel? +
*A:* Elijah and Elisha

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our lesson for today what nation is troubling Israel? +
*A:* Syria

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the captain of the army of the king of Syria? +
*A:* Naaman

*Q:* (Younger Children): What disease did he have? +
*A:* leprosy

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who told him how he might be cured? +
*A:* captive Hebrew girl

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did Elisha tell Naaman to do? +
*A:* wash in Jordan seven times

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Naaman at first say? +
*A:* rivers of Damascus were better

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened when he obeyed Elisha? +
*A:* he was cured

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he ask Elisha to give him? +
*A:* two loads of dirt

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Elisha's servant do? +
*A:* took a reward

*Q:* (Older Children): What was his punishment? +
*A:* leprosy

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do Israel and Judah represent? +
*A:* mind and body separated

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does washing in Jordan seven times represent? +
*A:* consistently obeying the commandments
