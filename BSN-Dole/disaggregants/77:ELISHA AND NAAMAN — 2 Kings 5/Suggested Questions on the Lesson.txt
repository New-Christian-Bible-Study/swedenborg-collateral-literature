
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What evil did Jeroboam commit? set up two gold calves

Older Children: Why did he set up the golden calves? afraid people would return to Rehoboam

Older Children: Where were they set up? Bethel and Dan

Younger Children: What two great prophets did the Lord send to Israel? Elijah and Elisha

Younger Children: In our lesson for today what nation is troubling Israel? Syria

Younger Children: Who was the captain of the army of the king of Syria? Naaman

Younger Children: What disease did he have? leprosy

Younger Children: Who told him how he might be cured? captive Hebrew girl

Younger Children: What did Elisha tell Naaman to do? wash in Jordan seven times

Older Children: What did Naaman at first say? rivers of Damascus were better

Younger Children: What happened when he obeyed Elisha? he was cured

Older Children: What did he ask Elisha to give him? two loads of dirt

Older Children: What did Elisha's servant do? took a reward

Older Children: What was his punishment? leprosy

Younger Teens: What do Israel and Judah represent? mind and body separated

Older Teens: What does washing in Jordan seven times represent? consistently obeying the commandments
