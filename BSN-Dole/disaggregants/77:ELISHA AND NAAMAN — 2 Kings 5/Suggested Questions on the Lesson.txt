
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What evil did Jeroboam commit? set up two gold calves

Junior: Why did he set up the golden calves? afraid people would return to Rehoboam

Junior: Where were they set up? Bethel and Dan

Primary: What two great prophets did the Lord send to Israel? Elijah and Elisha

Primary: In our lesson for today what nation is troubling Israel? Syria

Primary: Who was the captain of the army of the king of Syria? Naaman

Primary: What disease did he have? leprosy

Primary: Who told him how he might be cured? captive Hebrew girl

Primary: What did Elisha tell Naaman to do? wash in Jordan seven times

Junior: What did Naaman at first say? rivers of Damascus were better

Primary: What happened when he obeyed Elisha? he was cured

Junior: What did he ask Elisha to give him? two loads of dirt

Junior: What did Elisha's servant do? took a reward

Junior: What was his punishment? leprosy

Intermediate: What do Israel and Judah represent? mind and body separated

Senior: What does washing in Jordan seven times represent? consistently obeying the commandments
