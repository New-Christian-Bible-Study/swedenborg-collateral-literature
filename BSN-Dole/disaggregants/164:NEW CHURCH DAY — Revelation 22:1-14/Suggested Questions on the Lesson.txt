
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What special day do we celebrate today? +
*A:* New Church Day

*Q:* (Older Children): Why is it so named? +
*A:* [see @TCR 791]

*Q:* (Older Children): Who tells us this? +
*A:* Swedenborg

*Q:* (Older Teens): Of whom was Swedenborg the agent? +
*A:* the Lord in His Second Coming

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did John see in the holy city? +
*A:* river, tree of life

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did we first read of the tree of life? +
*A:* Genesis 1

*Q:* (Older Children): What other tree was in the garden of Eden? +
*A:* knowledge of good and evil

*Q:* (Older Children): What were men told about the two trees? +
*A:* not to eat of latter

*Q:* (Younger Children): What must we learn to do if we want to live in the holy city? +
*A:* obey commandments

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why must we study the Bible? +
*A:* to learn how to live

*Q:* (Younger Children): What is our other name for the Bible? +
*A:* the Word

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the river of water of life? +
*A:* pure truth coming from the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the only way to true happiness? +
*A:* receiving the Lord's truth, living according to it

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is our special duty as New Churchmen? +
*A:* to learn and live the truths of the Word in the light of the heavenly doctrines
