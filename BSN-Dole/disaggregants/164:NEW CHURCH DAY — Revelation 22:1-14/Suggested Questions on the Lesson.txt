
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What special day do we celebrate today? New Church Day

Junior: Why is it so named? [see @TCR 791]

Junior: Who tells us this? Swedenborg

Senior: Of whom was Swedenborg the agent? the Lord in His Second Coming

Primary: What did John see in the holy city? river, tree of life

Junior: Where did we first read of the tree of life? Genesis 1

Junior: What other tree was in the garden of Eden? knowledge of good and evil

Junior: What were men told about the two trees? not to eat of latter

Primary: What must we learn to do if we want to live in the holy city? obey commandments

Primary: Why must we study the Bible? to learn how to live

Primary: What is our other name for the Bible? the Word

Intermediate: What is the river of water of life? pure truth coming from the Lord

Intermediate: What is the only way to true happiness? receiving the Lord's truth, living according to it

Senior: What is our special duty as New Churchmen? to learn and live the truths of the Word in the light of the heavenly doctrines
