
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What special day do we celebrate today? New Church Day

Older Children: Why is it so named? [see @TCR 791]

Older Children: Who tells us this? Swedenborg

Older Teens: Of whom was Swedenborg the agent? the Lord in His Second Coming

Younger Children: What did John see in the holy city? river, tree of life

Older Children: Where did we first read of the tree of life? Genesis 1

Older Children: What other tree was in the garden of Eden? knowledge of good and evil

Older Children: What were men told about the two trees? not to eat of latter

Younger Children: What must we learn to do if we want to live in the holy city? obey commandments

Younger Children: Why must we study the Bible? to learn how to live

Younger Children: What is our other name for the Bible? the Word

Younger Teens: What is the river of water of life? pure truth coming from the Lord

Younger Teens: What is the only way to true happiness? receiving the Lord's truth, living according to it

Older Teens: What is our special duty as New Churchmen? to learn and live the truths of the Word in the light of the heavenly doctrines
