
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What feast did the Hebrews observe before they left Egypt? Passover

Primary: How were they led on their journey? pillar of cloud/fire

Primary: What happened to them at the Red Sea? waters parted

Primary: How did they express their gratitude to the Lord? sang song

Primary: Who was the sister of Moses and Aaron? Miriam

Primary: What musical instrument did she use? timbrel

Junior: How long did they travel in the wilderness before they found water? three days

Primary: What was the matter with the water when they did find it? bitter

Junior: What was the name of this place? Marah

Junior: Where did they go next? Elim

Primary: What did they find there? twelve springs, seventy palm trees

Intermediate: What is the meaning of the flight from Egypt? leaving slavery to worldliness and seeking true spirituality

Intermediate: What does the pillar of cloud and fire represent? literal sense of the Word

Senior: What are meant by (1) the bitter water, and (2) the wood which made it sweet? (1) that which opposes our natural inclinations (2) a genuine desire to live a good life

Senior: What are meant by (1) the twelve wells, and (2) the seventy palm trees? (1) the truth of the saving power of the Lord (2) the good which comes from knowing that truth

Intermediate: What does the wilderness journey picture? reformation
