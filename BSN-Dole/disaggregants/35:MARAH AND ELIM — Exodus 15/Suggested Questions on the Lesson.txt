
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What feast did the Hebrews observe before they left Egypt? Passover

Younger Children: How were they led on their journey? pillar of cloud/fire

Younger Children: What happened to them at the Red Sea? waters parted

Younger Children: How did they express their gratitude to the Lord? sang song

Younger Children: Who was the sister of Moses and Aaron? Miriam

Younger Children: What musical instrument did she use? timbrel

Older Children: How long did they travel in the wilderness before they found water? three days

Younger Children: What was the matter with the water when they did find it? bitter

Older Children: What was the name of this place? Marah

Older Children: Where did they go next? Elim

Younger Children: What did they find there? twelve springs, seventy palm trees

Younger Teens: What is the meaning of the flight from Egypt? leaving slavery to worldliness and seeking true spirituality

Younger Teens: What does the pillar of cloud and fire represent? literal sense of the Word

Older Teens: What are meant by (1) the bitter water, and (2) the wood which made it sweet? (1) that which opposes our natural inclinations (2) a genuine desire to live a good life

Older Teens: What are meant by (1) the twelve wells, and (2) the seventy palm trees? (1) the truth of the saving power of the Lord (2) the good which comes from knowing that truth

Younger Teens: What does the wilderness journey picture? reformation
