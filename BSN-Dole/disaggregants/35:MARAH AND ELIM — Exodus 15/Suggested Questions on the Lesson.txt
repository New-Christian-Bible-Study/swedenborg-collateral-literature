
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What feast did the Hebrews observe before they left Egypt? +
*A:* Passover

*Q:* (Younger Children): How were they led on their journey? +
*A:* pillar of cloud/fire

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to them at the Red Sea? +
*A:* waters parted

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did they express their gratitude to the Lord? +
*A:* sang song

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the sister of Moses and Aaron? +
*A:* Miriam

*Q:* (Younger Children): What musical instrument did she use? +
*A:* timbrel

*Q:* (Older Children): How long did they travel in the wilderness before they found water? +
*A:* three days

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the matter with the water when they did find it? +
*A:* bitter

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the name of this place? +
*A:* Marah

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did they go next? +
*A:* Elim

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they find there? +
*A:* twelve springs, seventy palm trees

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the meaning of the flight from Egypt? +
*A:* leaving slavery to worldliness and seeking true spirituality

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the pillar of cloud and fire represent? +
*A:* literal sense of the Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): What are meant by (1) the bitter water, and (2) the wood which made it sweet? +
*A:* (1) that which opposes our natural inclinations (2) a genuine desire to live a good life

*Q:* (Older Teens): What are meant by (1) the twelve wells, and (2) the seventy palm trees? +
*A:* (1) the truth of the saving power of the Lord (2) the good which comes from knowing that truth

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the wilderness journey picture? +
*A:* reformation
