
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Joshua accomplish during his lifetime? +
*A:* conquered Holy Land

*Q:* (Older Children): How was the Holy Land divided among the tribes? +
*A:* by lot

*Q:* (Older Children): Did another great leader immediately follow Joshua? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Younger Children): After Joshua died, what mistake did the children of Israel make? +
*A:* forgot the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the result? +
*A:* various enemies troubled them

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord do for them each time they turned to Him for help? +
*A:* called a leader

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did they call the leaders whom the Lord raised up for them? +
*A:* Judges

*Q:* (Younger Children): What woman was a Judge? +
*A:* Deborah

*Q:* (Older Children): What enemy was attacking at this time? +
*A:* Jabin

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was the leader of the army of the enemy? +
*A:* Sisera

*Q:* (Younger Children): What man did Deborah call to lead the army of Israel? +
*A:* Barak

*Q:* (Older Children): On what condition did he agree to lead it? +
*A:* if she also went

*Q:* (Older Children): From what tribes did he draw his forces? +
*A:* Naphtali, Zebulun

*Q:* (Older Children): Where was the battle fought? +
*A:* near Kishon

*Q:* (Older Children): Who won? +
*A:* Israel

*Q:* (Older Children): To whom did Sisera flee? +
*A:* Jael

*Q:* (Older Children): What did she do to him? +
*A:* killed him

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does the period of the Judges picture in our lives? +
*A:* a time when we think we know enough not to have to learn more from the Lord

*Q:* (Older Children): What does an enemy from the north picture? +
*A:* temptation to yield to false ideas

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is represented by the tent pin? +
*A:* a simple truth from the Word which binds or "nails" our worship to our daily life
