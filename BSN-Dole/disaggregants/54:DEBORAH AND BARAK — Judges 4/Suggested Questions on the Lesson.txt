
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What did Joshua accomplish during his lifetime? conquered Holy Land

Older Children: How was the Holy Land divided among the tribes? by lot

Older Children: Did another great leader immediately follow Joshua? no

Younger Children: After Joshua died, what mistake did the children of Israel make? forgot the Lord

Older Children: What was the result? various enemies troubled them

Older Children: What did the Lord do for them each time they turned to Him for help? called a leader

Younger Children: What did they call the leaders whom the Lord raised up for them? Judges

Younger Children: What woman was a Judge? Deborah

Older Children: What enemy was attacking at this time? Jabin

Older Children: Who was the leader of the army of the enemy? Sisera

Younger Children: What man did Deborah call to lead the army of Israel? Barak

Older Children: On what condition did he agree to lead it? if she also went

Older Children: From what tribes did he draw his forces? Naphtali, Zebulun

Older Children: Where was the battle fought? near Kishon

Older Children: Who won? Israel

Older Children: To whom did Sisera flee? Jael

Older Children: What did she do to him? killed him

Older Teens: What does the period of the Judges picture in our lives? a time when we think we know enough not to have to learn more from the Lord

Older Children: What does an enemy from the north picture? temptation to yield to false ideas

Older Teens: What is represented by the tent pin? a simple truth from the Word which binds or "nails" our worship to our daily life
