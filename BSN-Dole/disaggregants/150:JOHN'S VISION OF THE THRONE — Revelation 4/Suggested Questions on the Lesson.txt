
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: What books in the Bible tell about the beginnings of the Christian Church? Acts, Epistles

Older Children: Why do we not study these books in Sunday school? they do not have an inner sense

Younger Children: What book are we now studying? Revelation

Older Children: By what other name is this book called? Apocalypse

Older Children: What is it about? visions

Younger Children: To whom were these visions given? the apostle John

Older Children: Where was John and why? Patmos, exiled

Younger Children: What did John see first? the risen Lord

Older Children: What messages were given him? letters to seven churches

Younger Children: In our chapter for today what did he see? a throne

Older Children: Can you describe the throne? [see Revelation 4:2-5]

Older Children: How many elders were there? twenty-four

Younger Children: How many creatures were there? four

Younger Children: What faces did they have? lion, calf, man, eagle

Older Children: Where have we learned of beasts like these before? Ezekiel 1

Older Children: By what other name do we know them? cherubim

Older Children: What did they cry before the Lord? Holy, holy, holy

Older Children: Where have we heard these words before? a familiar hymn, the Sanctus, the Trisagion

Younger Teens: What does a throne represent? judgment

Older Teens: What do the four beasts represent? protecting power of divine providence:
lion = power for good
calf = natural affections
man = wisdom
eagle = understanding
