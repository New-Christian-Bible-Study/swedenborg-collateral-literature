
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What books in the Bible tell about the beginnings of the Christian Church? Acts, Epistles

Junior: Why do we not study these books in Sunday school? they do not have an inner sense

Primary: What book are we now studying? Revelation

Junior: By what other name is this book called? Apocalypse

Junior: What is it about? visions

Primary: To whom were these visions given? the apostle John

Junior: Where was John and why? Patmos, exiled

Primary: What did John see first? the risen Lord

Junior: What messages were given him? letters to seven churches

Primary: In our chapter for today what did he see? a throne

Junior: Can you describe the throne? [see Revelation 4:2-5]

Junior: How many elders were there? twenty-four

Primary: How many creatures were there? four

Primary: What faces did they have? lion, calf, man, eagle

Junior: Where have we learned of beasts like these before? Ezekiel 1

Junior: By what other name do we know them? cherubim

Junior: What did they cry before the Lord? Holy, holy, holy

Junior: Where have we heard these words before? a familiar hymn, the Sanctus, the Trisagion

Intermediate: What does a throne represent? judgment

Senior: What do the four beasts represent? protecting power of divine providence:
lion = power for good
calf = natural affections 
man = wisdom
eagle = understanding
