
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Where were the ten commandments given? +
*A:* Mount Sinai

*Q:* (Older Children): What other laws did the Lord give Moses at Mount Sinai? +
*A:* conduct and worship

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the tabernacle? +
*A:* tent church

*Q:* (Older Children): What were its walls made of? +
*A:* boards covered with gold

*Q:* (Older Children): What was used to cover the top? +
*A:* animal skins

*Q:* (Younger Children): Into how many parts was it divided? +
*A:* three

*Q:* (Older Children): What were they called? +
*A:* Holy of Holies, Holy Place, outer court

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was in the Holy of Holies? +
*A:* ark

*Q:* (Younger Children): What were in the Holy Place? +
*A:* table, incense altar, lamp

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What formed the wall of the outer court? +
*A:* curtains

*Q:* (Older Children): What two things were in the outer court? +
*A:* laver, altar

*Q:* (Older Children): What three metals were used in the construction of the tabernacle? +
*A:* gold, silver, bronze

*Q:* (Older Children): How and where was each of them used? +
*A:* gold covered wood, silver castings, bronze sockets

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What was the veil? +
*A:* inner curtain

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did the Israelites have to worship in a tent? +
*A:* nomads

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the tabernacle represent? +
*A:* orderly human life or character

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do the three divisions represent? +
*A:* will, thought, actions

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by the ark's being in the Holy of Holies? +
*A:* God's law written in our hearts
