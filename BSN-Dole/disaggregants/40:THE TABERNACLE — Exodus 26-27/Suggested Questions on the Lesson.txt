
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: Where were the ten commandments given? Mount Sinai

Junior: What other laws did the Lord give Moses at Mount Sinai? conduct and worship

Primary: What was the tabernacle? tent church

Junior: What were its walls made of? boards covered with gold

Junior: What was used to cover the top? animal skins

Primary: Into how many parts was it divided? three

Junior: What were they called? Holy of Holies, Holy Place, outer court

Primary: What was in the Holy of Holies? ark

Primary: What were in the Holy Place? table, incense altar, lamp

Intermediate: What formed the wall of the outer court? curtains

Junior: What two things were in the outer court? laver, altar

Junior: What three metals were used in the construction of the tabernacle? gold, silver, bronze

Junior: How and where was each of them used? gold covered wood, silver castings, bronze sockets

Intermediate: What was the veil? inner curtain

Primary: Why did the Israelites have to worship in a tent? nomads

Intermediate: What does the tabernacle represent? orderly human life or character 

Intermediate: What do the three divisions represent? will, thought, actions

Senior: What is pictured by the ark's being in the Holy of Holies? God's law written in our hearts
