
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Where were the ten commandments given? Mount Sinai

Older Children: What other laws did the Lord give Moses at Mount Sinai? conduct and worship

Younger Children: What was the tabernacle? tent church

Older Children: What were its walls made of? boards covered with gold

Older Children: What was used to cover the top? animal skins

Younger Children: Into how many parts was it divided? three

Older Children: What were they called? Holy of Holies, Holy Place, outer court

Younger Children: What was in the Holy of Holies? ark

Younger Children: What were in the Holy Place? table, incense altar, lamp

Younger Teens: What formed the wall of the outer court? curtains

Older Children: What two things were in the outer court? laver, altar

Older Children: What three metals were used in the construction of the tabernacle? gold, silver, bronze

Older Children: How and where was each of them used? gold covered wood, silver castings, bronze sockets

Younger Teens: What was the veil? inner curtain

Younger Children: Why did the Israelites have to worship in a tent? nomads

Younger Teens: What does the tabernacle represent? orderly human life or character

Younger Teens: What do the three divisions represent? will, thought, actions

Older Teens: What is pictured by the ark's being in the Holy of Holies? God's law written in our hearts
