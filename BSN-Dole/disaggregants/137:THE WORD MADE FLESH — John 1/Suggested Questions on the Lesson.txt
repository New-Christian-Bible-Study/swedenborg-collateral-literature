
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Who was Jonah? a prophet of Israel

Older Children: Why did he try to run away from the Lord? too proud to warn Nineveh

Younger Children: What happened to him? swallowed by great fish

Older Children: What prophecy did he make to the Ninevites? doom in forty days

Older Children: Why was it not fulfilled? people repented

Younger Children: How did Jonah feel about it? angry

Older Children: What happened to him which pictured his state of heart and mind? story of gourd

Older Children: What lesson did the Lord draw from it for him? should love all people

Older Children: When the Lord came into the world, what had the scribes and Pharisees done with the Word? added their own ideas

Older Children: Can you repeat the first verse of the Gospel of John?

Younger Teens: What do we mean when we call the inspired Scriptures the "Word of God"? the written form of divine truth

Younger Children: Why do we always have the Word on the altar in church and open it at the beginning of the service and close it at the end? symbol of the Lord's presence

Older Children: How was the Word made flesh? in the person of Jesus Christ

Younger Children: In our chapter who testified to the fact that Jesus was the Messiah? John the Baptist

Younger Children: What sign had he been given? dove

Older Children: Which of the apostles are named in this chapter? Andrew, Peter, Philip, Nathanael

Older Children: By what other name is Nathanael elsewhere called? Bartholomew

Younger Teens: What is meant by saying that the Lord is the light of the world? He enlightens men's minds

Older Teens: What is meant by "the Son of God"? the Lord's visible divine humanity

Older Teens: Why did everyone not receive the Lord? some loved darkness
