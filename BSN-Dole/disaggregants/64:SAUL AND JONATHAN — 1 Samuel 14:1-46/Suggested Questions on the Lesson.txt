
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the first king? Saul

Primary: Why did the people like him? tall, strong

Junior: What enemy did he first conquer? Ammonites

Junior: What weaknesses did Saul have? impatient, disobedient, lacked faith

Junior: With what enemy did he have trouble? Philistines

Primary: Who was Saul's son? Jonathan

Primary: How was he different from his father? trusted the Lord

Junior: What did Jonathan and his armor-bearer decide to do? attack Philistine camp

Primary: Why did Jonathan think two men could win a victory over so many? faith

Junior: In what kind of place did they face the enemy? rocky

Junior: What sign did Jonathan ask of the Lord? If they say, "Come ... "

Primary: What happened in the Philistine army after Jonathan's victory? panic

Junior: What order did Saul give to his men when they started after the Philistines? no food

Junior: What did Jonathan do? ate some honey

Junior: What did Saul try to do to him? kill him

Junior: Who saved Jonathan? people

Junior: What does a king represent? ruling principle

Senior: What does Saul represent? superficial idea of truth

Senior: What does Jonathan represent? knowledge from experience

Senior: What is pictured by the Philistines' being thrown into confusion? arguments which support self-pride conflict with themselves
