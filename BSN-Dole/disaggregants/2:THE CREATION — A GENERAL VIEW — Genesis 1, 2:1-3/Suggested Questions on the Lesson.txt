
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What book do we study in Sunday School? the Bible

Younger Children: Who is the Author of the Bible? God

Younger Children: By what other names do we call the Bible? the Lord's Book, the Word, the Book of Life

Younger Children: What is the name of the first book of the Bible? Genesis

Younger Children: Can you repeat the first verse of the Bible? In the beginning ...

Younger Children: What is the first chapter about? the Creation

Younger Children: In how many days was the earth and everything on it created? six

Younger Teens: Were these days of twenty-four hours each? no

Younger Teens: What happened on the first day? light

Younger Teens: What happened on the second day? firmament

Younger Teens: What happened to the waters on the third day? sea, dry land

Younger Teens: What new things were created on that day? grass, herbs, trees

Younger Teens: What appeared on the fourth day? sun, moon, stars

Younger Teens: What were created on the fifth day? fish, birds

Younger Teens: On what day were the animals created? sixth

Younger Teens: What was the final creation? man

Younger Teens: In whose image and likeness was man created? God's

Intermediate. What two kinds of people were created? male, female

Intermediate. What did the Lord tell them to do? be fruitful

Younger Children: What did the Lord do on the seventh day? rested

Younger Teens: What is the Lord really telling us about in this first chapter? the beginning of our spiritual growth

Older Teens: What is meant by the heaven and the earth? spiritual and natural

Older Teens: What do the sun, moon, and stars correspond to? love to Lord, faith in Him, knowledges of heavenly things

The brief answers to questions supplied in all the lessons are intended only as a general guide to teachers. Fuller and more detailed answers formulated and worked out with the class are generally recommended.
