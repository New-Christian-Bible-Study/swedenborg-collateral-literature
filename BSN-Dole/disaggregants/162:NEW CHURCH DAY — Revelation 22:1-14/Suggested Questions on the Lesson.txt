
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What special day are we celebrating? New Church Day

Primary: Why do we celebrate it? [see @TCR 791]

Junior: What happened on the first New Church Day? the Lord sent his apostles throughout heaven

Junior: When was that? June 19, 1770

Junior: How did the Lord tell His disciples He would come again? "in the clouds ... "

Junior: What did this mean? through the letter of the Word

Intermediate: Why did the Lord have to make this Second Coming? to reveal needed new truth

Junior: Why did He not come again in a physical body? not necessary

Primary: From what book of the Bible is our lesson for today? Revelation

Intermediate: What is the book of Revelation? closing prophecy of the Word

Primary: To whom was this revelation given? the apostle John

Primary: At the beginning of our chapter what did John see? river

Primary: Where else have we heard of the river and the tree of life? Genesis 1

Intermediate: What does the river picture? truth from the Lord

Intermediate: What does the tree of life picture? that all good and truth are from the Lord

Senior: What did John teach us when he fell at the feet of the angel? angels are men

Intermediate: Why do some believe that the angels are a separate creation? wrong translation of Psalm 8:5

Intermediate: How may we know that they were all once men? from what the angel told John

Intermediate: Why did the first Christian Church break up into so many sects? lacked truth

Senior: What makes the Church on earth? people loving and living divine truth

Senior: What important instruction do we receive in our chapter for today? the Lord has a use for us in His New Church

Senior: What are the distinctive doctrines upon which the Church of the New Jerusalem is founded? (1) Doctrine of the Lord, (2) Doctrine of the Word, (3) Doctrine of Freedom
