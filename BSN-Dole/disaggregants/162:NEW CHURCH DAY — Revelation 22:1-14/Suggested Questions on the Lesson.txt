
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What special day are we celebrating? New Church Day

Younger Children: Why do we celebrate it? [see @TCR 791]

Older Children: What happened on the first New Church Day? the Lord sent his apostles throughout heaven

Older Children: When was that? June 19, 1770

Older Children: How did the Lord tell His disciples He would come again? "in the clouds ... "

Older Children: What did this mean? through the letter of the Word

Younger Teens: Why did the Lord have to make this Second Coming? to reveal needed new truth

Older Children: Why did He not come again in a physical body? not necessary

Younger Children: From what book of the Bible is our lesson for today? Revelation

Younger Teens: What is the book of Revelation? closing prophecy of the Word

Younger Children: To whom was this revelation given? the apostle John

Younger Children: At the beginning of our chapter what did John see? river

Younger Children: Where else have we heard of the river and the tree of life? Genesis 1

Younger Teens: What does the river picture? truth from the Lord

Younger Teens: What does the tree of life picture? that all good and truth are from the Lord

Older Teens: What did John teach us when he fell at the feet of the angel? angels are men

Younger Teens: Why do some believe that the angels are a separate creation? wrong translation of Psalm 8:5

Younger Teens: How may we know that they were all once men? from what the angel told John

Younger Teens: Why did the first Christian Church break up into so many sects? lacked truth

Older Teens: What makes the Church on earth? people loving and living divine truth

Older Teens: What important instruction do we receive in our chapter for today? the Lord has a use for us in His New Church

Older Teens: What are the distinctive doctrines upon which the Church of the New Jerusalem is founded? (1) Doctrine of the Lord, (2) Doctrine of the Word, (3) Doctrine of Freedom
