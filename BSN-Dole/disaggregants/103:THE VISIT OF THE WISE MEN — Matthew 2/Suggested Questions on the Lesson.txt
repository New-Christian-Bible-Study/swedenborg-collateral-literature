
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Whose birthday is Christmas? Jesus'

Older Children: How long ago did the Lord come into the world? [relate to current year]

Younger Children: Where was He born? Bethlehem

Younger Children: Who else was born in Bethlehem? Benjamin, David

Older Children: What does the name Bethlehem mean? house of bread

Older Children: Where did the Wise Men come from? the East

Younger Children: Why did they come? saw His star

Older Children: How did they know what the star meant? ancient knowledge of correspondence

Younger Children: Where did they go first? Jerusalem

Younger Teens: How did Herod find out where to send them? scribes found prophecy

Younger Children: How did they find the right house? star

Younger Children: What gifts did they bring the Lord? gold, frankincense, myrrh

Older Children: Why did they not go back to Herod? warned in a dream

Younger Children: Where was Joseph told to take the Lord? to Egypt

Younger Children: How was he told? in a dream

Older Children: What terrible crime did Herod commit? killed all boy babies in and around Bethlehem

Older Children: When was Joseph told to bring the Lord back to the Holy Land? after Herod's death

Older Children: Where did they go to live? Nazareth, in Galilee

Younger Teens: What great gift did the Lord give us on the first Christmas Day? Himself

Older Teens: What are the three gifts we should bring Him? love faith, obedience
