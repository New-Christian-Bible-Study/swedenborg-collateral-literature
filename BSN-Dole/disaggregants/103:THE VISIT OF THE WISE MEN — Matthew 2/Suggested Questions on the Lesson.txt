
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Whose birthday is Christmas? Jesus'

Junior: How long ago did the Lord come into the world? [relate to current year]

Primary: Where was He born? Bethlehem

Primary: Who else was born in Bethlehem? Benjamin, David

Junior: What does the name Bethlehem mean? house of bread

Junior: Where did the Wise Men come from? the East

Primary: Why did they come? saw His star

Junior: How did they know what the star meant? ancient knowledge of correspondence

Primary: Where did they go first? Jerusalem

Intermediate: How did Herod find out where to send them? scribes found prophecy

Primary: How did they find the right house? star

Primary: What gifts did they bring the Lord? gold, frankincense, myrrh

Junior: Why did they not go back to Herod? warned in a dream

Primary: Where was Joseph told to take the Lord? to Egypt

Primary: How was he told? in a dream

Junior: What terrible crime did Herod commit? killed all boy babies in and around Bethlehem

Junior: When was Joseph told to bring the Lord back to the Holy Land? after Herod's death

Junior: Where did they go to live? Nazareth, in Galilee

Intermediate: What great gift did the Lord give us on the first Christmas Day? Himself

Senior: What are the three gifts we should bring Him? love faith, obedience
