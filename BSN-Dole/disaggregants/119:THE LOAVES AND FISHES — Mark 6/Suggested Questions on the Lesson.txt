
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Older Children): Why could the Lord not do many miracles in Nazareth? +
*A:* lack of faith in Him there

*Q:* (Older Children): What instructions did He give the apostles when He sent them out? +
*A:* take no "extras"

*Q:* (Older Children): Who did king Herod think the Lord was? +
*A:* John the Baptist

*Q:* (Younger Children): What had Herod done to John the Baptist? +
*A:* beheaded him

*Q:* (Older Children): Who really caused John's death? +
*A:* Herodias

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do Herod and Herodias represent in us? +
*A:* selfâ€”interest, self-love ruling

*Q:* (Older Children): When the multitudes were hungry, how did the disciples suggest that they be fed? +
*A:* send away for food

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell them to do? +
*A:* you feed them

*Q:* (Younger Children): What food did they have on hand? +
*A:* five loaves, two fish

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the Lord have the people do? +
*A:* sit down by hundreds and fifties

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did He do with the loaves and fishes? +
*A:* blessed, broke

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many people were fed? +
*A:* five thousand

*Q:* (Younger Children): How many baskets of fragments were left? +
*A:* twelve

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Where did the Lord send the disciples afterward? +
*A:* Bethsaida (across the sea)

*Q:* (Younger Children): What trouble did they have? +
*A:* strong headwind

*Q:* (Younger Teens): How did the Lord reach and help them? +
*A:* walked on water

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What kind of food do we need beside food for our bodies? +
*A:* food for our souls: wisdom and love

*Q:* (Older Teens): How is our spiritual food multiplied? +
*A:* put scattered thoughts in order, use our available talents as best we can, look to the Lord for knowledge and strength
