
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Older Children: Why could the Lord not do many miracles in Nazareth? lack of faith in Him there

Older Children: What instructions did He give the apostles when He sent them out? take no "extras"

Older Children: Who did king Herod think the Lord was? John the Baptist

Younger Children: What had Herod done to John the Baptist? beheaded him

Older Children: Who really caused John's death? Herodias

Younger Teens: What do Herod and Herodias represent in us? selfâ€”interest, self-love ruling

Older Children: When the multitudes were hungry, how did the disciples suggest that they be fed? send away for food

Older Children: What did the Lord tell them to do? you feed them

Younger Children: What food did they have on hand? five loaves, two fish

Younger Children: What did the Lord have the people do? sit down by hundreds and fifties

Younger Children: What did He do with the loaves and fishes? blessed, broke

Younger Children: How many people were fed? five thousand

Younger Children: How many baskets of fragments were left? twelve

Younger Teens: Where did the Lord send the disciples afterward? Bethsaida (across the sea)

Younger Children: What trouble did they have? strong headwind

Younger Teens: How did the Lord reach and help them? walked on water

Younger Teens: What kind of food do we need beside food for our bodies? food for our souls: wisdom and love

Older Teens: How is our spiritual food multiplied? put scattered thoughts in order, use our available talents as best we can, look to the Lord for knowledge and strength
