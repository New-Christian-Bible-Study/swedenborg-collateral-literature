
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What do we celebrate on Christmas day? +
*A:* the birth of Jesus Christ

*Q:* (Older Children): About how many years ago was the Lord born on earth? +
*A:* two thousand (more or less)

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was He born? +
*A:* Bethlehem

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was His mother? +
*A:* Mary

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was her husband? +
*A:* Joseph

*Q:* (Older Children): Who in a far-off country were told of His birth? +
*A:* Wise Men

*Q:* (Older Children): How were they told? +
*A:* "We have seen his star ... "

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who were told near Bethlehem? +
*A:* some shepherds

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where were these shepherds and what were they doing? +
*A:* in the field watching their sheep

*Q:* (Younger Children): How were they told? +
*A:* by an angel

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did the angel tell them to do? +
*A:* "You will find the babe wrapped in swaddling clothes lying in a manger"

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was the message of the heavenly host? +
*A:* peace on earth to men of good will

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did the shepherds find the Lord? +
*A:* in a stable

*Q:* (Older Children): Whom did they tell about Him? +
*A:* everyone they saw

*Q:* (Older Children): Who remembered all they said? +
*A:* Mary

*Q:* (Older Children): Who was Jesus? +
*A:* God-with-us

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did He come in to the world? +
*A:* to save us from our sins

*Q:* (Older Teens): What did He accomplish for us? +
*A:* redemption, making salvation possible for all
