
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What do we celebrate on Christmas day? the birth of Jesus Christ

Older Children: About how many years ago was the Lord born on earth? two thousand (more or less)

Younger Children: Where was He born? Bethlehem

Younger Children: Who was His mother? Mary

Younger Children: Who was her husband? Joseph

Older Children: Who in a far-off country were told of His birth? Wise Men

Older Children: How were they told? "We have seen his star ... "

Younger Children: Who were told near Bethlehem? some shepherds

Younger Children: Where were these shepherds and what were they doing? in the field watching their sheep

Younger Children: How were they told? by an angel

Younger Children: What did the angel tell them to do? "You will find the babe wrapped in swaddling clothes lying in a manger"

Younger Children: What was the message of the heavenly host? peace on earth to men of good will

Older Children: Where did the shepherds find the Lord? in a stable

Older Children: Whom did they tell about Him? everyone they saw

Older Children: Who remembered all they said? Mary

Older Children: Who was Jesus? God-with-us

Younger Teens: Why did He come in to the world? to save us from our sins

Older Teens: What did He accomplish for us? redemption, making salvation possible for all
