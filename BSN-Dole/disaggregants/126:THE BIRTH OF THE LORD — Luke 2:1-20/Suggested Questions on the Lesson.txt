
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What do we celebrate on Christmas day? the birth of Jesus Christ

Junior: About how many years ago was the Lord born on earth? two thousand (more or less)

Primary: Where was He born? Bethlehem

Primary: Who was His mother? Mary

Primary: Who was her husband? Joseph

Junior: Who in a far-off country were told of His birth? Wise Men

Junior: How were they told? "We have seen his star ... "

Primary: Who were told near Bethlehem? some shepherds

Primary: Where were these shepherds and what were they doing? in the field watching their sheep

Primary: How were they told? by an angel

Primary: What did the angel tell them to do? "You will find the babe wrapped in swaddling clothes lying in a manger"

Primary: What was the message of the heavenly host? peace on earth to men of good will

Junior: Where did the shepherds find the Lord? in a stable

Junior: Whom did they tell about Him? everyone they saw

Junior: Who remembered all they said? Mary

Junior: Who was Jesus? God-with-us

Intermediate: Why did He come in to the world? to save us from our sins

Senior: What did He accomplish for us? redemption, making salvation possible for all
