
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the second king of Israel? David

Primary: Who anointed him? Samuel

Junior: Did he become king as soon as he was anointed? no

Junior: Who became his great friend? Jonathan

Junior: What made Saul jealous of David? "Saul has killed his thousands, David his ten thousands!"

Primary: Who saved David when Saul tried to kill him? Jonathan

Junior: How did Saul and Jonathan die? in battle with Philistines

Junior: What great city did David take and make his capital? Jerusalem

Primary: What did he want to bring there? ark

Intermediate: Where was the ark at this time? Kirjath-jearim

Primary: What happened to Uzzah and why? struck dead for steadying ark

Junior: Who laughed at David for singing and dancing before the ark? Michal

Junior: What was her punishment? to remain childless

Junior: Where did David put the ark in Jerusalem? Mount Zion

Intermediate: What does the ark represent? commandments loved and obeyed

Senior: What is pictured by bringing the ark to Jerusalem? putting divine law in center of life

Senior: What is pictured by Uzzah's act? doubting power of God's truth
