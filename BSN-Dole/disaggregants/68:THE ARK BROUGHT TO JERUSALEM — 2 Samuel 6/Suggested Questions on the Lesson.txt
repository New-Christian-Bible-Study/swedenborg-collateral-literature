
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the second king of Israel? +
*A:* David

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who anointed him? +
*A:* Samuel

*Q:* (Older Children): Did he become king as soon as he was anointed? +
*A:* no

*Q:* (Older Children): Who became his great friend? +
*A:* Jonathan

*Q:* (Older Children): What made Saul jealous of David? +
*A:* "Saul has killed his thousands, David his ten thousands!"

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who saved David when Saul tried to kill him? +
*A:* Jonathan

*Q:* (Older Children): How did Saul and Jonathan die? +
*A:* in battle with Philistines

*Q:* (Older Children): What great city did David take and make his capital? +
*A:* Jerusalem

*Q:* (Younger Children): What did he want to bring there? +
*A:* ark

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Where was the ark at this time? +
*A:* Kirjath-jearim

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to Uzzah and why? +
*A:* struck dead for steadying ark

*Q:* (Older Children): Who laughed at David for singing and dancing before the ark? +
*A:* Michal

*Q:* (Older Children): What was her punishment? +
*A:* to remain childless

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did David put the ark in Jerusalem? +
*A:* Mount Zion

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does the ark represent? +
*A:* commandments loved and obeyed

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by bringing the ark to Jerusalem? +
*A:* putting divine law in center of life

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is pictured by Uzzah's act? +
*A:* doubting power of God's truth
