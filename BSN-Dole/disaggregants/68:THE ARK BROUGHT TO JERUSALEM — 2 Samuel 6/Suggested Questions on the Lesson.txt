
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the second king of Israel? David

Younger Children: Who anointed him? Samuel

Older Children: Did he become king as soon as he was anointed? no

Older Children: Who became his great friend? Jonathan

Older Children: What made Saul jealous of David? "Saul has killed his thousands, David his ten thousands!"

Younger Children: Who saved David when Saul tried to kill him? Jonathan

Older Children: How did Saul and Jonathan die? in battle with Philistines

Older Children: What great city did David take and make his capital? Jerusalem

Younger Children: What did he want to bring there? ark

Younger Teens: Where was the ark at this time? Kirjath-jearim

Younger Children: What happened to Uzzah and why? struck dead for steadying ark

Older Children: Who laughed at David for singing and dancing before the ark? Michal

Older Children: What was her punishment? to remain childless

Older Children: Where did David put the ark in Jerusalem? Mount Zion

Younger Teens: What does the ark represent? commandments loved and obeyed

Older Teens: What is pictured by bringing the ark to Jerusalem? putting divine law in center of life

Older Teens: What is pictured by Uzzah's act? doubting power of God's truth
