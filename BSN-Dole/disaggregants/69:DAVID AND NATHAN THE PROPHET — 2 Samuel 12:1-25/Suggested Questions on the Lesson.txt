
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the second king of Israel? David

Younger Children: Where was his home? Bethlehem

Younger Children: What was his first occupation? shepherd

Younger Children: Who anointed him? Samuel

Older Children: How did he come to Saul's attention? played lyre

Older Children: How did he conquer Goliath? sling and stone

Older Children: Where did David first reign? Hebron

Older Children: How long did he reign there? seven years

Younger Children: After he became king over all the land, what city did he take as his capital? Jerusalem

Younger Children: What precious thing did he bring there? ark

Older Children: What did he accomplish for Israel? conquered all enemies

Older Children: What sin did he commit? took Bathsheba

Younger Children: Who was sent by the Lord to rebuke him? Nathan

Younger Children: What parable did Nathan tell David? rich man, poor man, one ewe lamb

Younger Children: How did David judge the man in the parable? should die

Younger Children: Then what did Nathan tell him? "Thou art the man"

Older Children: How did David take Nathan's condemnation? repented (see Psalm 51)

Younger Teens: Why did Nathan tell him the parable first? to get unbiased judgment (also fear of his own life)

Older Teens: What is the difference in correspondence between (1) Saul, and (2) David? (1) worldly idea of truth (2) spiritual idea of truth
