
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the second king of Israel? +
*A:* David

*Q:* (Younger Children): Where was his home? +
*A:* Bethlehem

*Q:* (Younger Children): What was his first occupation? +
*A:* shepherd

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who anointed him? +
*A:* Samuel

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he come to Saul's attention? +
*A:* played lyre

*Q:* (Older Children): How did he conquer Goliath? +
*A:* sling and stone

*Q:* (Older Children): Where did David first reign? +
*A:* Hebron

*Q:* (Older Children): How long did he reign there? +
*A:* seven years

*Q:* (Younger Children): After he became king over all the land, what city did he take as his capital? +
*A:* Jerusalem

*Q:* (Younger Children): What precious thing did he bring there? +
*A:* ark

*Q:* (Older Children): What did he accomplish for Israel? +
*A:* conquered all enemies

*Q:* (Older Children): What sin did he commit? +
*A:* took Bathsheba

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was sent by the Lord to rebuke him? +
*A:* Nathan

*Q:* (Younger Children): What parable did Nathan tell David? +
*A:* rich man, poor man, one ewe lamb

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did David judge the man in the parable? +
*A:* should die

*Q:* (Younger Children): Then what did Nathan tell him? +
*A:* "Thou art the man"

*Q:* (Older Children): How did David take Nathan's condemnation? +
*A:* repented (see Psalm 51)

*Q:* (Younger Teens): Why did Nathan tell him the parable first? +
*A:* to get unbiased judgment (also fear of his own life)

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is the difference in correspondence between (1) Saul, and (2) David? +
*A:* (1) worldly idea of truth (2) spiritual idea of truth
