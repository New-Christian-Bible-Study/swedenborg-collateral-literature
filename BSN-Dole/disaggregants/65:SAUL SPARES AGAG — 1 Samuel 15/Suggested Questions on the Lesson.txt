
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: Who was the first king? Saul

Primary: How was he chosen? by lot

Primary: Why did the people like him? tall, strong

Junior: In what part of the country did he win his first victory? across Jordan

Junior: What was the second enemy he fought? Philistines

Primary: What mistake did he make? did not wait for Samuel

Primary: By whose help was the victory finally won? Jonathan

Primary: In our lesson for today, what enemy is Saul told to attack? Amalek

Junior: Where have we heard of the Amalekites before? first enemy in wilderness

Junior: How was the first battle against them won? Moses' hands held up

Junior: What did the Lord tell Saul to do to them? destroy completely

Primary: How was Saul disobedient? saved king, best animals

Junior: What did Samuel tell him? he would lose kingdom

Junior: What sign was given of this? torn robe

Primary: What happened to Agag? Samuel killed him

Senior: What do the Amalekites represent? falsity based on interior evil which leads to discouragement

Intermediate: What does Saul as king represent? worldly idea of truth
