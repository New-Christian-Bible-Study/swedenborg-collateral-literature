
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who was the first king? +
*A:* Saul

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was he chosen? +
*A:* by lot

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why did the people like him? +
*A:* tall, strong

*Q:* (Older Children): In what part of the country did he win his first victory? +
*A:* across Jordan

*Q:* (Older Children): What was the second enemy he fought? +
*A:* Philistines

*Q:* (Younger Children): What mistake did he make? +
*A:* did not wait for Samuel

*Q:* (Younger Children): By whose help was the victory finally won? +
*A:* Jonathan

*Q:* (Younger Children): In our lesson for today, what enemy is Saul told to attack? +
*A:* Amalek

*Q:* (Older Children): Where have we heard of the Amalekites before? +
*A:* first enemy in wilderness

*Q:* (Older Children): How was the first battle against them won? +
*A:* Moses' hands held up

*Q:* (Older Children): What did the Lord tell Saul to do to them? +
*A:* destroy completely

*Q:* (Younger Children): How was Saul disobedient? +
*A:* saved king, best animals

*Q:* (Older Children): What did Samuel tell him? +
*A:* he would lose kingdom

*Q:* (Older Children): What sign was given of this? +
*A:* torn robe

*Q:* (Younger Children): What happened to Agag? +
*A:* Samuel killed him

*Q:* (Older Teens): What do the Amalekites represent? +
*A:* falsity based on interior evil which leads to discouragement

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does Saul as king represent? +
*A:* worldly idea of truth
