
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: Who was the first king? Saul

Younger Children: How was he chosen? by lot

Younger Children: Why did the people like him? tall, strong

Older Children: In what part of the country did he win his first victory? across Jordan

Older Children: What was the second enemy he fought? Philistines

Younger Children: What mistake did he make? did not wait for Samuel

Younger Children: By whose help was the victory finally won? Jonathan

Younger Children: In our lesson for today, what enemy is Saul told to attack? Amalek

Older Children: Where have we heard of the Amalekites before? first enemy in wilderness

Older Children: How was the first battle against them won? Moses' hands held up

Older Children: What did the Lord tell Saul to do to them? destroy completely

Younger Children: How was Saul disobedient? saved king, best animals

Older Children: What did Samuel tell him? he would lose kingdom

Older Children: What sign was given of this? torn robe

Younger Children: What happened to Agag? Samuel killed him

Older Teens: What do the Amalekites represent? falsity based on interior evil which leads to discouragement

Younger Teens: What does Saul as king represent? worldly idea of truth
