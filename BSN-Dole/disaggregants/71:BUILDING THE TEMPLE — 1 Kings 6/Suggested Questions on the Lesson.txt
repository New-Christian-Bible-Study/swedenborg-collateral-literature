
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Junior: What was David's principal work? conquering enemies

Junior: Why could he not build the temple? needs time of peace

Primary: Who did build it? Solomon

Primary: What does the name Solomon mean? peace

Junior: Who furnished some of the materials for the temple? Hiram

Junior: How did Solomon pay him? grain, oil, cities

Primary: What were the principal materials? stone, cedar, gold

Junior: How was the temple like the tabernacle? same general plan

Junior: How was it different? more elaborate, permanent

Junior: Where in Jerusalem was it built? Mount Moriah

Junior: Why was it built more quietly than most buildings? stone cut and shaped at quarry

Primary: How long did it take to build it? seven years

Intermediate: What are pictured by (1) Saul, (2) David, and (3) Solomon? obeying truth from (1) duty, (2) reverence, and (3) love

Senior: What is meant in our lives by David's not being permitted to build the temple? character takes final form only after temptation battles

Senior: What is meant in our lives by the 'temple materials' being prepared before they were brought to Jerusalem? forming character by right actions in outer, everyday life
