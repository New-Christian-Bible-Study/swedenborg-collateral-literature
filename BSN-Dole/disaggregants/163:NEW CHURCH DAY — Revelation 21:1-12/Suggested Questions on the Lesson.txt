
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Younger Children: What special day are we celebrating today? New Church Day

Younger Children: Why do we call June nineteenth "New Church Day"? [see @TCR 791]

Older Children: For what is our Church named? the holy city New Jerusalem

Younger Children: Who first saw the holy city coming down from God out of heaven? the apostle John

Younger Teens: What does a city correspond to? doctrine

Younger Teens: What do its walls correspond to? letter of the Word

Younger Children: What does the word Jerusalem mean? place of peace

Older Children: Why did the world have to wait so long for the final revelation? till men were ready

Older Children: Which is more important, knowledge of this world or knowledge of the Lord and His will? knowledge of the Lord

Younger Children: How did the Lord make His Second Coming? by revealing inner sense of Word

Older Teens: Why did the Lord have to give man freedom of choice? love to Lord, neighbor must be freely chosen

Older Teens: What does this teach us about His permission of evil? necessary in order to preserve human freedom

Younger Teens: What is the great temptation we face in the world today? pride of self intelligence

Older Teens: What is our duty as members of the New Church? to help lead others to the Lord by means of the Word and the Writings
