
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

*Q:* (Younger Children): What special day are we celebrating today? +
*A:* New Church Day

*Q:* (Younger Children): Why do we call June nineteenth "New Church Day"? +
*A:* [see @TCR 791]

*Q:* (Older Children): For what is our Church named? +
*A:* the holy city New Jerusalem

*Q:* (Younger Children): Who first saw the holy city coming down from God out of heaven? +
*A:* the apostle John

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What does a city correspond to? +
*A:* doctrine

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What do its walls correspond to? +
*A:* letter of the Word

*Q:* (Younger Children): What does the word Jerusalem mean? +
*A:* place of peace

*Q:* (Older Children): Why did the world have to wait so long for the final revelation? +
*A:* till men were ready

*Q:* (Older Children): Which is more important, knowledge of this world or knowledge of the Lord and His will? +
*A:* knowledge of the Lord

*Q:* (Younger Children): How did the Lord make His Second Coming? +
*A:* by revealing inner sense of Word

*Q:* (Older Teens): Why did the Lord have to give man freedom of choice? +
*A:* love to Lord, neighbor must be freely chosen

*Q:* (Older Teens): What does this teach us about His permission of evil? +
*A:* necessary in order to preserve human freedom

*Q:* (Younger Teens): What is the great temptation we face in the world today? +
*A:* pride of self intelligence

*Q:* (Older Teens): What is our duty as members of the New Church? +
*A:* to help lead others to the Lord by means of the Word and the Writings
