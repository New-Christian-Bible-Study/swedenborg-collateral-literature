
=== Suggested Questions on the Lesson

Primary: What special day are we celebrating today? New Church Day

Primary: Why do we call June nineteenth "New Church Day"? [see @TCR 791]

Junior: For what is our Church named? the holy city New Jerusalem

Primary: Who first saw the holy city coming down from God out of heaven? the apostle John

Intermediate: What does a city correspond to? doctrine

Intermediate: What do its walls correspond to? letter of the Word

Primary: What does the word Jerusalem mean? place of peace

Junior: Why did the world have to wait so long for the final revelation? till men were ready

Junior: Which is more important, knowledge of this world or knowledge of the Lord and His will? knowledge of the Lord

Primary: How did the Lord make His Second Coming? by revealing inner sense of Word

Senior: Why did the Lord have to give man freedom of choice? love to Lord, neighbor must be freely chosen

Senior: What does this teach us about His permission of evil? necessary in order to preserve human freedom

Intermediate: What is the great temptation we face in the world today? pride of self intelligence

Senior: What is our duty as members of the New Church? to help lead others to the Lord by means of the Word and the Writings
